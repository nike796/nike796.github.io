Cornbread Chicken Strips with Sauce step by step

  1. Heat oil..
  2. Slice the chicken strips and season.
  3. Add cornbread mix.
  4. Fry into batches.
  5. Turn as needed.
  6. Move to paper towels to absorb excessive oils.
  7. Repeat till all chicken is done.
  8. Mix the Bleu cheese, parsley, dill weed, mayonnaise, and vinegar, and sour cream..
  9. Mix well add seasoned salt to taste..
