Chicken Tenders in Air Fryer step by step

  1. Mix all the above ingredients & marinade chicken for 1 - 2 hours..
  2. Put the marinated chicken in the tray and spray some oil on it. You can put some frozen French fries also in the same tray..
  3. Put the tray in Air Fryer for 10 minutes on 250 temperature..
  4. After 10 minutes take out the tray & change the side of chicken & French Fries. Again put it for 10 minutes on 250 temperature..
  5. Ready to Serve. Yummy & Tasty..
