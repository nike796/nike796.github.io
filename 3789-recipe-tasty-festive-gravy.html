Festive Gravy step by step

  1. Heat the oil and add onions then season with seasoned salt.
  2. Add sausage, garlic paste, spices, Apple, and mushroom stems..
  3. When the sausage is browned and done add 10 ounces of the milk.
  4. Mix the 2 ounce of milk with the the cornstarch and stir into the sausage mixture and simmer stirring constantly till it thickens..
  5. Serve over biscuits, rice, noodles, and so on..
