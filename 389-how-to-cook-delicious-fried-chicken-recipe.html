Fried chicken recipe instructions

  2. Add red chilli powder,kasuri methi,salt,ginger garlic paste, lemon juice,besa,red food colour and dahi and marinate the chicken for 4-5 hours..
  3. Your crispy fried chicken is ready..
  4. Take a pan heat the oil to deep fry the chicken fry the marinated chicken till it gets cooked and get a golden brown colour..
