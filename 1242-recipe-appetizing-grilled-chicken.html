Ingredients of Grilled Chicken

  1. You need 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  2. It's 1/2 teaspoon of red chilli powder.
  3. Prepare 2 pinches of Turmeric powder.
  4. It's 1 tablespoon of curd.
  5. You need 1 teaspoon of lemon juice.
  6. Prepare 2 of chicken leg pieces and wings.

Grilled Chicken instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with all the ingredients and mix well..
  2. Store in the refrigerator for 15 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, preheat microwave for 15 minutes in grill mode.
  4. After preheat, grease the microwave pan with oil and place the chicken on the pan.
  5. Place the pan in the microwave and grill in grill mode for 20 minutes.
  6. After 20 minutes, flip the chicken so that the grilling is done evenly.
  7. Grill again for 15 minutes.
  8. Once done, place the chicken on a plate or a bowl and let the chicken rest for 4-5 minutes. Eat and enjoy!.
