Chicken Steak with Veggies instructions

  1. Wash chicken steaks and marinate with barbecue sauce, soya sauce, salt, pepper, ginger garlic paste, and red chilli flakes.
  2. Heat little oil in a pan and add chicken steaks and cook well until tender.
  3. Boil red beans and drain water cut onion, cucumber and tomatoes in cubes. Add salt pepper and lemon juice mix well.
  4. Cut cauliflower, lauki, green beans, corns, shimla and saute in oil with salt, pepper, zeera and soya sauce.
  5. Make some fries for the plating..
  6. Now make a plate, add some steaks, vegetables, beans and fries. Enjoy your meal.
