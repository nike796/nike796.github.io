Ingredients of Grilled Chicken

  1. You need 1 kg of chicken.
  2. Prepare 10 of pepper.
  3. It's 4 tbsp of soy sauce,.
  4. You need 1 of bulb onions.
  5. It's 2 tbsp of garlic and ginger paste.
  6. You need 2 tbsp of oregano.
  7. It's to taste of salt.
  8. It's 6 cube of knorr.
  9. You need leaf of coriander.

Grilled Chicken instructions

  1. Wash chicken and pat dry, blend pepper and onions and mix in garlic and ginger paste together with the pepper blend, salt, knorr cube, oregano, soy sauce, coriander leaf and set aside.
  2. Combine chicken and the mixed pepper paste for marinade and in a bowl and cling film the bowl and refrigerate overnight.
  3. Preheat oven on 180degree and turn on the grill. remove chicken and place in a baking dish or an oven tray and send to the oven for about 1 hour, I had to do 2 batches cos the bought a quarter carton of chicken.
  4. Once the chicken is cook through inside and the body is getting dry and abit brown remove from oven and set aside and serve with meal of the day and store the remaining in the fridge for emergency meal prep.
