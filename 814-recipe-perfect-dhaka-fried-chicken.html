Dhaka fried chicken instructions

  1. In a bowl add egg lemon juice n garlic..whisk well.
  2. Add cornflour,sesame seeds,crushed chillies,chicken pwdr,salt mix well..
  3. Add chicken strips.marinate it for 20 mins...I added fries in it for kids.
  4. In a wok heat oil..fry till golden brown.
  5. In a bowl add Mayo ketchup salt n black pepper..mix it well..sauce is ready....
  6. Sprinkle Chaat masala n serve.
