Ingredients of Chicken wings with caramelized sauce

  1. You need 500 g of chicken wings.
  2. Prepare 2 tbs of lemon juice.
  3. You need 2 cm of ginger.
  4. Prepare 4 cloves of garlic.
  5. Prepare 1 tbs of oyster sauce.
  6. It's 1 tsp of salt.
  7. You need 1 tsp of brown sugar.
  8. You need 1 tsp of coriander powder.
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp of black pepper.
  10. You need 1/2 tsp of cinnamon powder.
  11. It's 1/2 tsp of nutmeg powder.
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp of chilli powder.
  13. Prepare 5 tbs of olive oil.
  14. It's 150 ml of water.

Chicken wings with caramelized sauce step by step

  1. Clean the chicken wings and add the lemon juice.
  2. Take the ginger and garlic, and grind them manually using the mortar or you can also use blender.
  3. Add all the dry seasonings and mix it until it becomes fine paste.
  4. Marinate the chicken with this paste and add oyster sauce, once it's evenly distributed, cover the bowl and keep it in fridge for 2-3 hours.
  5. After 2 or 3 hours, place the pot on the stove on medium heat. Add some oil.
  6. Get the chicken from the fridge, put it in the pot and cover it.
  7. Let it cook for about 10mins, the water will automatically come out from the chicken, no need to add water in this stage.
  8. Check once is a while, don't let it burn when simmer. And flip the chicken if required, to get uniform colour on the skin, then add 150ml water.
  9. Chicken will get soften and fully cooked once the water is dried up.
  10. Once the chicken is cooked and the sauce becomes caramelized, you can remove the cover and let the water completely gone whilst flipping the chicken one by one.
  11. Remove from the pot once you get the desired colour of the chicken wings, serve it with fries or rice. Enjoy!.
