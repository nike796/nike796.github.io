Simple Chicken Fried Rice w/o the carrots, onions, and peas step by step

  1. Prepare Rice: Heat 1 1/2 cup of water to boiling. Pour in 1 cup of rice and 1 tablespoon of butter. Stir then set to simmer covered for 20 minutes.   2. Prepare Chicken: Chop chicken breast into quarter pieces. In a frying pan heat chicken at medium heat for about 7 minutes, stiring occasionally. Once finish sit aside..
  3. Prepare Egg: In a bowl stir egg. Heat egg in a pan at low-medium. Scramble egg until it is no longer moist. When done set aside..
