Elisa's Baked Chicken instructions

  1. Clean all the chicken trying to remove as much skin and fat as you can, I remove it from all chicken except drumsticks..
  2. Mix all ingredients, except the butter and the chicken, in a plastic bag . Then add the chicken and toss very well..
  3. Let the chicken rest in the fridge for about 3-4 hours..
  4. Preheat your oven to 350째F..
  5. In a baking pan, put your chicken and the butter and cover with aluminum foil. Poke some holes in the aluminum foil. And put in the oven..
  6. Turn your chicken after 15-20 minutes. Ready when internal temperature is 175째..
  7. Can uncover and broil for a nice color..
