Homestyle Fried Chicken step by step

  1. Mix eggs, milk or half n half until well combined in a wide or deep bowl.
  2. Mix flour and all seasonings until well combined in separate bowl.
  3. Dip chicken in flour mixture then into egg mixture and back into flour, set aside on plate and continue until all chicken pieces are coated.
  4. Set chicken aside, add oil to skillet and heat on medium to med-high until bubbly when u drop a pinch of flour in oil.
  5. Add chicken in oil slowly, cooking only 3-5 pieces at a time.
  6. Cook on first side for about 5-7 minutes to set crust then flip carefully.
  7. Keep cooking and flipping every so often until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear when poked with fork or knife.
  8. Take out and place on cookie sheet and put in warm oven while cooking the rest so that it doesn't get cold before serving.
  9. Each time a batch of chicken is done add to oven on pan until all chicken pieces are done.
