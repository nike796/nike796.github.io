Ingredients of Grilled Lamb chops with potatoes and carrot including rice with veggies

  4. It's 1 cup of rice.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of mixed veggies.
  6. It's 1 of onion, chopped.
  7. You need 2 scoops of Monkey Gland Sauce.

Grilled Lamb chops with potatoes and carrot including rice with veggies instructions

  1. Put lamb chops in a bowl chop onion in slices mix the steak and chops spice, worcester sauce, and one spoon of sugar in the bowl and mix together to have the taste..
  2. Boil rice for 10 minutes and in 15 minutes add mixed veggies.
  3. Then put potatoes and carrots on a flat tray in the oven and the lamb chops and onion..
  4. Oven heated up to 150 C on grill and bake, prepared and cooked for 1 hour 45 minutes.
