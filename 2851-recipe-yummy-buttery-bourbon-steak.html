Buttery Bourbon Steak step by step

  1. Season the steak.
  2. Add steaks to a hot skillet seasoned side down..
  3. Season the unseasoned side which is up.
  4. Turn every 3-4 minutes..
  5. After fourth turn add butter.
  6. While that is cooking to a pot add the butter and onions.
  7. Sauté the onion till tender then add the bourbon.
  8. Simmer for 10 minutes add to the steak.
  9. Add flour to the skillet with the steaks and make a Roux..
  10. After it has sautéed for 6 minutes add water with bouillon cube and stir till thickened.
  11. Let rest 5 minutes in the bourbon sauce.
  12. Serve I hope you enjoy!.
