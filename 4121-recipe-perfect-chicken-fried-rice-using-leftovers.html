Chicken Fried Rice using leftovers step by step

  1. Put 1 tablespoon oil in hot wok or frying panel and scramble eggs. Put aside..
  2. Put 1-2 tablespoons of oil in wok and fry rice until rice is soft (approximately 10 minutes). Stir frequently..
  3. Add soy sauce until rice is brown. Taste so you don't add too much soy sauce!.
  4. Add eggs, chicken, onions, and other vegetables (peppers are a good option).
  5. Cook until warm, stirring frequently..
