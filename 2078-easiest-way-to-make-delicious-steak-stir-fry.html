steak stir fry step by step

  1. add butter to a warm saute pan..
  2. add your favorite mixed veggies once butter is melted (I used carrots, onions, corn and green beans).
  3. once veggies start to cook up add your sliced up steak and potato..
  4. season it to your liking (paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper).
  5. let it cook up for a minute then add 3 oz water (or chicken stock)..
  6. let the water boil off and its ready to be served.
