Chicken Fried Steak instructions

  1. Start with 2 lbs cube steak..
  2. Whip Milk and eggs together in one bowl..
  3. Mix flour and seasonings in a second bowl..
  4. Dip each piece of steak in the flour first, then the milk mixture, and then in the flour again..
  5. Place each piece of steak on a plate after breading..
  6. Fry steak in hot oil and butter (medium high heat) approximately 2 minutes on each side..
  7. Remove steak from skillet after browning and keep warm while you make the gravy..
  8. After removing last steak from skillet, add 1/2 cup flour to leftover grease and stir until smooth and browned. (Medium high heat) Slowly add milk.
