Grilled Chicken Tikka Karahi instructions

  1. Add ginger garlic paste in chicken..
  2. Add chicken Tikka Masala, zeera powder and coriander powder..
  3. Add red chilli powder and turmeric powder..
  4. Add oinon paste, tomato paste and lemon juice and mix well. Marinate for 1 hour..
  5. Heat the oil in grill pan..
  6. Grill the chicken in Grilled pan, and keep aside..
  7. Heat oil in Karahi add chicken mixture and mix it.
  8. Add chopped tomato and fry oinon and mix well,add water and cover the lid on the low flame until water dried..
  9. The masala is ready add grilled chicken and 1/4 cup water and Cook well..
  10. Add Green chilli and mix well..
  11. Chicken is cooked add green coriander leaves and sprinkle the Garam Masala Powder..
  12. Serve hot with roti or naan. Enjoy the delicious Grilled Chicken Tikka Karahi..
