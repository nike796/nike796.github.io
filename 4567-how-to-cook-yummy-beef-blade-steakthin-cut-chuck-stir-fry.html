Beef blade steak(thin cut Chuck) stir fry step by step

  1. Mix Worcestershire sauce ,salt , and Weber Chicago steak seasoning together in a bowl..
  2. Rinse steak and pat dry..
  3. Add steak to the bowl with marinade and let sit for 30 minutes..
  4. Add olive oil to pan on high. Add steak to fry each side 3-4 minutes ..
  5. Turn off pan and cover with a lid for 5 minutes..
  6. Remove lid and place steak on as plate. Cut steaks in cubes and set aside..
  7. Coarse chop bell peppers and onions. Add them to the pan and sautee them for 5 minutes..
  8. Add mushrooms to pan for 3 minutes . Add the steak back to the pan ..
  9. Serve 'as is' or with rice..
