Pork Steak With Apple Sauce And Kumara Chips instructions

  1. Peel kumara and slice thinly. Put into bowl with oil and paprika and mix with hands so that the kumara is evenly coated. Lay chips out on a wire cooling rack and bake in oven at 200C for 20 minutes or until crispy..
  2. To make apple sauce peel and chop apple and place in saucepan with lemon, honey and 1/4 cup of water. Simmer for 10 minutes until apple is soft. Add mixed spice and blend until smooth..
  3. Cook pork steak in fry pan. Serve with chips and apple sauce while hot :).
