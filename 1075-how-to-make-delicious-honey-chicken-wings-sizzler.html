Honey chicken wings sizzler step by step

  1. Mix chicken wings, ginger powder, pepper powder, curd or cream and salt as required. Keep it in the fridge for marination..
  2. Make a paste of onion and tomatoes..
  3. Pour 2 tablespoon oil in a pan, cook chopped garlic, all three colour chopped capsicums, onion and tomato paste, chopped green chillies. Add chilli powder, Peri Peri, coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala. Cook the sauce for at least 15 to 20 mins..
  4. As the sauce is getting ready. Pan-fry the wings with ginger garlic paste. Toss them with honey. Once your sauce is ready. Add lemon juice and vinegar. Add the fried chicken wings to the sauce..
  5. Serve it with chopped coriander on the hot fairy sizzler plate..
