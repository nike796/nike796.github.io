Ingredients of Beef Gravy

  1. You need of Beef juices.
  2. It's of Water (I use hot or warm water so it'll heat up faster).
  3. You need of Beef bouillon cubes.
  4. You need of Cornstarch.
  5. It's of Salt.
  6. Prepare of Black pepper.
  7. You need of Garlic powder.

Beef Gravy step by step

  1. Take beef juices from pan and put into a saucepan. Use a little less than the amount you want of gravy. (example: if you wanted half a saucepan of gravy, use enough beef juices to get close to half since you'll be adding water.).
  2. Add salt, black pepper and garlic powder to taste..
  3. On medium/high heat, stir occasionally until the beef juices begin to boil..
  4. Once boiling, add some beef bouillon cubes for added flavor.
  5. Add a bit of hot or warm water to compensate for the added bouillon cubes..
  6. Bring back up to a low boil..
  7. Once at a boil, take a small cup and add a few teaspoons of the cornstarch and mix in a bit of water. Mix well until it is all combined. (This step is so you don't end up with lumps of cornstarch in your gravy.) Once mixed well, add to your gravy mixture..
  8. Keep mixing with a wire whisk so nothing burns. Gravy will start to thicken shortly. If it's not thick enough for you, add more cornstarch by repeating the previous step..
  9. When gravy is at desired thickness you're done! Take off heat and serve, enjoy!.
