great grandma's zucchini and steak step by step

  1. trim fat and cut steak into cubes.
  2. cut zucchini into bite sizes pieces.
  3. add olive oil to frying pan.
  4. Brown cubed steak.
  5. add zucchini, stir fry until zucchini starts to turn transparent.
  6. pour in gravy.
  7. mix spaghetti mix with 1 cup water, add to steak, zucchini and gravy.
  8. add seasonings and simmer until zucchini is tender.
  9. serve over rice.
