Ingredients of Country Fried Steak With Mushroom Sauce

  1. It's 6 of Cube steaks.
  2. You need 4 tbsp of Flour.
  3. Prepare 1 of Salt and pepper to taste.
  4. You need 1 1/2 lb of Sliced mushrooms.
  5. You need 1 can of Cream of mushroom soup.
  6. Prepare 1/3 cup of Half and half.
  7. It's 4 tbsp of Vegetable oil.

Country Fried Steak With Mushroom Sauce step by step

  1. First, heat oil to medium heat. Sprinkle salt and pepper on cube steak. Flour both sides of cube steak and lightly fry on both sides. Remove and set aside..
  2. Next, add mushrooms to pan with remaining oil and scrap the bits of the bottom of pan. After about 3 minutes add in the mushroom soup and cream. Mix to combine and add back the steaks. Simmer on low for about 5-10 minutes..
