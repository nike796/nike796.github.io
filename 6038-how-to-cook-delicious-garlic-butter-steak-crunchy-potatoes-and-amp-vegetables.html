Garlic Butter Steak, Crunchy Potatoes & Vegetables! instructions

  1. 1- Slice Carrots & Add Corn Cobbs, frozen Peas & Carrots To steamer or pot & cook for 15-20 minutes or until cooked..
  2. 2- Preheat deep fryer to hot chips setting or fairly high. Whilst heating up, Season Cubed potatoes with chives & salt. Once the fryer is hot Add potatoes & cook until golden Brown..
  3. 3- Heat Pan & cook Steak Until Desired. Whilst steak is cooking mix Garlic & Softened butter until smooth, scoop in to 3 separate ball piles & put in the freezer to firm until the steaks are ready..
  4. 4- When Everything is cooked serve & Add a scoop of garlic butter on each steak..
  5. 5- Sit Back & Enjoy :).
