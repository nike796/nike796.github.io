  2. You need 3 of medium pork chops.

Pork stew and shirataki noodles instructions

  1. Place your shirataki noodles in hot water then boil for 10-15 min till firm but tender to the bite remove from hot water once cooked and set aside.
  2. Chop the pork into cubes, and fry for 5 minutes until cooked remove from pan and set a side..
  3. Add vegetables and fry till soft in pan for 5-7 minutes add coconut cream and bring to a boil. Until it has been reduced to a thicker consistency.
  4. Add chicken to the sauce that you have made, add salt and pepper to your taste.
  5. Plate your noodles and sauce and enjoy!.
