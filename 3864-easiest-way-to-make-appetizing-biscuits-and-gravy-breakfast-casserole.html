Biscuits and Gravy Breakfast Casserole instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Spray 9x13 pan.
  3. Cut biscuits into 1 inch pieces and line bottom of pan.
  4. Brown sausage and scatter over biscuits.
  5. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese.
  6. Whisk eggs and milk with a pinch of salt and pepper and pour over the pan.
  7. Make gravy mix per package directions and pour over.
  8. Bake in oven for about 35-40 minutes.
