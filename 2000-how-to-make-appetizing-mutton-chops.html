Mutton Chops instructions

  1. 1. Wash and drain the chops in a colander.
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients together. Rub onto the chops making sure they are fully coated.
  3. Next add the oil and lemon juice and mix well Leave to marinate for 1-2 hours - overnight works best.
  4. Heat oil in a large wide based non stick frying pan Once it's nearly smoking hot add the chop.
  5. Cook for 1-2 minutes & turn the side and add any leftover marinade and keep turning and basting with oil and marinade as you cook further.
  6. Cook chops till done to your preference Remove and serve immediately..
