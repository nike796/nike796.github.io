Ingredients of Beef steak with bell peppers

  1. It's 1 of Ribeye steak.
  2. Prepare Pinch of sea salt and ground pepper.
  3. It's 1 Table spoon of butter.
  4. Prepare 1 of green or red bell pepper.
  5. It's 4 of Table spoons of Olive Oil.
  6. It's 2 Sprigs of Rosemary (dried or fresh).

I know some purists prefer to eat a great steak with nothing more than salt and pepper as seasoning. Bell pepper adds a spicy flavor to this hearty beef steak. A flavorful Italian-style marinade adds flavor and tenderness to beef steaks. Pre-heat until the oil just beginning to smoke.

Beef steak with bell peppers step by step

  1. Defrost the beef until room temperature, marinade the meat with chopped rosemary(or use dry one). Rub with olive oils, sea salt and ground pepper.
  2. Heat up iron or pan until it get some smoke. The pan needs to be very hot. Grill the steak with high heat. Each side for 2 minutes.
  3. Before finished grilling, put butter on top and let it melt into steak. Then rest the steak on the cutting board for 3 minutes.
  4. For bell peppers, use the same pan, put olive oil. Wait until pan is warm. Stir fry until bell peppers are soft..
  5. Season bell peppers with salt and ground peppers.
  6. Serve steak and bell peppers with mustard sauce.
