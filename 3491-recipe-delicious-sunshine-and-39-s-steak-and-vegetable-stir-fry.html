Ingredients of sunshine's steak and vegetable stir fry

  3. You need 1 tbsp of minced garlic.

  5. Prepare 1/4 cup of vegetable oil.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of sliced green onions (1 inch slices).
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of green peppers cut into 1 inch squares.

  9. You need 2 of stalks celery chopped.
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp of cornstarch.
  11. Prepare 1 of salt and.pepper to taste.
  12. It's 1/2 cup of chopped white onions.
  13. It's 1 1/2 cup of water divided.
