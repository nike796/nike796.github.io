Ingredients of Easy "Baked" Chicken

  1. Prepare 1 of Chicken breast.
  2. It's 3 slice of Bacon.
  3. You need 1/2 of of an onion Onion.
  4. It's 1/3 bunch of Green onion.
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp of Butter (or margarine).
  6. It's 1 tbsp of White flour.
  7. Prepare 100 ml of Milk.
  8. You need 30 grams of Cream cheese.
  9. It's 1 of Krazy Salt.
  10. It's 1 of Pepper.

Easy "Baked" Chicken step by step

  1. Take the skin off the chicken breast and cut it into 1-2 cm wide strips. Season with the Krazy Salt and pepper. Cut the bacon into 1-2 cm wide pieces, and slice the onion thinly. Chop the green onion..
  2. Put the butter in the frying pan and heat. Sauté the chicken and onion over medium heat, adding the bacon a bit later..
  3. When the chicken is cooked through and the onions are softened, turn down the heat to low and add the flour. Sauté until it's no longer floury..
  4. Add the milk little by little, mixing well between additions..
  5. Add the cream cheese and mix in..
  6. Season with Krazy Salt and pepper, add the green onions and mix together well..
  7. Use as a sandwich filling using pita bread for instance, or just put it on a serving plate and eat with rice. It should be good as a rice bowl topping too..
