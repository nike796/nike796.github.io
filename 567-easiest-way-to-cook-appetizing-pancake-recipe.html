Pancake Recipe step by step

  1. First separate the egg whites from the yolk in 2 different bowls.
  2. Add 2 tbsp of all purpose flour and 1 tablespoon of milk to the egg yolk mix well and add 1 tablespoon of vanilla essence and continue mixing until you get a consistent batter.
  3. In 2nd bowl of egg white, add a teaspoon of lemon juice. Stir well. Beat the mixture until its foamy..
  4. Now add 3 tablespoons of powder sugar and beat the mixture until you get nice fluffy batter. this is a very important step. If you see that the better is runny you could add more powder sugar to it.
  5. Take one third of this batter and mix it to the yolk.
  6. And then add remaining of it and mix well gently..
  7. Now take a pan and heat it on a medium flame. Grease it with some butter. Next time I'm not going use butter or anything. Add one scoop of the batter. Pour a teaspoon of water in the pan and close the lid. Let it cook for 2 minutes on a very slow flame..
  8. After 2 minutes add another scoop of batter. Pour some water on the sides and close the lid. Let it cook for 3 or 4 minutes..
  9. Now flip the pancake. Again add teaspoon of water at the sides. Close the lid and let the other side of the pancake cook for another 3 or 4 minutes.
  10. Serve hot with chunks of salted butter :).
