Ingredients of Dabeli

  1. Prepare 7-8 of Boiled Potatoes.
  2. It's As per taste of Salt.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of roasted fennel+ coriander coarse mix.
  4. Prepare 2-3 tbsp of Shreeji Dabeli Masala.
  5. It's 1/2 of lemon juice.
  6. Prepare 2-3 of coarse green Chillies.
  7. It's Some of Chopped coriander leaves.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of pomegranate seeds.
  9. You need 8-10 of Homemade Pav (Recipe is uploaded).
  10. Prepare 1 cup of spicy green chutney (Recipe is uploaded).
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup of termarind-dates chutney.
  12. You need 2-3 of finely chopped onions.
  13. You need 1/2 cup of masala sing (ground nuts)(homemade).
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup of Homemade Simple Sev (Recipe is uploaded).
  15. You need Some of Homemade Achaar Masala (for more spiciness).
  16. It's as required of Oil or butter for Roasting.

Dabeli step by step

  1. Boiled the Potatoes. Peel them. Mash all Potatoes in mixing bowl..
  2. Add salt, fennel+ coriander coarse mix, crushed green chillies, Dabeli masala..
  3. Mix it all well. Add chopped coriander, squeeze lemon juice..
  4. Mix it all well. Add some fresh promogranate seeds. Get all ingredients ready to assemble..
  5. Now assemble the Dabeli. Cut the pav from the middle. Apply tamarind-dates chutney on both sides. Apply Spicy green chutney. And take one scope of a spoon filled of Dabeli stuffing place it..
  6. Spread it on one side of pav. Now spread some masala sing, Chopped onions. Want spicier than sprinkle some achaar masala..
  7. Close it. Make others. Place them on hot tawa. Apply oil or butter. Toast it from both sides. And serve hot with Chutneys and green chillies. Enjoy the Spicyness and osm taste..
