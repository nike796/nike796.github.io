Chicken steak burgers instructions

  1. Cut chicken breast into small steak pieces and marinate it with ketchup, salt,peppers, red chilli flakes and soya sauce.
  2. Shallow fry chicken or grill it.
  3. In a pan add butter and saute brown sugar add onion rings ho caramelize.
  4. Apply ketchup or chili garlic on bun put steak pieces add mayonnaise barbecue sauce and caramelize onion and serve.
