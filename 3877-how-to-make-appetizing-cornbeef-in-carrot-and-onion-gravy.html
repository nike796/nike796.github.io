Ingredients of Cornbeef in carrot and onion gravy

  1. It's 5 lb of cornbeef brisket.
  2. Prepare 5 cup of sweet onion.
  3. Prepare 1 lb of baby carrots.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of meat tenderizer.
  5. It's 1 tbsp of granulated garlic powder.
  6. You need 2 tbsp of fresh ground black pepper.
  7. You need 1/3 cup of vegetable oil.
  8. It's 1/3 cup of water.
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup of cornstarch.

Cornbeef in carrot and onion gravy instructions

  1. Mix the cornstarch and water set it aside..
  2. Heat a big pan add oil when hot add the brisket.
  3. Add onions and carrots. Add half the spices. When the brisket is browned turn it over let cook covered 10 minutes..
  4. Take the brisket out and slice it return back to pan. Let cook covered 3 minutes stir cornstarch solution and add to pan stirring constantly till it thickens..
  5. Let sit 10 minutes covered serve hope you enjoy!.
