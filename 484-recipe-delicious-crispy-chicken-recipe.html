Crispy chicken recipe step by step

  1. Chop your onion and put it in a preheated pan with butter, garlic and ginger and let it fry for 4 minutes just to mix the ingredients very well.
  2. In a separate pan add your dried chicken seasoned with salt and pepper in a preheated pan.
  3. Fry each side for 7 minutes on a medium high heat.
  4. Fry the other side for the other 7 minutes and put your onion salad on top(optional).
  5. Drizzle your lemon juice over the chicken and add the onion sauce mix on top.
  6. Serve in a plate and decorate with your fresh parsley. Serve with salad.
