Ingredients of Paleo Seared tuna with citrus marinade and sauted spinach with mushrooms, onions, and bacon

  1. Prepare of seared tuna and marinade.
  2. Prepare 2 of tuna stakes.
  3. Prepare 1 of fresh squeezed lime.
  4. You need 2 clove of of garlic crushed.
  5. You need 1/4 tsp of salt and pepper.
  6. You need 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  7. You need 1/2 tbsp of coconut oil.
  8. You need 1 tsp of toasted sesame seeds.
  9. You need 1/2 tsp of ginger powder or 1 teaspoon fresh grated.
  10. Prepare of Sauted spinach, mushrooms, onions, and bacon.
  11. It's 3 cup of fresh spinach.
  12. You need 1/2 small of red onion cut in half and sliced thin.
  13. Prepare 2 medium of mushrooms sliced.
  14. It's 3 of srips of bacon sliced into 1/2 inch pieces.
  15. It's 1 clove of of garlic sliced thin or minced.
  16. You need 1 of salt and pepper to taste.

Paleo Seared tuna with citrus marinade and sauted spinach with mushrooms, onions, and bacon step by step

  1. Whisk marinade ingredients add tuna stake set in fridge for about 20 mins. Turning at lesst once so the fish is completely covered.
  2. Heat fry pan add chopped bacon.
  3. Fry bacon until light brown. Add onions and mushrooms.
  4. Cook bacon, onions, and mushrooms until onions are translucent. Add garlic..
  5. Meanwhile, place flat top grill on stove and heat on low temp. Or ready another fry pan for tuna..
  6. When garlic has become a beautiful light brown and the bacon is well cooked add 3 cups of fresh spinach. ** I would only add one cup at a time because fresh spinach is so fluffy until it cooks down.**.
  7. On the flat top (or fry pan) add 1 teaspoon of olive oil and place tuna on..
  8. Sear tuna on each side for about 2 min. You want the center of the stake to be nice, pink, and juicy!.
  9. Add a squeeze of lemon and enjoy!.
  10. Last note*** I love tasted almond slices so I topped the sauted spinach with some. :).
