Cube Steak with Homemade gravy instructions

  3. once steak is done take them out and empty out the oil but not the left over residue. it is best to use left over oil that u cooked chicken in the night before. Add the onions and stuff to the reside. Aslo Add the 3-4 tablespoons of flour..
