Potato lyonnaise with grilled chicken step by step

  1. Make a marinade of yaji, soysauce,ginger and garlic powder, maggi. Wash and marinade chicken overnight.
  2. Peel and cut your potatoes in round. Wash and boil aldente in salted water. Cut your onions in large slices.
  3. Grease your baking tray and put your chicken. Pop into hot oven for 15 to 20minutes. Bring out then add your cooking butter, potatoes and onions. Sprinkle some black pepper. Put back in oven till potatoes are tender and onions are sauteed.
