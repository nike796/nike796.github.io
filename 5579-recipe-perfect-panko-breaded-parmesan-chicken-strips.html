  3. You need 1 box of panko crumbs.
  4. Prepare 2 cup of parmesan cheese.
  5. You need 1 of salt and pepper to taste.
  6. You need 1/4 cup of rosemary.
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup of basil.
  8. You need 1 tbsp of ground sage.

  2. Dip chicken strips in egg and transfer into breading mix dipping and patting to get good coverage.
  3. Deep fry at 390Â°F for 5- 6 minutes for every 3-4 strips..
  4. Transfer to a plate with paper towels to soak the grease. Add salt to taste once dry..
  5. Serve with honey mustard and white country gravy.
