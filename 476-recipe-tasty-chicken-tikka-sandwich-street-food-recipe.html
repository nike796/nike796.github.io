Chicken Tikka sandwich (street food recipe) step by step

  1. Chicken mein yogurt Ke sath sare masaly daal kar mix karein..
  2. 1 ghanty ke liye rakh dein, ke achi trah masala jazab hojaye..
  3. Pateeli mein chicken dal ke yogurt ka pani dry hony tak cook karein..
  4. Koyla grm kar lein. Chicken Tikka ready mein steel ka bowl rakhein. GaramKoyla rakhein..
  5. Oil Ke Few drops Koyla par dal kar dhkn de dein. 20 mint smoke dein..
  6. Egg ke liye egg me tomato, green chilli salt or red chilli powder dal ke mix karein..
  7. Taway par slice ko hlka colour dein...
  8. Dono Slice par mayonnaise lgayen.
  9. Chicken Tikka boti ke raishy kar lein..
  10. Slice par mayonnaise lagake chicken Tikka lgayen. 2sre slice se cover kar ke press kar dein.
  11. Taway par oil dal Ke 1 mint garam karein. Egg batter phaila ke dal dein..
  12. Anch dum jitni rakhein. Phir 2 mint baad chicken Tikka sandwich egg omlait ke medium me rakh dein..
  13. Egg omlait se slice ko 4aro trf se cover karein..
  14. Plat kar dousri side se b 1 mint cook karein..
  15. Ketchup ke sath serve karein..
