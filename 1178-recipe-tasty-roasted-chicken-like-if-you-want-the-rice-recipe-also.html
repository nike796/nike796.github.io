  2. Start by putting salt all over just enough that u think will make the chicken have some taste not too salty tho maybe 3 spoons or less will do.
  3. Than sprinkle the black pepper all over the chicken.
  4. Sprinkle the paprika and the mixed spice if u didn't us butter sprinkle oil all. Over the chicken.
  5. Coat chicken all over put into foil and put a plate under a Medel or glass pan will do.
