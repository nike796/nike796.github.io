Ingredients of Pork Chops w/ Buttermilk Gravy

  1. It's 1 cup of all purpose flour.
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp of onion powder.
  3. You need 2 tbsp of garlic powder.
  4. You need 1 tsp of cayenne.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of salt.
  6. You need 1/2 tsp of fresh ground pepper.
  7. Prepare 4 of pork chops.
  8. You need 1/4 cup of olive oil.
  9. It's 1 cup of chicken broth.
  10. You need 1/2 cup of buttermilk.
  11. You need 1 of chopped parsley (garnish).

Pork Chops w/ Buttermilk Gravy step by step

  1. place flour in a shallow plate and add onion powder, garlic powder, cayenne, salt, and pepper. mix with fork until seasoning is even..
  2. cover pork chops with the flour mixture evenly throughout..
  3. heat up a saute pan with oil over medium heat. place pork chops in the heated oil and fry 3 minutes per side until golden brown. at the same time you may saute onions or mushrooms as an optional topping. remove pork chops from the pan along with the optional toppings, leaving the pan drippings behind..
  4. sprinkle a few pinches of the seasoned flour into the pan with the left behind fat. mix contents in the pan until flour dissolves. pour in the chicken broth and let it cook for 5 minutes until it slightly thickens. stir in the buttermilk and return the pork chops to the pan . If toppings were sautéed, place them on top of the pork chops..
  5. simmer the contents for 5 minutes until pork is cooked through. season with salt and pepper with chopped parsley before serving.
