Beef Noodle Stir Fry step by step

  1. Cook noodles according to the package directions..
  2. Fry the green bell pepper, ginger, and the garlic with the olive oil in a pan..
  3. Add the beef, soy sauce, and the juice of the limes to the pan..
  4. Add the cooked noodles to the pan..
  5. Mix well, add honey..
  6. Serve..
