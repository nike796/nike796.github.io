Ingredients of Zaxby chicken finger sauce

  1. Prepare 1/2 cup of mayonnaise.
  2. It's 1/4 cup of ketchup.
  3. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of garlic powder.
  4. It's 1/4 tsp of worcestershire sauce.
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp of ground black pepper.

Zaxbys Chicken Fingers Dipping Sauce Recipe. Zaxby's Restaurant is a chain of restaurants found in the Southern part of the US. They are know for serving Chicken Fingersand for their famous Zax Sauce. This signature Zaxby's Sauce is perfect dip for chicken or french fries.

Zaxby chicken finger sauce instructions

  1. Wisk mayo ketchup and garlic powder In a bowl until well blended.
  2. Add Worcestershire sauce and wisk until smooth add pepper to taste place in fridge let ingredients blend for about an hour.
