Ingredients of Beef Tapa

  1. Prepare 3 1/2 tbsp of vinegar.
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp of salt.
  3. You need 7 tbsp of white/brown sugar.
  4. You need 1 tsp of ground blackpeper.
  5. You need 1 head of garlic, minced.
  6. Prepare 1 of cooking oil for frying.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of soy sauce.
  8. Prepare 1 lb of beef sirloin/ thin sliced.
