My Crispy Deep fried Beef with Dip. ðŸ˜Š step by step

  2. Heat up the oil when it's hot test with a pinch of flour if it bubbles right away you can add the coated beef leave to cook for 3 -5 minutes. Let it drain on kitchen paper..
