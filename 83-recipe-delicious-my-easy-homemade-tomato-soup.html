Ingredients of My Easy Homemade Tomato Soup

  1. You need 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  2. You need 1 of large onion, diced.
  3. Prepare 4 of garlic cloves, minced.
  4. It's 3 tbsp of all-purpose flour.
  5. You need of Salt snd pepper.
  6. Prepare 2 (14.5 oz) of cans crushed or diced tomatoes.
  7. It's 2 1/2 cups of chicken stock.
  8. You need 1 tbsp of Italian seasoning.
  9. It's 1/8 tsp of ground all spice.
  10. You need 1 cup of milk (optional).

My Easy Homemade Tomato Soup instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan or dutch oven over medium heat..
  2. Cook the onions until they’re soft (about 5 minutes), then add the garlic and cook for a further minute or so..
  3. Season with salt and pepper..
  4. Add the flour, and mix well, cook for 30 seconds..
  5. Add the tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, ground all spice and stock, whisking to blend and thicken..
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 5-10 minutes..
  7. Using an immersion blender blend until smooth..
  8. Remove from heat, then add the cream if using. I used Horizon Organic White Milk, Mixing thoroughly..
  9. Test for seasoning, add more is needed, then serve..
  10. Garnish with dried parsley, croutons, green onions and then devour..
