Jollof rice and chicken with plantain (Ramadhan recipe contest) step by step

  1. Parboil the rice and set aside, blend crayfish, tomatoes, pepper,onions,garlic and ginger and set aside..
  2. Wash the chicken, add seasoning, onions and steam then fry and mix with small stew. Slice the plantain and fry..
  3. Place the pot on the fire and pour vegetable oil, add the blended ingredient and tomato paste into the hot oil and fry, add the chicken stock till all the water dry.
  4. Adjust the seasoning then Pour the parboiled rice into the stew and mix verywell add little water depending on the type of rice. Cover and cook with medium heat..
  5. When the rice is done add the carrot and green pepper and mix then cover and allow it simmer for like 5 minutes..
  6. Enjoy with dice carrot, cucumber and plantain..
