Chipotle Cilantro Lime Grilled Chicken instructions

  1. Season chicken legs lightly with salt and pepper..
  2. Puree (In a blender for 2 minutes) the remaining ingredients and marinade the chicken legs for at least 4 hours up to the day before..
  3. Take the chicken out of the marinade and reserve the remaining marinade for later.
  4. On a clean and well lubricated grill with a medium to medium high heat grill until the chicken reaches 170 degrees internal temperature..
  5. As you grill it towards the end, brush with the leftover marinade let cook additional 5 minutes to insure it is fully cooked..
  6. You also can grill for about 10 minutes and get the color and grill marks. Then finish in A 350 degree oven for 20 minutes or until they reach 170 ⁰ F. internal temperature..
