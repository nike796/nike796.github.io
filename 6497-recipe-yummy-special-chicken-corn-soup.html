Ingredients of Special Chicken Corn Soup

  1. It's 1/2 Pound of Chicken.
  2. It's 1 tsp of Salt.
  3. It's 1 tsp of Black Pepper.
  4. Prepare 1 TBsp of Soya Sauce.
  5. You need 1/4 Cup of Corn ground.
  6. It's 3 TBSp of Corn Flour.
  7. Prepare of Knor chicken cube 1.

Special Chicken Corn Soup step by step

  1. Boil Chicken in 6 glasses of water with salt. And shred the chicken and put it aside, separating the stock that becomes 1/2..
  2. Now take chicken stock, add knor chicken cube, roughly ground corns, black pepper & Soya Sauce. Cook 10 minutes. Then add Cornflour dissolved in water. Keep stirring..
  3. When it is thick, add chicken. Mix. Then slowly pour beaten egg. Keep stirring until it is ready to serve. Taste salt & pepper, if required add more..
  4. Serve with cut green chilies dipped in vinegar, hot sauce & Soya sauce. Enjoy.
