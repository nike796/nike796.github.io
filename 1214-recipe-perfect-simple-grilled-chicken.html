Ingredients of Simple grilled chicken

  1. Prepare 800 gm of chicken pieces.
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup of hung curd.
  3. It's 2 tbsp of ginger garlic paste.
  4. You need 1 tsp of green chilli paste.
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of olive oil or of your choice.
  6. It's 2 tbsp of kashmiri red chilli powder.
  7. You need 1 tsp of coriander powder.
  8. You need 1 tbsp of roasted jeera powder.
  9. It's 1 tsp of garam masala powder.
  10. Prepare 1 tsp of black pepper powder.
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp of kasoori methi.
  12. Prepare 2 tbsp of lemon juice.
  13. It's to taste of Salt.

Simple grilled chicken instructions

  1. Wash the chicken and pat dry. In a bowl mix curds,ginger garlic paste, green chilli paste, olive oil, red chilli powder, coriander powder, jeera powder, garam masala powder, black pepper powder, kasoori methi,lemon juice and salt. Add the chicken pieces,mix it with the masala and keep it for 2 hours.
  2. Place the grilling net on stove and on medium place the chicken pieces on it and cook for 7 to 8 mins and flip,cook on the other side also for 7 to 8 mins.
  3. Serve it with mint chutney and onion rings.
