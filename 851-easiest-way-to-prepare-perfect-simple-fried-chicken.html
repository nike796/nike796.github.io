Simple fried chicken step by step

  1. Wash chicken very well and cut into pieces.
  2. Put in clean pot and put on fire on medium heat.
  3. Add little water,cut onion into chunks and add maggi cubes.
  4. Add crushed garlic and seasonings.
  5. Boil fr 15 to 20 mins,then strain.
  6. Put oil in frying pan and put on fire.
  7. When hot,add chicken pieces and fry chicken until golden brown and enjoy :).
