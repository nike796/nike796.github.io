Funyun Chicken Strips step by step

  1. In 3 medium shallow dishes: Dish #1 - flour with salt, pepper, other desired herbs & spices to taste (teaspoon or 2 of each). Dish #2 - egg, milk, salt, pepper & hot sauce to taste. Dish #3- funyuns mashed or processed into small crumbs/powder.
  2. Trim excess fat off chicken strips. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper, or spray liberally with cooking spray. Line up, in order, on counter: chicken, flour, egg/milk wash, funyun dust, baking sheets..
  3. Coat each chicken tender lightly in flour, then soak for 15-30 seconds in egg wash, then push into funyuns coating thickly, then onto the pan..
  4. Bake at 400°F for 15-20 minutes on 1st side & 10-15 minutes on 2nd side, depending on thickness, until golden brown & crispy. Enjoy with whatever dipping sauce(s) you like..
