Ingredients of Chicken adobo with Chicken gizzard and liver

  1. Prepare 4 pcs of any chicken parts.
  2. You need of Chicken liver.
  3. You need of Chicken gizzard.
  4. You need 8 tbsp of soy sauce.
  5. It's 4 tbsp of vinegar.
  6. Prepare 3 of crushed garlic.
  7. Prepare Half of onion.
  8. You need 1 1/2 cup of water.
  9. You need of Sugar.
  10. It's of Pepper.
  11. Prepare of Salt.

Chicken adobo with Chicken gizzard and liver step by step

  1. Marinate the chicken on soy sauce and garlic for at least 1 hr.
  2. Pan fry the chicken until brown then set aside.
  3. Put some oil. Then add the onions and garlic..
  4. When the garlic and onions are fragrant. Saute the chicken gizzard until it is slightly tender. Then add the chicken liver until it goes brown.
  5. Put some salt and pepper.
  6. Pour the marinade over then add sugar and water to avoid burning the soy sauce. put the chicken back in the pan..
  7. When the gizzard is tender and the sauce starts simmering. Add the vinegar.(Don't stir.).
  8. Wait until it thickens..
  9. Turn of the fire then serve with bed of rice..
