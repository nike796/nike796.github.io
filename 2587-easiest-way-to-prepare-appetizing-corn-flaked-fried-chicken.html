corn flaked fried chicken instructions

  1. preheat your oil too 400.
  2. beat your egg then add it to your cup of milk.
  3. add garlic salt red pepper and onion powder to your flour and mix (i usually throw in some Andy's hot n spicy batter also) throw in however much you feel is necessary to your liking.
  4. put corn flakes into a bowl and crush then how ever much you can.
  5. You can cut your chicken if you want to make it into strips or pieces.
  6. drop your chicken into the milk and egg mixture.
  7. take your chicken and drop it into the batter and cover the chicken in it then dip it into the milk/egg mix then role it around in the corn flakes.
  8. when oil is ready place chicken in it and cook until it starts turning a dark golden brown.
