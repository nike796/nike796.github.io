Ingredients of Brad's bacon parm burger

  1. You need 1 lb of lean ground beef.
  2. It's 5 of strips of bacon, diced.
  3. You need 1/3 cup of parmesan or parmesan blend, grated.
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp of each salt, pepper, garlic powder, chili powder.
  5. It's 6 slice of cheddar or american cheese.
  6. Prepare 3 of whole wheat buns.
  7. It's 1 tbsp of apple cranberry chutney (found in my 5 spice pork chop recipe).
  8. Prepare 1 of onion lettuce and tomato if desired.

Brad's bacon parm burger instructions

  2. make into 1/3 pound pattys. fry or grill burgers until done..
  3. plate toasted buns. add cheese to bottom. add burger patty. add other slice of cheese. add chutney. then add desired toppings..
  4. get a napkin. enjoy.
