Sausage Gravy instructions

  1. Brown sausage while sprinkling half of the flour so the sausage absorbs it. Gradually add all of the flour..
  2. Once sausage is browned add the milk while stirring over medium heat. Stirring constantly..
  3. Continue to stir while gravy thickens. 12-15 minutes.
  4. Serve over your favorite southern styled biscuits!!.
