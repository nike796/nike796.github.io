Ingredients of Easy lemon pie

  1. You need of Crust.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of melted salted butter.
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of graham cracker crumbs or galleta María crumbs.
  4. Prepare of Filling.
  5. Prepare 1 can of condensed milk I use la lechera.
  6. It's of Small can of table cream (crema de leche) it’s usually 1/2 cup.
  7. Prepare 1/3 of green lime fresh juice (add more if you like).

Easy lemon pie instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350f.
  2. Melt butter then in a bowl add the butter the crumbs and mix them until they come together, take the pie mold I use the Publix one grease it really well and start forming the crust in the Mold making it really compact when you are done covering all the mold put it on the oven for 10-15 minutes until you see the crust is getting brownish and get it out and put it on a side..
  3. In a blender or nutribullet add the can of condensed milk the can of table cream and the fresh lemon juice and blend together really well!.
  4. Put this mixture in the already baked crust add green lime zest on the top for the final touch and put it on the fridge for at least 1-2 hours before consuming..
