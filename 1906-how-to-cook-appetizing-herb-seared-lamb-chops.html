Ingredients of Herb Seared Lamb Chops

  1. You need of Lamb.
  2. You need 2 large of lamb chops.
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of minced rosemary.
  4. Prepare 2 tsp of salt.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of freshly ground black pepper.
  6. Prepare 4 tbsp of olive oil, divided.
  7. You need 3 clove of garlic, minced.

Herb Seared Lamb Chops instructions

  1. In a small bowl mix rosemary, salt, pepper, garlic and 2 tbsp olive oil together..
  2. Rub mix on lamb chops and set in refrigerator for 30 minutes..
  3. Heat remaining 2 tbsp olive oil in sauce pan over high heat..
  4. Remove lamb chops from refrigerator and sear 1-2 minutes each side..
