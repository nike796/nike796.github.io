Chicken Steak With Creamy Sauce step by step

  1. Hammer the chicken. In a bowl add chicken, olive oil, salt, garlic-ginger paste, Paprika powder, lemon juice, red chilli powder, coriander powder, zeera powder and mix it well..
  2. Now grill the marinated chicken. In a pan add butter, milk, salt, black pepper, parmesan cheese, cream, and cook until thickened..
  3. In other pan add butter, add all the vegetables and saute for few minutes. In a bowl add boiled potatoes, butter and mash it well. Add 2 tbsp milk, parsley, salt and black pepper and mix it well..
  4. Your tasty Chicken Steak with creamy sauce, mashed potatoes and fryed vegetables is now ready..
