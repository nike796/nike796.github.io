Ingredients of Sorakai kulambu (Bottle gourd gravy)

  1. Prepare 1/2 bottle of gourd cut into small pieces.
  2. Prepare 1 of onion chopped.
  3. You need 1 of tomato chopped.
  4. You need 1 tsp of kulambu milagai thool (or chilly powder and coriander powder).
  5. Prepare 1 of small gooseberry sized tamarind.
  6. You need of Mustard seeds to temper.
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp of oil.
  8. Prepare of Salt as required.

Sorakai kulambu (Bottle gourd gravy) step by step

  1. Soak tamarind in hot water for 20 mins. Extract juice out of it..
  2. Heat a pan with oil and temper with mustard seeds..
  3. Add onion and sauté well. Add Bottle gourd and cook it covered until it leaves water. Add tomato and cook it covered until it’s cooked. Add kulambu milagai thool and salt. Sauté until raw smell goes. Add tamarind water and cook for 5 to 10 mins..
  4. Serve with rice.
