Arizona Fire Steak instructions

  1. Mix all spices together in a bowl with a whisk,than marinate your steaks in this marination for minimum 2 hours..
  2. Take 2 piece of marinated undercut,heat 1 tsp oil in a grill pan,add in the steaks,and cook for 8-10 mins almost 4 min on each side.Remove steaks on a tray,serve with fire sauce,mashed potatoes and veggies..
  3. Fire Sauce Method: Combine all ingredients and blend together for 2 mins.Sauce is ready..
  4. Mashed Potatoes Peel boil and mashed potatoes.Heat butter,add garlic than set aside.Take mashed potatoes add garlic butter and all ingredients than again mash well.Mashed potatoes is ready to serve..
