Easy, quick, pan fried chicken tenders step by step

  1. Heat approx 1/4 inch deep vegetable oil in high wall pan.
  2. Beat eggs in one bowl and crush crackers in another.
  3. Rub chicken with Weber honey garlic rub.
  4. Dip chicken in eggs until coated and then in crackers until coated.
  5. Fry until both sides are golden brown.
