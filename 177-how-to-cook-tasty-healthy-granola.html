Ingredients of Healthy Granola

  1. Prepare 1/2 cup of coconut oil.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of maple syrup.
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup of honey.
  4. It's 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon.
  5. It's 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
  6. It's 3 cups of old fashioned organic oats.
  7. It's 1 cup of sliced almonds.
  8. It's 1 cup of rasins.

Healthy Granola instructions

  1. Preheat oven at 300° fh.
  2. In a bowl mix together oil, syrup, salt and cinnamon.
  3. Mix in oats and almonds and Rasins.
  4. Pour all of it on a greased cookie sheet and flatten.
  5. Bake for 12 min.
  6. Stir and reflatten bake for additional 10 min or till almonds are golden brown..
  7. Place some where to cool.
