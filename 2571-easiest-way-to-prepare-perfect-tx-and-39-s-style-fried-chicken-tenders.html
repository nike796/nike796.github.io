TX's Style ~ Fried Chicken Tenders instructions

  1. Preheat oven 350°.
  2. Place milk in one bowl.
  3. In a seperate bowl mix flour and season salt.
  4. Cover a sheet pan with foil, for easy clean up afterwards.
  5. Place chicken in milk, one at a time coat each tender with the flour mixture and place on foil, arrange chicken so they are all together - keeps the butter on and around the chicken.
  6. Cut a pat of butter in half and place each of them on 1 chicken tender, repeat.
  7. Bake on center rack for 45 min to an hour, depending on how well your oven bakes, gas oven - shorter baking time.
