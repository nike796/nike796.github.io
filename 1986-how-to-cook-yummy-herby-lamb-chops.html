Herby lamb chops step by step

  1. Combine chopped leaves of sprigs of rosemary and dry thyme with grated peel of half lemon and salt.
  2. Add crushed coriander seeds. Rub mixture on lamb chops and marinate for 10minutes.
  3. Baste chops with meat basting sauce and braai over medium coals to your liking.
  4. Serve with lemon wedges and green chillis.
