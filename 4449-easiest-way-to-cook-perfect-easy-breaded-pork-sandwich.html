Easy breaded pork sandwich instructions

  1. Take small amount of flour on plate mix flour chips and crumbs in bowl with garlic powder mix.
  2. Cut chops in half take of fat and hammer flat I use iron skillet.
  3. Take 3 eggs cup of milk beat roll pork in flour dip in egg /milk roll in crumbs repeat if want.
  4. Deep fry till golden brown garnish how u wish.
