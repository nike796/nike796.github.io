Chicken Jalfrezi and Fried Rice step by step

  1. Wash and put chicken in a large bowl. Add all marination ingredients. Leave aside for 30 minutes..
  2. In a large pan, add oil. When oil is hot, add thinly sliced garlic and fry. Add the chicken and fry until tender..
  3. Add the ketchup, spices. Mix well. Add water to bring sauce to required consistency..
  4. Add all cut up vegetables in the sauce. Fry on high flame so vegetables do not wilt..
  5. Turn down flame and let sauce simmer..
  6. Turn off heat. Dish out. Serve with rice..
