Fried rice instructions

  1. Chop all vegetables to desired size. I chop mine small to medium..
  2. Mix chopped vegetables together in a bowl with d exception of the onion and garlic.
  3. Heat pot to medium heat and add olive oil..
  4. Add chicken breast and stir occasionally let it fry a bit.
  5. Add onion and garlic and stir chicken until onion is nicely sauted.
  6. Stir in a portion of the mixed vegetables into pot..
  7. Add a moderate portion of rice and stir together with 2-3 dashes of soy sauce..
  8. Repeat aforementioned step(7). Until all d vegetables and rice have been added..
