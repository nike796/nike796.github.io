Lemongrass Chicken Breast step by step

  1. Start by marinating the chicken. In large bowl, mix the lime zest and juice, lemongrass, garlic, ginger, chili and fish sauce into a food processor. Blend until smooth paste..
  2. Rub the paste all over the chicken breast. Cling wrap and leave it in the refrigerator for 1-2 hours (I usually marinate it overnight).
  3. To start cooking. Heat up olive oil in a skillet or pan over medium heat..
  4. Add marinated chicken breast into hot pan. Season with salt and lime juice..
  5. Cook for 3-5 minutes on each sides until golden and cooked through. (depending on the thickness of your chicken).
  6. Once ready, remove and let it rest on a chopping board for 2 minutes. Serve with steamed white rice or you may also toss the lemongrass chicken with leafy salad and sprinkle with roasted walnuts or peanuts..
