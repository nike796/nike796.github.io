Classic Lemonade instructions

  1. Start by making the simple syrup. Place the 2 cups sugar in a medium saucepan with the 2 cups water. Set the heat to medium and stir fairly often until the sugar is completely dissolved. Then set the pan aside for it to cool..
  2. In a large pitcher, stir together the simple syrup, lemon juice and additional cold water..
  3. Let it chill in the fridge prior to serving or just serve immediately over ice..
  4. That's it. Simple and delicious!.
