Simple macaroni pasta recipe step by step

  1. Now firstly, boil the water and add 1 teb oil in it now add macaroni pasta and boil it. After boil soak it with cold water..
  2. Now, heat the 3 teb oil in the pan..
  3. Now add cumin & mustard seeds and curry leaves..
  4. Now add the onion, garlic and capsicum in it and stir. Do not golden brown the oil..
  5. Now add tomato puree in it and stir..
  6. Now add all spices and stir..
  7. After one minute add soya and tomato sauce in it..
  8. And also add macaroni pasta and mix stir it..
  9. Now garnish it with corriender and serve it in plate..
