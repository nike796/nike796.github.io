Garlic Butter Fried Chicken Drumsticks instructions

  1. De-bone chicken drumsticks and thighs and cut the meat into 2 cm pieces.
  2. Mince the garlic and shallots. Rinse and finely chop the coriander and dill..
  3. Season chicken with fish sauce, black pepper, MSG, shallots, garlic, dill, coriander in about 15-20 minutes..
  4. Heat up the skillet, apply butter all over until fully melted..
  5. When butter boils, drop chicken in to fry..
  6. Fry until golden brown and a little burnt on both sides..
  7. When chicken is thoroughly cooked, evenly golden brown, let butter drain off, transfer to a serving plate..
  8. This dish is best served with egg fried rice..
