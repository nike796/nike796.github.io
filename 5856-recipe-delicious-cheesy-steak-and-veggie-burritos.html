Cheesy, steak and veggie burritos instructions

  1. Slice steak into thin strips..
  2. In large skillet cook steak for a minute on med- then add mixed vegs with steak.
  3. put a large amount of cooking oil into a med-large pot and turn on medium-low..
  4. meanwhile let steak and vegs cook until vegs are soft..
  5. then put a good amount into a tortillas and roll up and pin with a toothpick, and drop into hot oil for 1-2 mins or until lightly browned. Repeat until ingredients are gone..
  6. mix cheese and rotel together and microwave until all cheese is melted. pour on top of burritos..
  7. I use onion powder, garlic salt, salt and pepper, and lawrys seasoned salt to season my steak! but don't have too..
  8. you may also use a deep fryer..
