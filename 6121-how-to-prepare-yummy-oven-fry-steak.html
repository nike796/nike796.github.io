Oven fry steak step by step

  1. Marinated the beef with steak seasoning, soy sauce, salt, pepper , onion and garlic powder. Give it little massage to get all flavor get through the steak.
  2. In the heating pan add olive oil put the beef and fry each side about 5 minutes not to cooked but to brown..
  3. Preheated the oven at 190 c ..
  4. Remove steak from heat and bake in oven for 10 to 15 minutes.
  5. Remove the steak from the pan and used the same pan to make gravy ,by frying butter and onion till brown add beef broth let's boil and stir in flour.
  6. Slices the steak and serve with gravy.
