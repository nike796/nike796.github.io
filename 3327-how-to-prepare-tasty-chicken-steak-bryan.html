Ingredients of Chicken (Steak) Bryan

Chicken (Steak) Bryan step by step

  1. Saute garlic & onion in 2 Tbsp butter over medium heat till tender..
  2. Stir in wine & lemon juice, raise heat to medium high & simmer to reduce by half..
  3. Reduce heat to low, add butter slices one at a time..
  4. Stir in tomatoes, basil, salt & white pepper, remove from heat and set aside..
  5. Brush chicken with olive oil & season with salt, black pepper. Grill 15-20 minutes till cooked through. (Season and grill steaks to taste.).
  6. Place equal amount of cheese on each breast (steak). Spoon sauce over chicken (steak). Serve..
