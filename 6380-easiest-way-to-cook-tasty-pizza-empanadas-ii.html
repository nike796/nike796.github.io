pizza empanadas II step by step

  1. slice the Spainish sausage fry in a little olive oil onion mushrooms and Spanish sausage add pepperoni set in bowl.
  2. add to a pan Italian sausage, sirloin , bacon, cook till done I a pot mix ingredients of sauce.
  3. after sauce has simmered 7 minutes add all the meats simmer 3 minute let sit.
  4. get a empanada wrapper some are oval if it is put it long ways up and down.
  5. about 1/2 way down add cheese 2 tablespoons add meat and sauce 1/2 down add cheese the meat sauce mixture moisten bottom edge of sheet with a little bit of water.
  6. crimp with fork all along edge deep fry till done 2-3 minutes.
