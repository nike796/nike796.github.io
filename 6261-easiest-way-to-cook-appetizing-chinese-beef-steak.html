Ingredients of Chinese Beef Steak

  1. You need 1/2 kg of beef.
  2. It's 1/4 cup of oil.
  3. Prepare 5 clove of garlic.
  4. You need 1 pinch of salt.
  5. Prepare 2 tsp of msg / magic sarap.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of ketchup.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of brown sugar.
  8. It's 1 tbsp of paprika / chili powder.
  9. You need 2 medium of onion.

Chinese Beef Steak step by step

  1. slice beef thinly.
  2. heat oil in pan and fry garlic.
  3. sear beef until almost fried in texture but do not overcook.
  4. add salt and msg.
  5. mix in the ketchup and sugar, lower down heat.
  6. while simmering add in the chili powder.
  7. when the sauce is almost dry add the onion rings and slightly cook.
  8. take off from heat and serve.
