Ingredients of Arroz Chaufa (Peruvian Chicken Fried Rice)

  1. You need 5 cups of cooked rice.
  2. You need 350 grams of Brest chicken(cut in cubes).
  3. Prepare 3 of eggs (mixing together white and yolks).
  4. You need 1 cup of chopped green onions.
  5. It's 200 grams of chopped and fried bacon.
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp of dark soy sauce.
  7. You need 2 tbsp of light soy sauce.
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp of oyster sauce.
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp of chop fresh garlic.
  10. You need 1/2 tsp of fresh ground ginger.
  11. You need 1 of and 1/2 tbsp rosted sesame oil.
  12. Prepare 1 pinch of white ground peper.
