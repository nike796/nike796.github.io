Ingredients of Sweet spicy tomatoes sauce chicken wings

  1. Prepare 1 kg of chicken wings.
  2. Prepare 330 ml of tomatoes puré.
  3. You need 1 dl of white vinegar.
  4. It's 0.5 dl of brown sugar.
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon of salt.
  6. It's 200 gm of sweet corn.
  7. It's 2 of whole yellow onions (thin slices).
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon of sambal chili.
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon of tapioca powder (mixture with water).
  10. Prepare 500 ml of water.
  11. It's 2 tablespoon of cooking oil.
  12. It's of Optional:-.
  13. Prepare of Bonito flakes.

Sweet spicy tomatoes sauce chicken wings instructions

  1. Heat up the frying pan and pour in the cooking oil. Continue to add in yellow onions slices then the sambal chili and the tomatoes puré..
  2. Stir well around 5 to 8 min. Then add in the chicken wings. Cook it up around 10min in medium heat..
  3. After that add in the rest of the ingredients, continue stir around. And except tapioca and water..
  4. After 10min add in the water then the tapioca mixture. Turn the heat into high level. Let it boil until the texture getting thicker, then is ready to served..
  5. Topping with bonito flakes..
