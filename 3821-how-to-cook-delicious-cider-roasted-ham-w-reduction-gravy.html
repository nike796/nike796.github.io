Cider Roasted Ham w/ Reduction Gravy instructions

  1. Pour apple cider in a large roasting pan atop onions. Place ham atop. Place cinnamon stick to the side of the ham in the liquid..
  2. Cover. Cook accordingly to package directions. Uncover and glaze during last 5 minutes..
  3. Strain and reaerve braising liquid. Discard onions and cinnamon stick..
  4. Bring liquid to a boil in a small sauce pot..
