Fusion Fried Chicken !!! step by step

  1. Marinate chicken pieces for an hour or 2 by adding salt ,pepper powder,ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder.
  2. Take the marinated chicken in a sauce pan and let it cook in its own juices for 5 to 10 mins on slow flame till all the juices dry up. Can add a little water (2 to 3 tblsp) if required so the chicken is boiled and cooked..
  3. In a separate bowl take beaten egg, add salt for taste and little pepper powder.
  4. In another bowl, take 1/2 cup breadcrumbs, 1/2 cup crushed cornflakes and 1/2 cup grated cheese ,toss it together gently ..
  5. Dip the cooked chicken pieces in egg and then roll in the dry mix of cheese breadcrumbs cornflakes.
  6. Heat oil in a frying pan and deep fry the chicken pieces till they are light golden brown in color.
