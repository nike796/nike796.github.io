Steak-n-Cheese Tacos (Flautas) step by step

  1. Heat skillet over med-high heat and add oil. Add meat when skillet is hot..
  2. Add all seasonings and Cook until browned..
  3. Remove meat from skillet to a large bowl and add cream cheese. Mix thoroughly..
  4. Hear oil over med high heat. Use toothpicks to secure flautas. I always toothpick 2 flautas together to keep them together while frying..
  5. Fry until browned. Serve with whatever you like! Rice, salad or just on their own!.
