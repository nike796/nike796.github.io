Skinny oven fried chicken step by step

  1. Place the chicken in a bowl with the yoghurt and mix well..
  2. Cover the mixture and place in the fridge for a few hours (overnight is best)..
  3. Place all the dry ingredients in a food processor and pulse till well combined..
  4. Heat the oven to 220°C (fan assisted)..
  5. Place the breadcrumb mixture in a plate and cover each chicken fillet well by pressing it down firmly into the mixture..
  6. Place the breadcrumb chicken onto a lightly oiled baking tray and bake for about 30 minutes turning half way so to crisp on both sides..
  7. The fillets can be refrigerated for up to 3 days or frozen for up to 2 months. Heat for 10-15 minutes at 180°C before serving..
  8. A great accompaniment is sweet chilli sauce and a salad..
