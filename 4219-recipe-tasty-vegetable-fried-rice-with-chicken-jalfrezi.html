Vegetable fried rice with chicken jalfrezi step by step

  1. For rice: in a wok add oil, fry vegetables..
  2. Add salt, knorr cube, white pepper and black pepper. Add in soyasauce. Mix well. Add boiled rice and mix well. Give dam for 5 minutes. Vegetable fried rice is ready to serve..
  3. For chicken jalfrezi: marinate chicken with above all the ingredients except oil for 30 minutes. In a wok add oil. Fry marinated chicken well. Cook till chicken tender and oil starts to float. Add capsicum and onion..
  4. Cook until it gets soft. Then add green chillies and make it on dam. Garnish with coriander leaves..
