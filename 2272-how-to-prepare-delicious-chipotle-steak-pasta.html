Ingredients of Chipotle steak pasta

  8. You need 1 lb of skirt steak cut into strips.
  9. Prepare 1 dash of oil for frying steak and veggies.
  10. You need 1 dash of garlic powder.

Chipotle steak pasta step by step

  1. cook pasta according to directions on package.
  2. cook your sliced steak and veggies with the dash of oil.
  3. in blender add cream cheese, garlic powder, chicken bullion, milk and 3 chipotle chilis ( you may add more less according to your liking).
  4. blend until smooth.
  5. add steak and veggies to the cooked and drained pasta.
  6. pour chipotle mixture over pasta and fold in with tongs.
  7. serve and enjoy :).
