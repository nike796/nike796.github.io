Chicken Pasta Recipe instructions

  1. First Boil Macaroni add 1.5 litre Water in a pot add 1 Tbsp Oil and bring it to boil then add 200 grams Macaroni and boil until its done drain water and pour cold water on macaroni and add 1 Tbsp Oil mix well to avoid stickiness..
  2. Heat oil in a pan add chopped garlic fry until light brown add boneless chicken and fry until color change add chopped green chillies add chicken powder,black pepper powder,salt,chilli sauce,soya sauce,vinegar mix well and add 1/4 cup water and cook for 5 minutes..
  3. Add carrots,capsicum,corn mix well and cook for 2 minutes on low flame until vegetables becomes soft. Add cabbage,spring onion Don't overcook vegetables add tomato ketchup add boiled macaroni mix well. Chicken Pasta is ready to serve..
