Pork Chops in Gravy instructions

  1. Preheat oven 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Season both sides of chops.
  3. Put into a pan and cook each side 4 minutes if thick add 1 minute per side.
  4. Mix the soup and rest of water.
  5. When done with all the chops add a layer to a baking pan and 1 cup of water.
  6. Then add the soup.
  7. Add another layer of chops.
  8. Then add the soup.
  9. Add another layer of chops.
  10. And soup till finished with the soup on top.
  11. Put into oven and cook for 1 hour..
  12. Let rest for 15 minutes and serve I hope you enjoy!!!!.
