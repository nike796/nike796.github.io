Ingredients of Steakburgers

  1. Prepare 1-1/3 pound of ground sirloin 85/15.
  2. You need 1/4 cup of steak sauce I used HP Sauce.
  3. Prepare To taste of Pink Himalayan Salt.
  4. You need 1 teaspoon of granulated garlic powder.
  5. It's To taste of ground black pepper.
  6. It's 1 teaspoon of onion powder.
  7. You need 4 tablespoon of diced onions.
  8. You need 4 slices of butter pats.
  9. It's 4 slices of sharp cheddar cheese off the block.
  10. It's 1-1/4 cup of water.
  11. Prepare As needed of your favorite condiments.

Steakburgers step by step

  1. Mix all the ingredients.
  2. After it is mixed form into 4 patties..
  3. Fry in a pan till done turn as needed..
  4. Add onions on top with a pat of butter on each patty..
  5. Add the sharp cheddar cheese cut from the block..
  6. Add water and lid to steam-melt the cheddar..
  7. Let rest for 3-5 minutes..
  8. Serve I hope you enjoy!!.
