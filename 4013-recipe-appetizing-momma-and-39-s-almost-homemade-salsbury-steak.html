Ingredients of Momma's Almost Homemade Salsbury Steak

  1. You need 2 1/2 lb of beef cube steak.
  2. You need 1 box of beef broth (4 cups).
  3. It's 1 envelope of onion soup mix.
  4. You need 4 envelope of mushroom gravy mix.
  5. It's 1 of salt (to taste).
  6. It's 1 of pepper (to taste).
  7. You need 2 can of 6.5oz mushroom pieces.
  8. Prepare 1/3 cup of water.

Momma's Almost Homemade Salsbury Steak step by step

  1. Salt and pepper all pieces of cube steak both sides.
  2. Sear both sides meat in hot pan.
  3. Add to pot with broth, onion soup mix, water and gravy mix. Mix well..
  4. Add mushroom pieces, mix well..
  5. Cover on med high heat simmer for 30 minutes or until meat is tender..
  6. Serve over rice or with mashed potatoes..
