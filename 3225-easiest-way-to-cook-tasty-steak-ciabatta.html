Steak ciabatta step by step

  1. Put oven on at gas mark 7 and start cooking curly fries n baking tray.
  2. Chop up gherkins and peppers and open rocket packet and leave to one side.
  3. After 15 mins get steak ready and fry on griddle pan until cooked to your preference.
  4. When fries have been in add ciabatta on lower oven level and warm through (about 5 minutes).
  5. When all cooked take out ciabatta and cut in half.
  6. Slice two sections open and spread on mustard.
  7. Slice steak and add to bread.
  8. Add gherkin and peppers and rocket and top with extra virgin olive oil.
  9. Close sandwich.
  10. Put curly fries in a bowl and serve.
