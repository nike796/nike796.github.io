Ingredients of Pork Chop With Onion Black Pepper Sauce

  1. Prepare 2 of Pork Chop.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of ready to used black pepper sauce.
  3. You need 100 ml of water.
  4. You need 1 of large Onion.
  5. You need of Salt and black pepper.

To make the peppers, heat a little olive oil in large frying pan and add the onion and peppers. The yummy delicious spicy bold black pepper sauce! Saute until the onions are soft and translucent, then add your black pepper sauce. Add pork chops to skillet and sprinkle with salt and pepper.

Pork Chop With Onion Black Pepper Sauce instructions

  1. Seaoson the pork Chop With salt and black pepper then with oil pan fry it for 4 to 5 minutes on both side.
  2. Use a knife poke thru both side and pan fry for another 1 to 2 minutes both side (or longer depending on the thickness of the pork Chop) until you don't see anymore pink juice.
  3. Remove and set aside to rest.
  4. With the balance of the oil about 4 to 5 tbsp stir fry the onion then add in the black pepper sauce and water and let it simmer for 2 minutes till sauce Are slightly thickenedr.
  5. Serve the pork Chop With Onion Black Pepper Sauce and enjoy.
