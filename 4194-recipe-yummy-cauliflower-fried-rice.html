Ingredients of Cauliflower Fried Rice

  1. You need 1 of brown onion.
  2. Prepare To taste of minced garlic.
  3. Prepare 2 cups of frozen vegetables of choice.
  4. Prepare 1-2 of capsicum (red and/or green).
  5. Prepare 200 g of mushrooms (approx a punnet).
  6. Prepare 1 head of broccoli.
  7. You need 500 g of chicken tenderloins.
  8. It's 500 g of riced cauliflower.
  9. It's 200 g of streaky bacon.
  10. Prepare To taste of Tamari sauce.
  11. Prepare 2 of eggs, scrambled.

Cauliflower Fried Rice step by step

  1. In an electric fry pan cook chicken and bacon.
  2. Remove chicken and bacon, scramble eggs, remove and then keep to the side covered.
  3. Cook onion, broccoli mushrooms and capsicum.
  4. Add cauliflower rice and frozen vegetables.
  5. Once all vegetables are cooked add chicken and bacon back, stir.
  6. Add minced garlic and tamari sauce to taste.
