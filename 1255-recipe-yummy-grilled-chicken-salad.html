Grilled chicken salad step by step

  1. Fire up the grill.
  2. Wash and cut apples in half. Remove core and stem. Coat with lemon juice and butter..
  3. Put on the grill cut side down.
  4. Turn as needed when done put back in butter lemon mixture cut side down.
  5. Add salt to chicken. Put on the grill.
  6. Turn often.
  7. Cook till done add butter over top.
  8. Slice the chicken into bite sized pieces..
  9. Carefully dice the apples. Slice the tomatoes in half..
  10. Mix all the ingredients except the cheeses.
  11. Add cheeses on top of salad serve with your favorite salad dressing..
  12. Hope you enjoy!.
