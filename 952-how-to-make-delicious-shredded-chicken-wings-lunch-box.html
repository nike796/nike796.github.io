How to Make Delicious Shredded Chicken wings Lunch box

Delicious, fresh and tasty.

Shredded Chicken wings Lunch box.

You can have Shredded Chicken wings Lunch box using 6 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Shredded Chicken wings Lunch box

  1. You need 1 cup of Rice.
  2. You need of Shredded 2 chicken wings.
  3. It's 2 Tbs of Teriyaki sauce.
  4. You need 1 of Scallion.
  5. You need of Avocado.
  6. It's of Cherry tomatoes.

Shredded Chicken wings Lunch box instructions

  1. Shred roasted chicken wings.
  2. Reheat chicken in a small pan.
  3. Add sauce and half of sliced scallion, cook together.
  4. Bring your favorite bento box, Put rice, chicken in and sprinkle half of scallions on the top..
  5. Put sliced avocado and tomatoes or any other vegetables.
  6. Enjoy!.
