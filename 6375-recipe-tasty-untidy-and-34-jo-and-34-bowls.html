Ingredients of Untidy "Jo" bowls

  1. Prepare of Untidy Jo.
  2. Prepare 1 1/4 lb of Ground sirloin.
  3. Prepare 1 can of 15 oz. Sloppy Joe mix.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of worcestershire sauce.
  5. Prepare of Bowls.
  6. It's 3 can of Pillsbury Grand Biscuit. I used 5 count cans for 15 bowls. There was plenty of untidy mix left to do 1 or 2 more biscuit cans..
  7. It's 1/2 packages of Grated cheddar, your preference, (mild/sharp)..

Untidy "Jo" bowls step by step

  1. In a fry pan, cook the ground sirloin with the worcestershire sauce until meat is no longer pink. Drain..
  2. Add the untidy "Jo" mix, following instructions on can to complete cooking of filler..
  3. Take each biscuit and flatten by hand or roller until approximately 4+ inches round. Using the bottom of a glass press the center of biscuit creating a well..
  4. Fill each biscuit with roughly 1 to 2 tbls of the untidy Jo mix..
  5. Once all biscuits are filled with untidy "Jo" mix, sprinkle with cheddar cheese. Add the amount of cheese based on how cheesy you like it..
  6. Normally oven is preheated to 350. But follow directions on the biscuit can. I allowed the biscuits to remain in the oven for the highest minutes suggested..
