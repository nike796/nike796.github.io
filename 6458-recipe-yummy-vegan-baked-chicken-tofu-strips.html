Ingredients of Vegan Baked ‘Chicken’ [Tofu] Strips

  1. It's 2 pcs of firm tofu.
  2. Prepare 6 tbsp of all-purpose flour.
  3. It's 6 tbsp of unsweetened plant-based milk (I use soy milk).
  4. You need 3 tbsp of water.
  5. It's 1 tsp of garlic powder.
  6. You need 1 tsp of paprika powder.
  7. Prepare 1 cup of breadcrumbs (I use Panko).
  8. You need 1/2 cup of red hot wings sauce (or you can use BBQ sauce).
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  10. Prepare 1 pinch of salt.

Vegan Baked ‘Chicken’ [Tofu] Strips step by step

  1. Preheat the oven to 160ºC..
  2. Drain and cut tofu into strips..
  3. In large bowl, combine all ingredients. Mix well..
  4. Coat tofu sticks into batter. Roll them in the breadcrumbs..
  5. Line baking sheets with parchment paper. Arrange tofu strips on the baking sheets (Don’t stack)..
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown. Serve hot..
