Crispy fried chicken step by step

  1. Lightly chop your onions and scotch bonnet.
  2. In a pot add in all your spices including your onion and scotch bonnet.
  3. Stir gently and gradually add in your chicken. Add a few drops of water.
  4. Cover and allow to simmer.
  5. Drain the chicken and keep aside.
  6. In a hot fry pan add vegetable oil and leave it in medium heat.
  7. Then gradually fry your chicken batch by batch..
  8. Drain in a paper towel or colander and serve.
