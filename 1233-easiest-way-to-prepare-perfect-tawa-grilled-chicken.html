Tawa Grilled chicken step by step

  1. In a bowl add garlic paste, lemon juice, salt, olive oil, black pepper powder, oregano and mix it properly..
  2. Rub this mixture all over the chicken fillet and leave it for 1 hour..
  3. Place this in a nonstick grill tawa and cook in a medium to low flame for about 5 min..
  4. Brush some oil if needed..
