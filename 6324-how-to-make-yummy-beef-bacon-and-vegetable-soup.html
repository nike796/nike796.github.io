Ingredients of Beef, Bacon, and Vegetable Soup

  1. Prepare 2 cups of chopped cabbage.
  2. It's 1 of small white onion.
  3. Prepare 1 of small red onion.
  4. It's 32 ounces of meat spaghetti sauce I used Mids brand.
  5. It's 1 of large carrot.
  6. You need 1 cup of sliced celery.
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of white ground pepper.
  8. Prepare To taste of salt.
  9. You need 1 teaspoon of granulated garlic powder.
  10. You need 1/4 pound of smoked bacon.
  11. Prepare 1 pound of ground sirloin.
  12. Prepare 1/2 pound of frozen French fries.
  13. You need 1/2 teaspoon of fennel seed.
  14. It's 1 pint of water.

Beef, Bacon, and Vegetable Soup step by step

  1. Wash and chop the vegetables..
  2. Slice the bacon and cook render the fat. Add the pepper. Stir for a minute..
  3. Add the fennel seeds, French fries, beef, and vegetables, stir coating them all. Add salt, cover and simmer till tender stirring as needed..
  4. When the vegetables are tender add the garlic give a good stir. Add the sauce. Add the water. Simmer 15 minutes..
  5. Serve I hope you enjoy!.
