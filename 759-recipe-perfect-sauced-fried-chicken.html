Sauced fried chicken step by step

  1. Mix the ingredients of the marinade and marinate overnight.
  2. Mix all ingredients of flour except the egg for the flour mix.
  3. Post marination remove the excess marinate into a bowl and add the eggs into the mix and stir well.
  4. Heat oil in a large pan for deep frying..
  5. To coat the chicken. First mix it into the flour then dip it in the egg wash and then again into the flour..
  6. Add the coated chicken into the oil and fry till golden brown.
  7. Sauce-.
  8. Grind the whole chillies and ginger garlic paste to make a fine paste.
  9. Take the tomatoes and grind it to the paste.
  10. Separate 3 tbsp of used oil into a different pan and add the chilli paste and fry till it changes to a dark red colour.
  11. Add the to fried paste the tomato puree and mix well.
  12. Once the puree is cooked add 2 tbsp chilli sauce 1 tbsp vinegar.
  13. Add salt and aginomoto to the mix.
  14. When the paste dries add 3 tbsp of the flour mix along with 2 cups of water and stir well till cooked.
  15. Once the paste thickens add the fried chicken to the paste or separate it in a bowl and serve seperately.
