Ingredients of Burger and Rice Simple

  1. It's 1-1/3 pound of beef.
  2. It's 2 cups of rice.
  3. You need 2 cups of water.
  4. It's 1 quart of beef broth.
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon of granulated onion powder.
  6. It's 1-1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon of ground white pepper.

Burger and Rice Simple instructions

  1. Brown the beef with the pepper, and half the salt. When browned add rice, onion powder, and allow to cook 5 minutes stirring often..
  2. Add half the broth and allow to start bubbling. Then add the rest of the broth. Add rest of the salt. Bring to a simmer add the water as needed till rice is where you like it cooked, the way you eat it..
  3. Allow to rest covered for 5 minutes. Serve I hope you enjoy!!!!!.
