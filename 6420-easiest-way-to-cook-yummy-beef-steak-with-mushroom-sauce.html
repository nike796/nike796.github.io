Beef Steak 🥩 with Mushroom Sauce🍄🥣 instructions

  1. Marinate steak in all spices and refrigerate for 2 hours..
  2. Cook on your grill pan in half and half olive oil and butter along with whole gatlic cloves..
  3. Give 3/4 minutes each side. Or cook to your desired choice..
  4. After your steak is done, set aside and cover it for about 10 minutes..
  5. In same pan add oil if needed than add onions and seasoned with black pepper and salt fry for half minute add mushrooms, butter, chopped garlic, worcestershire sauce and few drops of vinegar than add cream cook for a minute you can add milk for desired consistency..
  6. Add your steak in sauce..
  7. Serve with sautéed vegetables..
  8. Enjoy 🍴😋.
