Chicken Biryani Recipe step by step

  1. Pot mein oil aur pyaz dal kar golden brown fry kar lein,fried pyaz ki adhe quantity bahir nikal kar side per rakh dein..
  2. Baqi fried pyaz mein adrak lehsan paste dal kar ache tarhan mix kar lein..
  3. Chicken shamil karein aur chicken ka rang tabdeel hunay tak fry lein..
  4. Sabut kali mirch,baadiyan ka phool,darchini,laung,badi elaichi,hari elaichi,zeera,lal micrh powder, haldee powder,garam masala powder,zeera powder,dhania powder,namak,tez paat aur dahi dal kar ache tarhan mix karein aur 4-5 minutes kliya paka lein..
  5. Tamatar dal kar ache tarhan mix kar lein..
  6. Hari mirchein,aloo bukhara,hara dhania aur podina dal kar ache tarhan mix karein..
  7. Dhak kar 6-8 minutes kliya paka lein..
  8. For Dum: Cooked chicken & gravy ki adhe quantity ko bahir nikla kar rakh dein,lemon juice,cooked chawal,boiled aloo, remaining cooked chicken,fried pyaz,lemon juice,baqi bachay hoye cooked chawal,pani,zarda ka rang, Shimla Mirch aur oil dal dein,dhak kar 10 minutes kliya steam cook kar lein..
  9. Mazedar Sindhi Biaryani tayar hai isy podiny ke chutni ke sath serve kry aur Enjoy kry..
