Bacon fried steak step by step

  2. Mix flour,italian bread crumbs, pepper, and crumbled bacon in a bowl..
  3. Beat eggs in seperate bowl..
  4. Heat skillet to medium high..
  5. Take cube steak and dip into egg mixture. Then dip into flour mixture. Place in skillet..
  6. Fry steaks 7 minutes on each side..
