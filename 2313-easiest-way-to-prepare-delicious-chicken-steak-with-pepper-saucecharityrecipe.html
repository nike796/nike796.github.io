Chicken steak with Pepper Sauce#charityrecipe step by step

  1. Take the chicken & flatten it with a rolling pin.
  2. Season it with salt,pepper,paprika.
  3. Take 3 deep dishes. Put Egg beaten in one, flour in one, & bread crumbs(season with garlic powder) in the other.
  4. Deep the chicken in egg then flour,the bread crumbs.
  5. Continue with the rest.
  6. Cover & leave in the fridge for an hour.
  7. After an hour, take them out. Leave them out for 20 mins.
  8. Take a non stick pan, add abit oil & pan fry the chicken. Each side 7-8 mins.
  9. Once done put in a plate cover with foil.continue with the rest.
  10. Now make the pepper sauce. Take a pan add butter once done add the flour,cook it till light brown. Add milk,salt,pepper,soy sauce,paprika. Mix well. Simmer for 5 mins.
  11. Plate the chicken then pour the sauce on top. Serve with roast potatoes.
