Tawa steak burger and sandwich step by step

  2. Taway kay neachay hulki aag jala lain. Thora oil daal dain..
  3. Ab hari mirch, hara dhuniya, lahsan, salt, pepper aur vinegar ko blend kar kay aik paste bana lain. Aur chicken kay slices pay coat kar dain..
  4. Ab in slices ko garam oil pay rukh dain. Carefully rukhna hay kyun kay oil garam hota hay to stake rukhnay say taizi say oil kay cheetay app per par suktay hain..
  5. Aik side puknay tuk hilana nae. Puk jaey to ulta dain..
  6. Ab dusri sidr bhi paka lain. Achi tra dba kar pakani hay..
  7. Ab slice aur bun pay mayonnaise laga lain aur stake rukh dain, uper lettuce leave laga dain. Aur close kar dain. Dil chahay to cheese add krain. Zruri nae..
  8. Serve kar dain..
