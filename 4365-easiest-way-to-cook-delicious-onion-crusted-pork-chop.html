Onion Crusted Pork Chop instructions

  1. Set up 3 step frying station. Flour, egg wash, then fried onions. Crushed the fried onions with your hands, then add seasoning and mix to combine. Briefly whisk milk into eggs until smooth to make your egg wash..
  2. Use your left hand to dredge each pork chop in flour and shake off excess. Use your right hand as your 'wet hand' and dip the chop in the egg wash. Drop into third mixing bowl full of crushed, fried onions and use your left hand to press onion mixture firmly around every surface of chop. Place onto baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
