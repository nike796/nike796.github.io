Crispy chicken strips (my recipe) step by step

  1. preheat deep fryer or pan with oil.
  2. slice the chicken breast into strips.
  3. put the flour and all purpose seasoning into a big enough container to put strips of chicken in it ..mix.
  4. than in a separate container put the egg and milk and mix.. (might vary on how many eggs depending on how big your chicken breast are).
  5. put the strips of chicken into the flour coat well.
  6. than put the strips in the egg wash and coat well.
  7. put back into the flour again and coat well once again.
  8. deep fry the chicken strips for about 3 to for 4 mins.. once the chicken becomes golden brown!.
  9. Enjoy :) ...
