Hot chicken wings step by step

  1. Clean your chicken and pat dry. Marinated with all marinating ingredients. Stir it well. And keep it up to 1 hour or less..
  2. Cooked marinated chicken with half cup of water and stir it well. And leaved it in medium high heat until it caramelized and reduce the sauce.
  3. Preheat the oven 360 F in 15 minutes before roasting and roasting it for another 30-40 minutes. And place chicken on your baking tray. Do not pile up each other..
  4. When it's done sprinkle with sesame seeds and enjoy it!.
