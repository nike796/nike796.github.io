Chicken Chapli Kabab Recipe step by step

  1. Squeeze the minced chicken with your hand to drain the excess water..
  2. Mix all of the above ingredients except cooking oil in a bowl. Mix well with the help of your hand. Cover and keep it aside for an hour..
  3. Divide the chicken mixture into small equal portions. Make ball like shapes and flatten the ball by pressing firmly with your palms. Before that put oil in your hand to prevent the mixture sticking to your hand. Now place the tomato slices on it and press slightly press it..
  4. Heat 1-2 tablespoon oil in a nonstick frying pan. Place them one by one carefully. Fry 3-4 pieces at a time. Fry them till both sides turn slightly golden brown. Remember not to over-fry otherwise, it will lose moisture and becomes hard..
  5. Chicken Chapli Kabab is ready to serve..
