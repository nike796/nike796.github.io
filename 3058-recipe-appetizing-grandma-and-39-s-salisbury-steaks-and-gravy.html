Grandma's Salisbury Steaks and Gravy instructions

  1. EASY!!!! Put ground beef in large mixing bowl. add minced onion, tomato juice, salt and pepper, egg and garlic powder. add dried onion soup and mix in also..
  2. Mix ingredients by hand. Pat out like burgers and fry in skillet where they become firm and browned on both sides. NOT DONE!!!!.
  3. Take burgers out. use a spoon to get any burned peices out of the oil. in skillet make pan gravy with flour. it should be kinda watery. can use either broth or water..
  4. Put burgers back in the gravy and turn on simmer. gravy should thicken up, cook about 20-25 min on low. add salt, pepper, or garlic to taste..
