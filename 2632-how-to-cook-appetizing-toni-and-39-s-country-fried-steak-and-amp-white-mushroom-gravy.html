  10. You need 1 1/4 tsp of paprika.

  12. It's 1/3 cup of flour.

Toni's Country Fried Steak & White Mushroom Gravy step by step

  1. assemble a line of plates 1· Flour with a pinch of black pepper sifted throughout 2· 2 large beaten eggs with a pinch of pepper & a pinch of salt 3· Flour with lots of Pepper, Garlic powder, Onion powder, Cayenne & Paprika sifted throughout chop mushrooms set aside.
  2. heat a skillet with 2 tbsp of vegetable oil and 2 tbsp of unsalted butter.
  3. get 4 thin cut cubed steaks & season both sides with a pinch of salt & pepper, now go down the line, dip into flour, egg, then flour with all seasonings & into frying pan cook on both side for about 4 minutes over medium high heat, then place on a brown paper bag or paper towels..
  4. then to the same pan add your chopped mushrooms to 2 more tbsp of unsalted butter cook till brown with a wooden spoon scrape the bottom of the pan and get up all the drippings and mix around with mushrooms, then add 4 tbsp of flour with a whisk & whisk until all the clumps are out & till smooth & the roux has a nice golden brown color, now add 1 & a 1/2 cups of whole milk and add lots of fresh cracked black pepper stir and pour into a gravy server..
  5. You can serve country fried steak with biscuits, mashed potatoes, & favorite veggies or serve for breakfast biscuits eggs sunny side up & your favorite hash. Enjoy and Good Simple Eats!!.
