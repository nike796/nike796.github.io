Mexican salsa steak instructions

  1. **for chicken marination method**.
  2. In a bowl combine all ingredients..
  3. Now add chicken and rub mixture on it..
  4. Marinate chicken for at least 5_6 hours..
  5. Then heat oil / butter in a pan and fry chicken until done..
  6. *for salsa sauce**.
  7. In a blander add all ingredients and little water and blend it well..
  8. Take out in a pan cook for 5-8 mins on medium flame..
  9. **for veggies***.
  10. In a pan heat oil/ butter add onions and saute for 2 mins then add all veggies,salt,soya sauce and black pepper and stir well..
  11. Now place chicken piece in serving plate pour sauce on it and in side add veggies..
