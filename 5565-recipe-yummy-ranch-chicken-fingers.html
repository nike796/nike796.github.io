Ingredients of Ranch chicken fingers

  2. You need 3 of eggs.

  7. You need 1 cup of herb seasoned bread crumbs.
  8. It's 1 of whatever seasonings you like depends on taste.
  9. Prepare 2 cup of vegetable oil.
  10. You need 1/2 cup of Ranch seasonings.
  11. You need 1/4 tsp of poultry seasonings.

Ranch chicken fingers instructions

  1. Heat up some oil.
  2. Slice chicken bout a 1/4 thick.
  3. M I x eggs n cream.
  4. Mix flour and bread crumbs and ranch seasonings.
  5. Dip chicken in flour mix then egg mix and back in flour mix n fry til done!.
