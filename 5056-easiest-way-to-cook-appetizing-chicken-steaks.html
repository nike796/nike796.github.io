  9. You need 2 tbs of chilli sauce.

Chicken steaks instructions

  1. In a bowl add salt, shan tikka masala, red chilli powder, turmic powder, paprika powder, coriander powder, soya sauce, chilli sauce, vinegar, ginger garlic paste and lemon juice mix it the add (thinely sliced chicken steaks) mix it and keep marinated it for 1 to 2 hour in fridge....
  2. Now take out steaks from fridge again mix it.. Heat pan pour oil in pan until pan suface is full....
  3. Now add steak one by one in pan and cook steaks 2, 2 mins each sides until beautiful golden colour comes now take oit steak from pan and and put it in serving plate.. Now our steaks is ready to eat.. Serve it with steak sauce and ketchup....
