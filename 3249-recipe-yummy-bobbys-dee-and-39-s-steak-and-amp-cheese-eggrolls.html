Bobbys Dee's Steak & Cheese Eggrolls step by step

  1. heat skillet add 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  2. add diced onions, green peppers, and red peppers.sweat out ingredients till vegetables become translucent.
  3. add shredded roast beef to skillet.
  4. cook combined ingredients in skillet till nice and hot add salt, pepper, cayene pepper, and paprika.
  5. in a large bowl beat eggs and milk together well (eggwash).
  6. in a medium sauce pan add oil and heat on med high.
  7. brush on eggwash on eggroll wrap lightly coated.
  8. and desired amount on eggroll then wrap.
  9. drop wrapped eggrolls in oil and fry till golden brown all around.
  10. place on paper towels to dry excess oil.
  11. let cool for 10 min and serve with favorite dipping sauce!.
