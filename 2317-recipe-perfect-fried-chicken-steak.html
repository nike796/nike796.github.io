Ingredients of Fried chicken steak

  1. Prepare 1/4 kg of chopped chicken steak.
  2. It's 2 of garlic cloves chopped.
  3. It's 2 of capsicums chopped.
  4. It's of Cooking oil.
  5. Prepare of Salt.
  6. It's of Pepper.

Fried chicken steak step by step

  1. To a pan add just enough oil and heat till it's warm enough.
  2. Add the chicken and sprinkle just enough salt and pepper.
  3. Stir for two minutes then add the garlic.
  4. Keep stirring for another two minutes then leave it to cook on low heat for about 10minutes.
  5. Add the capsicum and stir for a few minutes.
  6. Leave to cook for another 5minutes till the chicken turns golden brown.
  7. Serve with ugali or as desired.
