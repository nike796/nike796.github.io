Grilled chicken with tarragon sauce served with fried rice instructions

  1. Tarragon Sauce: In a pan,add butter and let it melt,add onion and saute till translucent..
  2. Add water, chicken cube Stock and mix it well..
  3. Now turn off the flame, add cream and mix well..
  4. Turn on the flame; add salt,black pepper,dried tarragon leaves and mix well,cook until desired thickness..
  5. In a bowl add chicken fillet, salt, garlic powder, mustard paste, black pepper, olive oil, tarragon leaves, soy sauce mix well and keep aside for 20-25 minutes..
  6. Grease grill pan with oil and place marinated chicken. Grill each side on medium heat for 5-6 minutes..
  7. Drizzle tarragon sauce over grilled chicken and serve..
  8. For saute vegetables: add 2 tbsp in a wok then add all vegetables and saute for 2-3 minutes..
  9. Fried rice: In a wok add oil then add vegetables and saute for 1 min then add all spices and cook for 2 mins,add boiled rice and mix well.Cover and cook at low flame for 5-8 mins..
