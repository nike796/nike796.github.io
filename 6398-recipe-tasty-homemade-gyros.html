Homemade Gyros instructions

  1. First off.. thank you to @bbqtvtv.com a.k.a tom for his greek seasoning recipe found on his profile in wich I used for this recipe..
  2. And secondly a big thank you to @skunkmonkey101 as I used his tzatziki sauce recipe found on his profile..
  3. Thirdly: thank you to @taylor68too for her added seasoning suggestions! Now let's get to cooking!.
  4. Remove bones from meat and rinse under cool water. Pat dry..
  5. Slice meat into thin 1/4 inch slices and each slice should be about 1-2 inches long..
  6. In a large bowl, mix all marinade/seasonings. Wisk well..
  7. Add sliced meat. And toss to coat well..
  8. Cover bowl and refridgerate for 1 hour to soak. Tossing every 15 minutes or so..
  9. In a large skillet, heat oil..
  10. Toss in meat mixture with all the marinade..
  11. Fry, tossing frequently until outsides of meat is seared and insides are still slightly pink. About 5-10 minutes..
  12. Drain meat from juices and set meat aside but keep warm. Reserve juices..
  13. In another skiller heat until hot..
  14. Lightly brush pitas with a bit of the reserved drippings/juices. This will give pitas a nice flavor..
  15. Cook pitas until nice n browned but not crispy. ( if u don't like your pitas cooked u can skip this process) spray pan with cooking spray as needed..
  16. TO SERVE:.
  17. Place desired amount of meat onto a pita..
  18. Then garnish with onion,tomato and tzatziki sauce..
  19. Serve hot and enjoy!.
