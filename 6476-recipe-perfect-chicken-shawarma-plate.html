Chicken Shawarma Plate step by step

  1. Marinate chicken for 20m with: yogurt, salt, red chili powder, whole spices gram masala, black pepper powder, tandoori masala, lemon juice.
  2. Put oil in pan and heat.
  3. Add cumin seeds.
  4. Add garlic and ginger to sautee (paste or chopped).
  5. Add coriander seeds.
  6. Add chili flakes.
  7. Add marinated chicken.
  8. Cover the pan for 10-15m (or until chicken is cooked).
