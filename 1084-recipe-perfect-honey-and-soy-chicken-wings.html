Ingredients of Honey and soy chicken wings

  1. Prepare 4 tbsp of dark soy sauce.
  2. Prepare 4 tbsp of honey.
  3. You need 4 tsp of curry powder.
  4. Prepare 2 of chillies.
  5. You need of Sesame seeds.
  6. It's of Salt, paprika.
  7. You need of Chicken wings.

Honey and soy chicken wings instructions

  1. Deseed the chillies and cut in little pieces. Add the honey, soy sauce, curry and sesame seeds to the chillies and mix well until smooth.
  2. Give half of it over the chicken wings until they all are glazed and let it sit in the fridge for at least 2 hours. When ready to cook sprinkle with paprika and salt.
  3. Then put it in the oven for 30 min 200 degrees or on the bbq. After half way through glaze with the rest of the mix. Enjoy <3.
