Cube Steak & Gravy Instant Pot IP step by step

  1. Mix dredge ingredients in whatever container you wish..
  2. Cut your cube steak how you like, I like bite sized pieces..
  3. I just toss it in the dredge until coated..
  4. Chop half an onion, I like this long, but do what you like..
  5. Set your IP to saute.
  6. Add 2-3 Tbl. Oil to the liner. Once hot, put in your floured meat and brown it..
  7. Once your meat is just browned, add your onions..
  8. Keep stirring until the onions just turn a little.
  9. I happened to buy this gravy, use what you like..
  10. Add your gravy and broth to the mix in the liner. Give it a stir..
  11. Set your IP to pressure cook for 15 minutes.
  12. Quick Release (QR) pressure when time is up.
