  8. Prepare 2-14 oz cans of beef broth.

Crockpot Roast Beef and Gravy step by step

  1. Add carrots and potatoes to crockpot.
  2. Add beef broth and cream of mushroom soup on top of veggies and mix together. I then add seasonings (salt pepper garlic).. And little of Worcestershire sauce and A1 sauce (few teaspoons of each) and the beef bouillon cubes and mix together.
  3. I then add the roast beef and one small diced onion.
  4. Cover with lid on high at least 4 and low about 8 hours.
  5. About half hour to hour before done I remove roast and shred going along grain of meat should tear easily on its on by now just helping it and add back to pot covered for remaining time.
  6. Great over rice, noodles, or mashed potatoes.
