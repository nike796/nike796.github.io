Pancake recipe instructions

  1. Combine the dry ingredients flour,salt,baking powder, baking soda.
  2. In another bowl combine the wet ingredients, butter milk,melted butter, egg and vanilla,whisk to combine.
  3. Then add the dry ingredients to the wet,whisk until well combine.
  4. Melt some butter in a non stick pan,add spoonful of the pancake batter,cover it until bubbles,flip the other side until golden brown.
  5. Served with strawberry syrup and enjoy.
