Beef Steak Filipino Style instructions

  1. Marinade Beef steak meat, 1 tbs salt, and the calamansi..
  2. Fry until the beef oil and the meat is brownish..
  3. Put in pressure cooker with garlic, onions, 1 & 1/2 Sprite, bell pepper and ginger including the soy sauce for 15 minutes..
  4. Put in stove or frying pan. Add cornstarch,stir til its sticky, add the white onions and the dish is done.
