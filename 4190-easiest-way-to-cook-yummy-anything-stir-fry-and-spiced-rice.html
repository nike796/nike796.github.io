Anything stir fry and spiced rice step by step

  1. Put rice, water and half the garlic paprika and sage on to boil cook approx. 15 min.
  2. Brown the beef in a skillet.
  3. Add half of the garlic paprika and sage.
  4. Add veggies, brown sugar and soy sauce.
  5. Slowly add remaining water into meat mixture let simmer till rice is done.
  6. Remove from heat 2 min then serve over spiced rice.
