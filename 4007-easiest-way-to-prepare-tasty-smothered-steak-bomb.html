Ingredients of Smothered steak bomb

  1. You need 1 lb of steak (precut into cubes).
  2. Prepare 1 of Vidalia onion.
  3. You need 1 of yellow bell pepper.
  4. You need 1/2 of onion soup mix package.
  5. You need 1 cup of baby bella mushrooms (pre sliced).
  6. It's 1 tbsp of chopped garlic.
  7. It's 4 tbsp of olive oil, extra virgin.
  8. Prepare 3 tbsp of worcestershire sauce.
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp of soy sauce.
  10. It's 1/2 can of cream of mushroom (condensed).

Smothered steak bomb step by step

  1. Chop the onion and pepper into desired size and shape.
  2. Put olive oil, pound of cubed steak, pepper, onion, mushrooms, onion soup mix and garlic into pan. Cook on medium high..
  3. After meat has browned on all sides, add the worcesteshire and soy sauce..
  4. After about 10 min add the condensed mushroom soup..
  5. Let boil for 5 to 10 minutes. Then reduce to simmer for 10-15 min..
  6. Serve over white rice..
