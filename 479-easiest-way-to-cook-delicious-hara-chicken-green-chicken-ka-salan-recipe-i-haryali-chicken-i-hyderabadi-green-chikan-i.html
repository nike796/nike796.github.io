Hara Chicken Green Chicken ka salan Recipe I Haryali Chicken I Hyderabadi Green Chikan I step by step

  1. Wash the chicken well with water and cover it and keep aside..
  2. Put half a cup of oil in the pot and heat it. By adding whole spices, frying till the crackling and flavor comes..
  3. Put the chicken in the spices and fry it well. Then add garlic and ginger paste and saute it well..
  4. Put the powder spices and fried onions and roast them well. Put the curd and roast them well..
  5. Put the green spices paste (coriander leaf, pudina leaf, kasuri methi leaf,green chilli) the chicken and roast them well 5 minutes..
  6. Put the kewda water and 1 cup water in the chicken and cover it cook them well..
  7. Check the chicken, the chicken has cooked well and turn off the induction..
  8. Take the chicken out of the pot. Green chicken is ready to eat and now we can enjoy it..
