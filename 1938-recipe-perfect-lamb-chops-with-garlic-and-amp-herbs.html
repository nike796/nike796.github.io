Lamb Chops with Garlic & Herbs instructions

  1. Season both sides of the lamb chop generously with salt and pepper..
  2. Combine garlic, rosemary, thyme, and 2 tablespoons olive oil in a small bowl..
  3. Rub the paste on both sides of the lamb chops and let them marinate for at least 30 minutes at room temperature..
  4. Heat a large 12-inch frying pan over medium-high heat. Add 2 tablespoons olive oil, once hot add in the lamb chops..
  5. Sear until the surface is browned, about 2 to 3 minutes..
  6. Flip and cook until meat reaches an internal temperature of 125°F (51°C) medium rare, or 135°F (57°C) for medium, about 3 to 4 minutes..
  7. Rest lamb chops for 10 minutes before serving..
