Ingredients of Baked Fried Chicken

  1. Prepare 8 of Chicken Pieces (thighs or legs).
  2. Prepare 1 of Salt.
  3. You need 2 cup of Bisquick or Flour.
  4. Prepare 2 of Eggs.
  5. It's 3 cup of Crushed Ripple Potato Chips.
  6. You need 3 cup of Crushed Croutons.
  7. You need 3 tbsp of Vegetable Oil.
  8. It's 1 of Spray Oil.

Baked Fried Chicken step by step

  1. Rinse chicken with water..
  2. Cover large cookie sheet with foil and coat with vegetable oil..
  3. Crush potato chips and croutons in plastic bag..
  4. In separate bowls, add Bisquick, Eggs and crushed potato chips mixed with crushed croutons..
  5. Salt all sides of chicken..
  6. Coat chicken with Bisquick or flour, coat in beaten eggs then coat with potato chip/crouton mix and place on foil covered cookie sheet..
  7. Spray tops of chicken with spray oil or non stick spray..
  8. Bake 375 for 50 minutes..
