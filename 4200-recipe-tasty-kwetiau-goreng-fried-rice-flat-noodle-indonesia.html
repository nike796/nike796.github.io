Kwetiau Goreng (Fried Rice-flat Noodle) - Indonesia instructions

  1. If you have dried kwetiau noodle, prepare it as the instruction on the packaging. If you have the fresh one, make sure you separate it each other (not stick each other) by soak it in cold water..
  2. Cut off the vegetables, sausages, meatball and the chicken. Cut the carrot into slices..
  3. Heat the cooking oil and stir fry the ground seasoning.
  4. Set aside the seasoning before it totally cooked then add the egg and stir-fry.
  5. Add the carrot first and then other vegetables, sausages/meatball/chicken/seafood, the noodle, sweet soy sauce, tomato sauce and water.
  6. After a while, add the celery, evaluate the taste. You can add cucumber as garnish..
  7. Enjoy it and welcome to Indonesia :).
