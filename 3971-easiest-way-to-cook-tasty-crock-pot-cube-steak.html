Ingredients of Crock Pot Cube Steak

  1. Prepare 3 of Large Cube Steak.
  2. It's 2 can of Cream of Mushroom.
  3. You need 3/4 cup of Water.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of Garlic Powder.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of Salt.
  6. Prepare 2 tsp of Black Pepper.
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp of Dried or Fresh Minced Parsley.
  8. You need 1/2 of Medium Onion.
  9. Prepare 4 of Medium Sized Potatoes.

Crock Pot Cube Steak instructions

  1. Mix Cream of Mushroom, water, and seasonings in a bowl with a fork or whisk, getting out as many lumps as possible.
  2. Peel and quarter potatoes.
  3. Peel and slice onion (you can also chop the onion, I just prefer slices).
  4. Halve the steaks so they fit better into the crock pot and season to taste if desired.
  5. Spread a thin layer of cream of mushroom on the bottom of the crock pot.
  6. Add potatoes in single layer.
  7. Add half the onion on top of the potatoes and 1/3 to 1/2 of the cream of mushroom mixture.
  8. Lay in steaks over the potatoes and avoid as much overlapping as possible.
  9. Add the other half of the onions over steaks.
  10. Pour the rest of the cream of mushroom mixture over the steaks and onions, covering as much of the meat as possible ( I spooned it in to make sure it was all covered).
  11. Set crock pot on low for 8 hours and forget about it!.
