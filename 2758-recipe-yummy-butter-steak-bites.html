Ingredients of Butter Steak Bites

  1. It's 1.5-2 lbs of tenderloin steak, cut into bite size pieces.
  2. You need 1 of large onion, large diced.
  3. Prepare 1 lb of asparagus, cut into bite size pieces.
  4. Prepare 6 of garlic cloves, minced.
  5. Prepare 1 stick of butter, divided.
  6. You need 4 tbsp of olive oil.
  7. It's 1 tbsp of Dijon mustard.
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp of tomato paste.
  9. You need 1/2 cup of wine.
  10. Prepare 1 tsp of onion powder.
  11. You need 2 tbsp of Italian seasoning.
  12. Prepare Pinch of crushed red pepper flakes.
  13. It's of Salt & pepper.
  14. Prepare 2 tbsp of siracha sauce.

Butter Steak Bites instructions

  1. Prep veggies, set aside.
  2. Prep the steak and put in bowl. Add salt, pepper and onion powder. Mix to coat steak bites..
  3. Heat large skillet and add in 1/2 of the stick of butter and the olive oil heat to medium high. Add onions and cook until start to caramelize. Add in steak bites, garlic, Dijon and tomato paste. Stir to combine and cook meat. Once browned add in the wine to deglaze pan..
  4. Add in asparagus, Italian seasoning, crushed red pepper flakes and siracha sauce. Stir to combine. Add in last of butter to make sauce. Serve over mashed potatoes or rice or noodles..
