Chimichanga step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 200ºC.
  2. chop chicken into cubes.
  3. fry chicken on a medium heat.
  4. chop all your vegetables (you can really add whatever vegetables you prefer).
  5. add the vegetables, spices and herbs to the chicken and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. add oil to a pan on medium heat.
  7. warm 1 tortilla so it easily handled and wont break when you fold it.
  8. place 1/3 of the mixture into the middle of the tortilla then brush egg around the edges to help with sealing.
  9. fold the tortilla inwards to make a square/rectangle shape.
  10. fry the tortilla seem down til golden brown then flip to gold the other side.
  11. do this with the other two tortillas.
  12. place all 3 tortillas on a baking tray seem down and place in the over cook for 15-20 minutes.
  13. serve with salsa or sour cream.
