Ingredients of Spicy, salsa baked chicken

  1. Prepare 4 of large chicken breasts.
  2. It's 12 oz of favorite salsa (I made my own).
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup of canola oil.
  4. You need 2 cups of balsamic vinegar.
  5. You need 1 tbsp of tabasco.
  6. It's of Favorite bottle of hot sauce.
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp of Lawrys seasoning salt.
  8. You need 1/3 cup of garlic powder.
  9. It's 2 tsp of italian seasoning.
  10. It's 1 tsp of chili powder.
  11. You need 1 tbsp of black pepper.
  12. You need 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese, 1/3 pepper jack (or your fave).
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup of cilantro (for garnish).
  14. Prepare of sour cream to top (optional).

Spicy, salsa baked chicken step by step

  1. Remove chicken from package, do not rinse. Place in large bowl, add canola, balsamic, hot sauces, seasoning. Stir, make sure chicken is covered. Marinade for a min of 1 hour. I used a glass pyrex bowl and covered with a lid, but foil or plastic wrap will work..
  2. Preheat oven to 450. Place chicken breasts in a deep glass baking dish. Pour your favorite salsa on top. Cook for about 25-30 minutes or until chicken has reached safe temp of 160 degrees..
  3. Take the chicken out of the oven. Top with cheeses, bake for five minutes..
  4. Dollip with sour cream and cilantro, enjoy!!.
