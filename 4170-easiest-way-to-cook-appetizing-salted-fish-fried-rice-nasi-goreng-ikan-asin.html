Ingredients of Salted Fish Fried Rice (NASI GORENG IKAN ASIN)

  1. Prepare 300 grams of cooked white rice (at room temperature).
  2. It's 100 grams of salted fish fried, shredded/torn rough.
  3. You need 1 of egg, beaten off and fry while in scrambled.
  4. You need 1 of leek/leaf chives, cut askew.
  5. Prepare 5 of cayenne peppers (to taste), thinly sliced.
  6. Prepare 2 cloves of garlic, minced.
  7. It's 1 of small onion, chopped.
  8. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of soy sauce.
  9. You need 1 tsp of sesame oil.
  10. It's to taste of Salt and pepper.
  11. Prepare of Chicken flavoring (optional).
  12. You need of Oil for frying.

Salted Fish Fried Rice (NASI GORENG IKAN ASIN) instructions

  1. Saute onion, garlic and cayenne pepper until fragrant..
  2. Add rice, salted fish and eggs. Stir until blended..
  3. Add soy sauce, salt, pepper and flavoring (optional). Stir until all blended..
  4. Towards the end, add sesame oil and scallions. Cook, stirring frequently until wilted scallions..
  5. Arrange fried rice on plate and serve while warm..
