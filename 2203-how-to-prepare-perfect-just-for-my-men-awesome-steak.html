Ingredients of Just for my Men Awesome Steak

  1. Prepare 2 of Beef Round Sirloin Tip Boneless Steaks.
  2. You need 1 of worcestershire sauce.
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of Kirkland Steak Seasoning, approximately.
  4. You need 1/2 of onion, sliced into thin strips.
  5. Prepare 1/2 of pepper (i use green) sliced into thin strips.
  6. You need 1/2 cup of sliced canned mushrooms.
  7. Prepare 1 of garlic salt to taste.
  8. It's 1 of splash of sherry.
  9. You need 2 tbsp of butter.
  10. Prepare 1 of olive or vegetable oil for frying.

Just for my Men Awesome Steak step by step

  1. pound meat with meat mallet til thinned out and tender.
  2. sprinkle Worcestershire sauce, sherry, seasoning and garlic salt on both sides of each piece of meat. let sit..
  3. fry onions and pepper strips in oil until tender crisp. add mushrooms and fry 1-2 more minutes.
  4. remove vegetables from oil and let drain on paper towels..
  5. add steak to the oil and fry to desired fineness. top with butter.
  6. top with veggies and sauce from pan. serve over brown rice. enjoy!.
