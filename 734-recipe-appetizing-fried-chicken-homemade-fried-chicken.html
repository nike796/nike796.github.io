Fried Chicken- Homemade Fried Chicken instructions

  1. Pour buttermilk to a bowl. If you do not have it, add 4 tbsp curd to a bowl and pour 1 cup water. Whisk it well with ¼ tsp salt. Mix it well. Check if there is enough salt. Wash chicken and drain off all excess water. Add it to the buttermilk. Refrigerate overnight or at least for 4 hours. This makes the chicken turn tender and soft. Drain the buttermilk.
  2. Add pepper powder, ginger garlic paste, seasoning powder and red chilli powder. Beat an egg and pour it to the bowl. Add corn flour. Mix it well. Keep it aside for at least 45 mins. Pick up the chicken pieces and roll it in the bread crumbs and leave it there for a while.
  3. Heat a heavy bottom pan. check if the oil is heated enough.If the flame is high, chances of getting brown and without getting cooked inside. Drop the chicken pieces into oil. Fry in medium heat. Once it's fried serve hot with ketchup..
