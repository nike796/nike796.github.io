Indonesian coconut beef step by step

  1. Cut steak in 1/2 inch thick strips..
  2. Heat oil in a saucepan. Add Spanish onion slices and garlic and fry gently until soft. Add beef and fry, stirring, until brown..
  3. Add the spices to the beef and cook for 2 minutes..
  4. Add coconut, brown sugar, lemon juice, and beef stock; stir well..
  5. Simmer gently uncovered 30 to 35 minutes, stirring occasionally, or until mixture is thickened and dry. Stir mixture more frequently towards the end of cooking time to prevent sticking..
  6. Garnish with slivers of bell pepper green chilies and small slices of onion..
  7. If you prefer a more moist mixture, cook 20 to 25 minutes instead of 30 to 35 minutes..
