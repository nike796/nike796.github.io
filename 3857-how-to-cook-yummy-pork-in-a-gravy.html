Pork 游냥 in a gravy 游꼩 step by step

  1. In a bowl, mix together soy sauce, brown sugar and cornstarch. Add ginger, garlic, pepper and red chilly flakes to this..
  2. In the above mixture add pork. Mix well, cover and refrigerate for 15-20 min..
  3. Heat half the oil in a skillet at high. Remove pork from marinade and add to skillet. Stir and fry until pork is no longer pink..
  4. Remove the fried pork from and skillet and add remaining oil in the skillet..
  5. Heat the oil and add bell pepper, tomato and onion. Stir fry for 3 min or so..
  6. Add pork and salt to taste. Then add the marinade (the one used for pork). Bring to Boil..
  7. Cook until the sauce thickens, then take it of the heat and let it stay covered for 5 min..
