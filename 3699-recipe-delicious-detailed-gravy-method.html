Detailed gravy method step by step

  1. First make paste of onion garlic and ginger green chilli.
  2. Now in a pan make oil very hot so it doesn't smell bad from gravy.now turn the flame and make oil little cool down.
  3. Now turn the flame very low and add onion paste slowly.be careful this time bcuz hot oil can react when add any watery paste.
  4. Now cook it at low flame and cover the utensil and check every after 2 minutes.
  5. Cook until it spreads oil all around.
  6. Now add all the spices together and mix well again and cook by covering again for 5 to 6 minutes.
  7. Now when it start looking golden add tomatoes paste and mix well.
  8. Now cover the utensil and check every after two minutes until it start changing color and looking aromatic.
  9. It will take 20 to 25 minutes to look properly red and start spreading oil.
  10. Dont over cook and 25 minutes are very enough to cook the gravy.
  11. Preserve or use in once this by adding potatoes,soy,mushrooms or anything.
