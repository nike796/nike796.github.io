Ingredients of Gravy

  1. It's 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour.
  2. You need 3 tbsp of butter.
  3. You need 1 of beef cubes.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of soy sauce.
  5. Prepare 2 cups of water.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of blackpepper.

Gravy instructions

  1. Melt the butter in a pan (low heat).
  2. When the butter turns a little bit brown, pour the flour. Mix well until the butter and flour are evenly combined..
  3. Add the beef cubes and mix well.
  4. Pour the water and continue stirring.
  5. Add the blackpepper and soy sauce. Continue stirring until you reach your desired consistency.
