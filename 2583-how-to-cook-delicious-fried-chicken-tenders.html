Fried Chicken Tenders instructions

  1. Beat the egg in a medium bowl and place the chicken in to soak.
  2. Place the pork rinds, coconut flour, pepper, salt, garlic powder and chipotle powder in a food processor and pulse until the mixture resembles a flour.
  3. Pour the flour mixture onto a large plate and spread it out.
  4. Using a fork, individually coat each chicken strip with the flour mixture and set on a large clean plate.
  5. Heat the coconut oil over medium high heat in a large skillet.
  6. Using tongs, place some of the chicken strips in the oil and cook each side for 3 to 4 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Remove the chicken strips to a plate lined with paper towels to soak up the excess oil repeat with the remaining chicken strips, frying them in batches.
