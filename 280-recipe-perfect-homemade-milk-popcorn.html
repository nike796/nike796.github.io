Ingredients of Homemade Milk Popcorn

  1. It's 1 cup of maize.
  2. You need 4 tablespoons of sugar.
  3. You need 5 tbsp of powdered milk.
  4. Prepare 2 tsp of butter.
  5. It's of Condensed milk flavour, vanila flavour. Anyone you choose is ok.

Homemade Milk Popcorn instructions

  1. Put you 2 tsp of butter into a pot and allow it to melt. Before adding your sugar.
  2. After adding your sugar, close the pot for like 30 seconds before adding your maize..
  3. After adding the maize, you cover the pot and allow it to pop for like 3 minutes. Note: you have to reduce the heat to medium heat..
  4. After allowing it to pop, Add 3 tsp of flavour into the popcorn.
  5. Add your milk..
  6. Stir and enjoy.
