Ingredients of Fresh, Easy Blender Salsa

  1. Prepare 5 of medium tomatoes (I prefer home grown or vine ripened).
  2. It's 2 of jalapeños (de-seeded).
  3. It's 1/2 cup of sweet onion.
  4. You need 1/2 cup of loosely packed cilantro.
  5. It's of Juice from 1 lime.
  6. Prepare 2 tsp of minced garlic.
  7. You need 1 tsp of salt (I use sea salt).
  8. You need 1/4 tsp of fresh ground black pepper.
  9. It's Dash of cumin.
  10. You need Dash of chili powder.

Fresh, Easy Blender Salsa step by step

  1. Roughly chop the tomatoes, onion, cilantro. Just to make it blend easier. Big chunks are fine..
  2. Put everything in the blender. Pulse repeatedly for desired consistency, but don’t purée. You want some texture..
  3. Refrigerate at least an hour to cool it and let the flavors sink in..
  4. Enjoy with tortilla chips!.
