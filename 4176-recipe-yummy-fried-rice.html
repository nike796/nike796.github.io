Fried Rice step by step

  1. Dice the large onion and in a large skillet, sauté the onions in olive oil. Cook until they are opaque, once they are finished, set then aside..
  2. Dice the chicken, pork loin and green onion..
  3. In your large skillet, cook your eggs. Once the eggs are finished, add as much white rice as you'd like..
  4. Mix the eggs, rice and onions. Once mixed, season with salt and garlic powder to taste..
  5. Add the chicken and pork to rice mixture..
  6. In a separate skillet, cook your shrimp. Make sure not to overcook them, cook until they are no longer opaque..
  7. Add your cooked shrimp to your rice mixture. Finish by adding soy sauce to taste and green onions..
