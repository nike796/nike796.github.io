Ingredients of Crushed Crispy Fried Chicken (Ayam goreng geprek)

  1. It's 1 pcs of chicken breast.
  2. It's 1 pcs of chicken thigh.
  3. Prepare 100 g of seasoned flour.
  4. You need 1 pcs of egg.
  5. You need 20 pcs of bird eye chillies.
  6. You need 2 cloves of garlics.
  7. You need of Salt & sugar.
  8. You need of Cooking oil.
  9. Prepare of Indonesian basil.

Crushed Crispy Fried Chicken (Ayam goreng geprek) step by step

  1. Prepare the chicken then cut.
  2. Beat the egg, rub the chicken..
  3. Cover the chicken with the seasoned flour.
  4. Make the chilli: 20 bird eye chilli + garlics + salt and sugar.
  5. Deep fry the chicken until crisp and golden brown and fragrant..
  6. Crush and shred the chicken on the sambal and garnish with indonesian basil..
