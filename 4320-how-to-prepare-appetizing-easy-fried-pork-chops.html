Ingredients of Easy fried pork chops

  1. You need 8 of pork chops (boneless, thin).
  2. Prepare 2 of eggs whisked.
  3. You need 2 tbsp of mustard ( I use dijon).
  4. It's 2 cup of bread crumbs (I use unseasoned).
  5. It's 1/4 cup of dry ranch seasoning.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese from a can.
  7. Prepare 1 of oil to coat fry pan.

Learn how to make a simple and delicious Fried Pork Chops using simple ingredients. Bone-in or boneless pork chops will work, but I prefer bone-in as they have more flavor. Easy fried pork chops step by step

  1. Rinse and dry pork chops.
  2. Whisk eggs and mustard. Coat chops in mixture..
  3. Mix the cheese, crumbs and ranch. Dredge each chop in crumb mix and cover evenly..
  4. Heat oil til a drop of water sizzles. At that point GENTLY slide 3-4 chops into frying pan. Don't crowd the pan. Fry chops on medium heat 3-4 minutes each side..
  5. Let cool slightly and watch them disappear!.
