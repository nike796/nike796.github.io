Ingredients of Beef & Broccoli Stir Fry with variations

  1. You need 2 tbsp of lemon juice.
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp of firm packed dark brown sugar.
  3. Prepare 1 clove of garlic, crushed.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of black pepper.
  5. You need 2 tbsp of vegetable oil, divided.
  6. It's 2 lb of top sirloin steak, sliced 1/4 in. thick.
  7. It's 1 medium of onion, thin sliced.
  8. Prepare 2 medium of heads broccoli.
  9. Prepare 2 tsp of grated fresh gingerroot.
  10. It's 1/2 cup of water.
  11. It's 1/3 cup of soy sauce.
  12. You need 1 1/4 tbsp of corn starch.
  13. Prepare 6 oz of sliced mushrooms.

Beef & Broccoli Stir Fry with variations step by step

  1. in a small bowl, combine soy sauce, lemon juice, corn starch, brown sugar, garlic and pepper. mix well. set aside.
  2. heat 1tbl. vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. add beef and cook until almost done. about 2 minutes. transfer beef to a bowl and cover to keep warm. set aside.
  3. cut broccoli into florets. heat remaining oil in same skillet. add onions and saute for about 5 minutes. add broccoli and mushrooms and water. bring to boil, cover and reduce heat to a simmer for 3 minutes..
  4. return beef to skillet. add soy sauce mixture and ginger. bring to a boil. stir constantly and cook until sauce thickens. about 2 min..
  5. serve hot over rice. enjoy!.
  6. variation: instead of the soy sauce in step one.. add 1/2 cup black bean sauce. and serve over egg noodles and top with toasted sesame seeds..
