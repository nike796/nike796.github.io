Cool Ranch Doritos Baked Chicken üêî instructions

  1. Rinse and pat dry your chicken thighs.......
  2. Place thighs in a large baking pan......
  3. Season both sides of thighs with black pepper and garlic powder......
  4. Cover and bake in a preheated 400 degree oven for 1 hour.......
  5. Meanwhile combine in a large bowl : 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, cream of chicken soup, milk, sour cream, Rotel tomatoes and dry ranch seasoning, mix all well.......Set bowl to the side until chicken is done baking.......
  6. After chicken has baked for 1 hour remove chicken from oven and reduce oven temp to 350 degrees.......
  7. Place half of coarsely crushed Doritos all around chicken thighs......
  8. Now add cream of chicken/ tomato mixture to the top of chicken thighs.......Make sure you fully cover those thighs !!!!! üòä.
  9. Place remaining crushed Doritos on top of chicken thighs, then top that with the remaining mozzarella cheese........
