Oriental Pepper Steak step by step

  1. Heat a large skillet with oil to thin cover the bottom. Season beef lightly with salt and pepper, sear quickly to brown each piece, remove as done to a plate.
  2. Add mushrooms and onions to skillet, cover and cook until soft about 5 minutes..
  3. Uncover skillet and add all peppers and stir fry until tender, 5 to 10 minutes, add tomatos and cook just until soft, 1 or 2 minutes, remove all vegetables to another plate..
  4. Combine all sauce ingrdients in a bowl, whisk well, add to skillet and bring to a boil and cook until it thickens to a sauce 4 to 5 minutes, add vegetables and beef to sauce and heat through.Serve sprinkled with seasame seeds..
  5. Serve with cooked basmati or jasmati rice. https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/348908-basmati-or-jasmati-rice-basic-recipe.
