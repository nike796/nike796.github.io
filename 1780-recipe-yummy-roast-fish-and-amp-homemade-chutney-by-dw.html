Roast Fish & Homemade Chutney - By DW instructions

  1. FISH COOKING PROCESS.
  2. For the fish. My preference is to buy it fresh. However you buy it, ensure that the inside is clean. Next butterfly it like you would a shrimp..
  3. Rub the combined seasoning on the fish. Add salt and pepper to taste. After the rub - drizzle evenly with olive oil.
  4. Place fish in a rack or tray and grill over a flame until cooked..
  5. Once cooked, drizzle olive oil and squeeze fresh lime. Serve 1 fish per person..
