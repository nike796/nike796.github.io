Fried Chicken Joy step by step

  1. pound chicken breast thin.
  2. add 2 tbls of water to beaten egg.
  3. add salt and pepper to egg mixture.
  4. smother breast in egg mixture.
  5. heat oil to 375°F.
  6. mix panko, flour, and BBQ seasoning.
  7. dredge breast in flour mixture.
  8. fry breast 4 minutes on each side.
  9. slice roll, toast and coat both sided with stone ground mustard.
  10. allow breast to rest for 5 mins then assemble sandwich.
