Curried Chicken Wings OMG Yummy step by step

  1. Clean trim wings pat dry..
  2. Season with salt and pepper..
  3. Add to crock pot and cook in oven till crispy and cooked..
  4. Add curry sauce and chilli and onions.
  5. Cook until sauce is heated and reduced.
  6. Serve with cilantro over top.
