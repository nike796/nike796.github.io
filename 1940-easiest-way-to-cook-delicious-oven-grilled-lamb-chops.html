Ingredients of Oven-Grilled Lamb Chops

  1. Prepare 8 of Lamb chops.
  2. You need 1/2 of red pepper cubed.
  3. Prepare 1/2 of green pepper cubed.
  4. Prepare 1 of onion thickly sliced.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of bbq spice.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of steak and chops spice.
  7. You need 2 tsp of masala.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of mixed dry herbs.
  9. You need 1 Tbs of bbq sauce.
  10. It's 4 Tbs of oil.

Oven-Grilled Lamb Chops instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 °C. Marinate the chops with spices and bbq sauce leave aside for 30 mins..
  2. Add oil to an oven tray, add the chops. Cook on both sides for 5 mins each, add the peppers, onions and dry herbs on top of chops and grill for 20 mins turning the chops over to evenly grill..
  3. Enjoy with sides of mashed potato, gravy and veg of choice..
