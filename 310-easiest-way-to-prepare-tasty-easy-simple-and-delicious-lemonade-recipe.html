Easy, simple and delicious lemonade recipe step by step

  1. In a small sufuria make a simple syrup. Add the 1 cup of water and let it boil then on medium heat stir in the 1 cup sugar until the sugar dissolves completely.
  2. In a bigger jug add the syrup the ice cubes, extra water and the lemon juice and stir.
  3. Garnish with some cute lemon slices and enjoy.
