Black Pepper Beef Stir Fry instructions

  1. Marinate beef cubes and let sit 10 mins.
  2. Sear beef cubes in pan and remove.
  3. Add some oil to the pan and stir fry onion and capsicum.
  4. Return beef cubes to pan and pour in stir fry sauce and stir up for a minute.
  5. Finish up with some additional black pepper to taste.
