FRIED CHICKEN CASSEROLE! step by step

  1. Wash and boil the chicken with alittle water and fresh garlic,ginger,onion,spices and maggi. Allow the water to drain in the chicken to be testier..
  2. Deep fry the chicken in hot oil. Grate the pepper, tattashe and onion. Stir fry it with alittle oil adding alittle maggi and spices..
  3. Wash peel and slice the carrots,green pepper and green beans to desired shapes.boil and drain..
  4. Add the fried chicken, the boiled veggies and stir fry sauce together and stir fry again on a low heat. Enjoy.
