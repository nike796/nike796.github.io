My Boo's fav steak <3 instructions

  1. dice onion, mushroom, garlic & green pepper.
  2. rince steak under cold water & place in the pan (make sure they are all flat, don't over lap in pan.).
  3. spred the diced onion, mushroom, green pepper & garlic over the steak in pan..
  4. sprinkle adobo, garlic powder, & black pepper over steak next..
  5. add the soy sauce, the worcesteshire sauce & the balsomic italian vinigrette.
  6. put in fridge & marinate for at least a hour (the longer the better).
  7. get out ure fav frying pan n spray with nonstic spray.
  8. place steak on frying pan, poor marinaid on top. fry on med. heat about 10 minutes on eachside (depending on how well done you like it).
