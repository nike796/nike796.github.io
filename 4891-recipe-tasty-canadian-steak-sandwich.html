Canadian steak sandwich instructions

  1. BBQ steak at medium heat about 5 minutes a side for medium rare then sliced thin and divide between 4 sandwiches.
  2. Cook peppers and yams in boiling water for 7 minutes then pan fry on medium heat for 4 minutes.
  3. Coat buns with Miracle Whip and horseradish.
  4. Add vegetables and thinly sliced steak.
  5. Scramble 4 eggs 1 per sandwich great for a breakfast sandwich.
  6. Add 5 slices of cheese to each bun.
  7. Place buns on cookie sheet and bake on 350 for 4 minutes in the oven or until cheese is melted extra minutes for crunchy bread.
