  2. Cut steak into small pieces then, season with salt, pepper and, cornstarch, let sit for 1/2 hour..
  3. Add oil to skillet add garlic,peppers, steak, cook till no longer pink, add rice might have to add a little water cause of cornstarch, let simmer..
