My Grilling Spot Scallop Stew instructions

  1. Wash the scallops with water and set them aside, ideally inside the fridge..
  2. Pre heat the pan over the grill..
  3. Add the Butter and the Garlic Sauce..
  4. Mix Well and let it cook for 5 minutes..
  5. Ad the salt to the mix..
  6. Put the scallops in the pan and mix everything. You need to stir every other minute to ensure all the scallops are well cooked..
  7. Add the white wine and let it reduce for 10 minutes..
  8. Add the Olive Oil..
  9. Add the Parsley and mix well..
  10. Add the fish broth and let it cook for 10 minutes at low heat..
  11. Serve and enjoy!.
