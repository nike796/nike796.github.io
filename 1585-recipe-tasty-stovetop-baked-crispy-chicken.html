Stovetop baked crispy chicken step by step

  1. Grind up dry stovetop stuffing into breadcrumbs. I use a magic bullet on pulse but you can use blender, food processor or smash in a bag..
  2. In a shallow dish scramble 2 raw eggs.
  3. Put crushed stovetop stuffing in a large Ziploc bag.
  4. Dip chicken in egg and add to crumbs in Ziploc bag, shake well (I do about three pieces at a time).
  5. Place breaded chicken on foil lined dish.
  6. Add 2 small pieces of butter on each piece of chicken.
  7. Bake at 375°F, time depends on size and thickness of chicken. Check to make sure they are cooked through. Let cool 5 min before removing from foil..
