Non fried chicken roll instructions

  1. For dough: in a mixing bowl add all the ingredients and knead a smooth dough. Cover it and keep aside for 30 min..
  2. Meanwhile take a vessel and add oil and heat it. Add chicken mince and saute till it chance it's color. Add ginger garlic paste and saute till raw aroma disappears. Add all the spices and saute again for few minutes. Cover it and cook on medium flame till tender. Sprinkle some coriander leaves and transfer to a bowl. Add hot sauce and tomato ketchup. Mix well. Keep aside..
