Ingredients of cod roe vermicelli

  1. You need 400 grams of cod roe.
  2. You need 190 grams of diced onion.
  3. You need 4 clove of garlic.
  4. You need 2 tbsp of olive oil.
  5. You need 200 grams of rice vermicelli (uncooked).
  6. Prepare 500 grams of grilled vegetables (pre-packaged frozen or homemade).
  7. You need 1 tbsp of white wine vinagre.
  8. It's 4 tbsp of soya sauce.

cod roe vermicelli instructions

  1. put a pot of water to simmer adding in the vinagre and cod roe.
  2. allow the cod roe to fully cook without the water coming to a boil.
  3. remove the cod roe from the water.
  4. add the vermicelli to the cod roe cooking water and follow the manufacturer cooking instructions.
  5. while the pasta cooks remove the membranes from the cod roe and use your hands to separate the cod roe into as small parts as possible.
  6. in a large frying pan or wok heat the olive oil.
  7. in a separate pan cook the vegetables if you are using the frozen kind.
  8. finely chop or crush the garlic, add to the heated oil and cook until fragrant.
  9. add in the chopped onions and cook until onions are transparent.
  10. add the separated cod roe and mix well.
  11. add in 3/4 of the soya sauce and mix well.
  12. add the vegetables to the cod roe.
  13. allow to cook over low heat for 5min to unite the flavours.
  14. add in the vermicelli and mix well.
  15. add the remaining soya sauce to the mix and cook over low heat about 3 to 5min mixing constantly.
  16. enjoy!.
