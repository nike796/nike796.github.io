  6. You need 1.5 cup of White rice.

Pumpkin Rice instructions

  1. Heat up oil in pan. Put in Garlic and Red onion. Fry till fragance..
  2. Put in Anchovies and Chinese Sausage. Fry till fragrance..
  3. Put in Pork chop meat and fry till halfly cooked..
  4. Add in white rice to fry and well mixed..
  5. Add seasoning (salt, white pepper, soy sauce)..
  6. Transfer all into rice cooker. Put sliced pumpkins on top. Add in sufficient water and start the rice cooker..
  7. Once rice cooker done cooking, add in raisin and mix well. Enjoy!.
