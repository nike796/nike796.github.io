Ingredients of Brad's fried plantains over rice

  1. It's 2 of lg plantains.
  2. You need of oil for frying.
  3. It's 2 cups of uncooked rice.
  4. It's 4 cups of water.
  5. You need 4 tsp of granulated chicken bouillon.
  6. Prepare 1 of small can sweetened condensed milk.

The skin of the plantain should be almost entirely black when it's mature and ready to use. Heat the oil over medium-high heat in a saute pan until hot but not. Using plantains instead of regular rice gives this fried rice natural sweet flavor. Cover and set over medium-low heat.

Brad's fried plantains over rice instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan start rice by adding rice, water, and bouillon. Bring to a boil uncovered. Stir, then reduce heat to a low simmer. Cover and do not stir. When water is almost absorbed, shut off heat. Leave on burner to finish..
  2. Meanwhile, in a frying pan. Preheat over medium, enough oil to just cover bottom of the pan..
  3. Peel plantains. Cut 3" long. Cut those pieces lengthwise into thin strips. Fry in oil until browned on both sides. Drain on paper towels..
  4. Plate rice. Put fried plantains over the top. Drizzle with sweetened condensed milk and serve..
