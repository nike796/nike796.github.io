Fried porkchops step by step

  1. Heat cooking oil in frying pan..
  2. Wash pork chops thoroughly. I use vinegar to clean..
  3. Dry off chops..
  4. Season chops with all dry seasonings, minus 1 sazon packet and flour..
  5. Mix one sazon packet and flour together..
  6. Once chops are fully covered in seasonings, dip one at a time in flour mix..
  7. Make sure both sides are coated with flour mix..
  8. SLOWLY place 2-3 chops in got frying pan. Make sure the chops are not touching..
  9. Cook until brown on each side..
  10. When done frying, lay each piece on a paper bag to absorb the excess oil..
