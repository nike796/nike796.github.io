Recipe: Tasty Turmeric Grilled Chicken

Delicious, fresh and tasty.

Turmeric Grilled Chicken.

You can cook Turmeric Grilled Chicken using 14 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Turmeric Grilled Chicken

  1. It's 1/2 small of Onion.
  2. It's 1 of Shallot.
  3. It's 2 clove of Garlic.
  4. It's 1 tsp of Ginger.
  5. Prepare 1 stick of Lemon grass.
  6. It's of Grounded spice.
  7. You need 1/2 tsp of galangal.
  8. You need 100 ml of Water.
  9. It's 5 of pieces lemon leaf.
  10. You need 1 tsp of Turmeric powder.
  11. You need 100 ml of Coconut milk.
  12. You need 1 of Salt, sugar and black pepper.
  13. It's 1/2 tsp of Chilli powder.
  14. You need 8 slice of Chicken thigh.

Turmeric Grilled Chicken instructions

  1. Stir the grounded spice, galangal and water for 4-5 minutes..
  2. Add the rest ingredients except the chicken. Stir continuously and taste it. Squeeze a little bit lemon or lime to have a nice yellow color..
  3. Add the chicken and cook for 5-8 minutes. In the mean time preheat the oven 180°F Celsius..
  4. Take out the chicken, put it on the baking tray with some sauce. Bake it 20 minutes at 180°F and 10 minutes at 150°F (turn the chicken every 10 minutes). You can also grill the chicken and put some sauce on it..
  5. Thicken the rest sauce. Put it on medium heat for 5-10 minutes..
