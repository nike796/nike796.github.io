Beef Stew Made with Steak Meat step by step

  1. Cut the potatoes, onions, and carrots into bite-size pieces..
  2. Cut the beef into pieces of any size you like. I used sirloin steak..
  3. Saute the beef in a frying pan..
  4. Brown the meat on both sides..
  5. Add the red wine..
  6. Simmer until the amount of red wine is reduced by about half Now stir-fry the vegetables..
  7. Add the ● ingredients to a pot. Add the meat and stir-fried carrot and onion from Step 6 and bring to a simmer..
  8. Simmer for about 10 minutes..
  9. Now add the ★ ingredients and the potatoes and simmer until the potatoes are soft..
  10. I used this demi-glace sauce..
  11. Add butter for the finish and it's done..
  12. Drizzle heavy cream on top and serve. It looks delicious..
