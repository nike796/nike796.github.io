Sweet and spicy chicken wings step by step

  1. preheat oven 325°F.
  2. Combine Coke and sauce..
  3. Arrange cooked spicy wings in a casserole dish, grouped tightly together..
  4. Pour all of the sauce over the wings..
  5. Bake for twenty minutes..
  6. When done, put wings in a large bowl and pour the sauce from the dish over the wings again. Serve and enjoy!.
  7. Optional. You can customize this recipe however you like. You can use different flavor stir fry sauces or use non spicy wings..
  8. Also, if you start with raw chicken, cook for at least one hour or until temperature reaches 165°F..
