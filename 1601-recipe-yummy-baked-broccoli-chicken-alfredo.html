  4. It's 2 tbsp of oil.
  5. Prepare 3 tbsp of butter.
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of milk.
  7. Prepare 3 clove of garlic minced.
  8. Prepare 3 tbsp of flour.
  9. You need 1 1/2 cup of Italian cheese mix.
  10. It's of grated parmesan cheese.

Baked broccoli chicken alfredo instructions

  1. Pre heat oven 375. Boil salted water to cook pasta. Once pasta is about 2 minutes out add broccoli. Set aside once done..
  2. While pasta is cooking, cube chicken and salt and pepper. Saute chicken in oil till dine set aside..
  3. Melt butter. Add garlic. Cook 1 minute. Add 3 tablespoon flour. Whisk to create a rue. Add milk and salt pepper. Cook about 5 minutes while whisking. Add Italian cheese mix. Whisk ..
  4. Mix ingredients and put in a baking dish. Top with parmesan cheese. Bake for 15 minutes. Enjoy.
