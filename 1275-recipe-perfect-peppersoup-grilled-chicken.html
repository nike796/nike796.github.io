Peppersoup grilled chicken instructions

  1. Wash your chicken and cut into desired sizes.
  2. Mix the pepper, spice, salt and olive oil together.
  3. Rub the mixture into the chicken thoroughly.
  4. Cover and place the chicken in the refrigerator for 30 mins or more to marinate.
  5. Place the marinated chicken on your baking tray and grill on medium heat for 1 hour or until the meat is cooked through..
  6. Enjoy with meal or as is..
