Leftover chicken steak shawarma step by step

  1. Fry chicken steak pieces then julienne cut them.
  2. In the same pan add oil then fry capsicum, carrot and cabbage then add fried chicken and soy and chilli sauce. Mix them well. Cook for 2 mint.
  3. In a bowl mix chilli garlic ketchup and mayo garlic sauce to make shawarma sauce.
  4. Cut potatoes and fry them as we make french fries..
  5. Now take pita bread. Spread shawarma sauce then add chicken veggies and french fries. Wrap with butter paper. it's ready.
