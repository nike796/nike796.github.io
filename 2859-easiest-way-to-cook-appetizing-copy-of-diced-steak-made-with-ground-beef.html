  9. Prepare 2 tbsp of water.
  10. You need 1/4 tbsp of minced garlic.

Copy of Diced Steak Made with Ground Beef step by step

  1. Mix the first three ingredients in a bowl, including the garlic. Then flatten the meat into one square like in the picture. Cut into 2-3 cm squares..
  2. Cook on the griddle with your favourite sauce, such as a Yakiniku Japanese BBQ sauce (not listed). From a separate bowl, this is where I whisk the soy sauce, sugar, cornstarch, and water together. Then slowly add the mixture to the meat a tbsp at a time and stirring in between mix..
  3. Cook each side of the meat, turning with a spatula so it doesn't burn. I also added shredded carrots (optional and not listed). It's done when the sauce has mixed in..
  4. You can also make this in a frying pan, and serve it with grated daikon radish and ponzu sauce. It is delicious..
  5. I finished this delicious recipe on a lettuce wrap and garnish with shredded radishes..
