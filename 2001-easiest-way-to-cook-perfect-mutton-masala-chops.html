Ingredients of Mutton Masala Chops

  1. Prepare 12 of Lamb Chops.
  2. It's 6-8 tablespoon of Oil.
  3. You need 2 tablespoon of Chaat Masala.
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon of Chilli Flakes.
  5. It's 1 tablespoon of Cumin Seeds.
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoon of Mango Powder (Amchoor).
  7. Prepare 4 tablespoon of Lemon Juice.
  8. You need 1 tablespoon of Degi Kashmiri.
  9. It's of Mirch Powder.
  10. You need to taste of Salt.

Mutton Masala Chops instructions

  1. 1. Wash and drain the chops in a colander.
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients together Rub onto the chops making sure they are fully coated.
  3. Next add the oil and lemon juice and mix well Leave to marinate for 1-2 hours - overnight works best.
  4. Heat oil in a large wide based non stick frying pan Once it's nearly smoking hot add the chop Cook for 1-2 minutes & turn the side and add any leftover marinade and keep turning and basting with oil and marinade as you cook further.
  5. Cook chops till done to your preference Remove and serve immediately..
