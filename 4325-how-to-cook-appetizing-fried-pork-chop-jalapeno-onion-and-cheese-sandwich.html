Ingredients of Fried pork chop jalapeno onion and cheese sandwich

  1. You need 2 each of boneless pork chops.
  2. Prepare 1 1/4 slice of onion.
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 slice of jalapeno.
  4. Prepare 1 slice of cheese.
  5. It's 2 slice of bread.
  6. Prepare 1 of vegetable oil.
  7. Prepare 1 of garlic salt.
  8. It's 1 of ground black pepper.

Fried pork chop jalapeno onion and cheese sandwich step by step

  1. Get a frying pan add oil start heating oil while oil is heating season pork chop with garlic salt and pepper.
  2. Place pork chops in pan fry till golden brown add slice onions and peppers fry until soft.
  3. When all is done frying put pork chops on bread top with cheese add onions and jalapenos! Enjoy.
