Chicken Bread (Ramadan Recipe) step by step

  1. Add in a large bowl flour,yeast,salt,sugar and oil. Mix well..
  2. Add gradually milk and water.And knead until dough smooth..
  3. Cover and leave for 1 & 1/2 hours in a warm place..
  4. Add in pan oil and chopped onion. Fry onion when it golden brown..
  5. Now add chicken and cook for 2 min on high flame..
  6. Add soya sauce and cook for 1 min. Now add all spices (salt,black pepper powder,chilli powder)..
  7. Add ketchup and cook for 4-5 mins.Add coriander..
  8. Chop chicken coarsely..
  9. After 1& 1/2 hours knead the dough until smooth..
  10. Now divide the dough in three pieces.
  11. Take one piece of dough in make a rectangle shape..
  12. Take a chicken and put in the med of dough..
  13. Cut the each side of the dough with the help of knife..
  14. Now fold the one by one strip.repeat this process and make your own choices bread..
  15. Dust the flour on baking tray.and make all breads..
  16. Now brush egg on all the breads.And preheat the oven at 180 c..
  17. At 180 c bake bread for 20-25 mins..
  18. Serve..
