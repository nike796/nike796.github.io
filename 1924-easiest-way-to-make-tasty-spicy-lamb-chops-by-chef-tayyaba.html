Spicy lamb chops by chef tayyaba instructions

  1. Sb se pehly hum lamb chops ko meat tenderizer laga ke rakh dain gey 30 minutes ke liye.
  2. Ab hum marinate karen gey sb ingredients ko ek bowl main dal kar paste bana lain.
  3. Or ab jo lamb chops hain unko masala laga kar rakh dain 1 day ke liye or agr ziyada jaldi hai to 4 to 5 hours marinate laga kar rahkna lazmi hai.
  4. Ab lamb chops ko koila ka smoke dena hai yaad rahe jab hum ne koila ka smoke diya hai 30 minutes take isko open ni krna.
  5. Ab hum lamb chops ko chulha pa pakain gey slow flame pe water itna add krna hai jitne mein lamb gal jaye.
  6. Ab jab serve krna hai to grill pan pey color dena hai.
  7. Or jo vegetables hain unko b grill kar ke decorations kar lain.
  8. Ab platter main dal kar serve karen spicy lamb chops tiyaar hain.
