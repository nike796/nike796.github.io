Ingredients of Honey and French onion baked fried chicken

  1. It's 1 packages of chicken legs.
  2. You need 2 cup of all-purpose flour.
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup of honey.
  4. You need 1 packages of french onion dip.
  5. It's 1 tsp of salt.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of black pepper.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of crushed red pepper.
  8. You need 1 tsp of onion powder.
  9. You need 1 tsp of garlic powder.
  10. It's 3 tbsp of butter.
  11. It's 1 cup of vegetable oil.

Honey and French onion baked fried chicken instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 and put about 1 cup of veg oil in pan and butter..
  2. Rinse chicken in cold water.
  3. Mix flour, pepper, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, French onion dip and crushes red pepper together and put in a gallon size ziploc and shake..
  4. Put chicken in bag and shake one piece at a time works best..
  5. After you have battered each piece rub honey on each piece generously..
  6. One at a time re batter the chicken..
  7. Put chicken in oven and bake for about 60 min. Flipping chicken ever 10 min or so until done. Time depending on size of chicken legs..
