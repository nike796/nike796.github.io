Philly cheese steak pizza instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°..
  2. Unroll pizza dough onto greased cookie sheet pressing dough 1/2" up sides of pan..
  3. Bake 7 minutes. Remove from oven.
  4. Meanwhile, saute onion, pepper, shrooms & bullion cube in olive oil til very tender..
  5. Brush cooked crust with italian dressing.
  6. Break off pieces of roast beef and randomly place on crust..
  7. Top with white american.
  8. Add cooked veggies on top of cheese.
  9. Sprinkle with mozzerella.
  10. Place back in oven for another 7 minutes..
  11. Let cool, slice. Top with parmesan & red pepper flakes if you want..
  12. Serve with ranch for dipping.
