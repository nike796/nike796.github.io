Chicken Chilli dry recipe instructions

  1. Take a bowl and add beaten egg, 1tsp soya sauce, 1tbsp chilli sauce, 1tbsp corn flour, black pepper. Add salt according to taste. Mix all the ingredients well..
  2. Add chicken to the above mixture and marinate for 30 minutes..
  3. Take oil in a fry pan put on heat. Put the marinated chicken in oil and fry for 5-8 minutes..
  4. Now put chicken aside in plate. Add onion to the pan and cook for 2 minutes..
  5. Now add ginger- garlic paste. When it started smell than add fried Chicken, green chillies, remaining chilli garlic and soya sauce, salt as needed and cook for 3-5 minutes. Now chicken chilli dry is ready to serve ðŸ˜‹..
