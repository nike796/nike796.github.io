Ingredients of siracha cranberry bbq wings

  1. Prepare 4 lb of frozen chicken wings, thawed.
  2. You need 1 of McCormick © chicken rub.
  3. You need 1 1/2 cup of bbq sauce.
  4. Prepare 1 can of jellied cranberry sauce.
  5. Prepare 1/3 cup of siracha sauce.
  6. Prepare 1 of few splashes of Louisiana hot sauce.

siracha cranberry bbq wings step by step

  1. preheat oven to 400.
  2. place wings on a broiler pan. sprinkle liberally with chicken rub. bake for 45 min or until done..
  3. place all sauces in a blender. blend until smooth..
  4. place sauce in a pan and heat on med until warm.
  5. when wings are done, put in a bowl. pour sauce over and toss. serve while hot.
