Steak Sandwiches with Chipotle Secret Sauce instructions

  1. Prepare grill to 450. Season meat with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder..
  2. Grill 5 minutes per side or until the desired degree of doneness; let stand 5 minutes..
  3. Slice into 1/4 inch thick strips against the grain of the meat..
  4. Divide steak strips among bottom halves of rolls; assemble the sandwich with, onion, cilantro, lime juice, and avocado if desired..
  5. Add Chipotle Secret sauce to both sides on the bun..
