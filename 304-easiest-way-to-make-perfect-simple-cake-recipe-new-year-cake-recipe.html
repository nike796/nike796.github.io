Ingredients of Simple cake recipe/New year cake recipe

  1. It's 2 cups of flour.
  2. You need 1 cup of sugar.
  3. It's 1 sachet of simas butter.
  4. It's 4 of eggs.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of milk.
  6. You need 2 tsp of baking powder.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of baking soda.
  8. You need 2 tsp of vanilla extract.
  9. It's 1 1/2 cup of raisin.
  10. Prepare 1 cup of mini chocolate chips.

  3. Add milk and vanilla flavor.
  4. Shift over the dry ingredients, flour baking soda, and baking powder and mix all together..
