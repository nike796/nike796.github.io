Ingredients of Santa Fe Grilled Chicken

  1. Prepare 4 of skinless chicken breast filleted.
  2. You need to taste of pepper, garlic powder, and seasoning salt.
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup of butter.
  4. Prepare 3 slices of pepper jack cheese.
  5. You need of Fresh Salsa.
  6. You need 4 of small sweet tomatoes.
  7. You need 1/2 of onion.
  8. Prepare of hand full of cilantro.
  9. You need 2 of garlic cloves.
  10. It's tsp of lemon juice.
  11. You need to taste of salt, pepper, & garlic powder.

Santa Fe Grilled Chicken step by step

  1. In a food processor place tomatoes, garlic, onion and garlic and blend. Add salt, pepper, garlic salt, and lemon juice. Place in a small bowl and set aside..
  2. Preheat grill, fillet chicken breast and season to taste..
  3. Melt butter and pour on chicken breast and place on grill. Cook on grill 4 to 5 minutes then turn over for another 4 to 5 minutes or till done. Place cheese on chicken till melted..
  4. Place on a plate and add fresh salsa on top. Enjoy!!!.
