Huntsman stew instructions

  1. Wash the chicken breast and chop it into cubes.
  2. Chop the onion and the garlic.
  3. Chop the carrot, the tomatoe and the pepper not too small pieces.
  4. In a frying pan add the oil and the chicken chops.
  5. Add the garlic and onion.
  6. Add the curry powder or the chicken spices, stir and let fry for 3 to 5 minutes untill meat changes color.
  7. Add the tomatoe chops and stir.
  8. Add the passata and stir.
  9. Add the carrot and pepper slices.
  10. Stir well, add salt and pepper.
  11. Cover it and let it boil untill the chicken and vegetables are tender.
  12. Add the green beans, stir easily avoiding to smash them, let it cook for 5 more minutes uncovered.
  13. Chop the parsley and sprinkle all over.
  14. Serve hot with bread.
