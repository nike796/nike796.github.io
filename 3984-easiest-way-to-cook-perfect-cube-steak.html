Ingredients of Cube Steak

  1. You need 1/4 tsp of salt.
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp of all-purpose flour.
  3. Prepare 4 oz of cube steak.
  4. It's 2 tbsp of butter/divided.
  5. It's 1 tsp of Dijon mustard.
  6. You need 1 tsp of Worcestershire sauce,divided.
  7. You need 1/2 lb of fresh mushrooms, sliced.
  8. You need 1 pinch of pepper.
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp of chopped onion.
  10. You need 2 tbsp of minched fresh parsley.

Cube Steak instructions

  1. In a shallow bowl or reasealable plastic bag, combine flour, salt, and pepper..
  2. Add steaks; dredge or toss to coat..
  3. In a skillet over medium heat, brown steak in 1 tablespoon of butter..
  4. Remove the steaks; spread each with 1/2 teaspoon mustard. Pour 1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce over each; keep warm..
  5. In the same skillet, saute the mushrooms and onion in remaining butter until tender. Add parsley and remaining Worcestershire sauce. Return steaks to skillet; cover and simmer for 5-7 minutes or until meat is tender..
