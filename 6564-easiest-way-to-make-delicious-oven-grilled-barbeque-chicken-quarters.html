Oven Grilled Barbeque Chicken Quarters instructions

  1. Thoroughly wash and clean the chicken. After, using a paper towel pat dry the chicken quarters until dry..
  2. In a small bowl, combine all the seasonings and stir to combine..
  3. Now, with a brush, generously brush the seasoning onto the chicken, seasoning both sides of each. Let rest for 10 minutes uncovered on the counter..
  4. Meanwhile, grease the grill or grid of your oven with oil and preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit..
  5. After the chicken rests, place them skin side down on the grill or grid and put in the preheated oven. Place a dripping pan below the grill..
  6. Roast in the middle of the oven for 60 minutes+, depending on how dry or cooked you want your chicken. Basting the chicken with the seasoning mix every 20 minutes..
  7. Meanwhile, in a small saucepan combine all the ingredients for the barbeque sauce and cook for about 3 minutes stirring frequently until a dark red colour is reached. Set aside for later..
  8. With 10 minutes to go, take the chicken out of the oven and brush all sides of the chicken with the barbeque sauce, put back in the oven to roast for the last 10 minutes..
  9. Take out of the oven when done cooking and set aside to cool for 5 minutes. After, cut it up if desired and serve hot or warm. Enjoy!.
