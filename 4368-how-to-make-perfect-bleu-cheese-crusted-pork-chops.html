Ingredients of Bleu Cheese Crusted Pork Chops

  1. Prepare 2 of boneless pork chops.
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup of french fried onions.
  3. You need 1/2 stick of butter; melted.
  4. It's 1/3 cup of bleu cheese crumbles.
  5. You need 2 tsp of Italian seasoning.
  6. Prepare to taste of kosher salt and white pepper.
  7. It's as needed of olive oil.
