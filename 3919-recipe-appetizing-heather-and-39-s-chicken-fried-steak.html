Ingredients of Heather's Chicken Fried Steak

  1. You need 2 cup of flour.
  2. You need 2 large of eggs.
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup of milk.
  4. You need 1 tsp of salt.
  5. It's 1 tsp of black pepper.
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp of creole seasoning.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of chili powder.
  8. It's 1 tsp of onion powder.
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of garlic powder.
  10. You need 1 tsp of parsley.
  11. It's 2 medium of boneless tenderized shoulder steak.
  12. You need 1 of vegetable oil for frying.

Heather's Chicken Fried Steak step by step

  1. heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Meanwhile combine flour and seasonings. In a seperate bowl combine milk and eggs..
  2. Dip each steak in the flour, egg, flour, egg, flour. In that order..
  3. Once the steaks are breaded, put them in the skillet with oil and cook until they are fully cooked. Drain on paper towels. Serve with country gravy..
