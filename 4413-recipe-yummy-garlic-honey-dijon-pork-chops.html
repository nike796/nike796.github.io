Ingredients of Garlic Honey-Dijon Pork Chops

  1. Prepare 8 of thin cut pork chops.
  2. You need of Marinade.
  3. Prepare 2 TBS of minced garlic.
  4. You need 2 TBS of dijon mustard.
  5. Prepare 1 TBS of stone ground mustard.
  6. Prepare 3 TBS of honey.
  7. It's 1 half of fresh juiced orange.
  8. Prepare 2 tsp of black pepper.
  9. It's 2 tsp of sea salt.
  10. Prepare 1 TBS of Dried sweet basil leaves.
  11. It's 1 TBS of soy sauce.

Garlic Honey-Dijon Pork Chops step by step

  1. Mix all marinade ingredients in bowl/container large enough to hold it and pork chops. Optional: set aside some of marinade to put on chops when serving!.
  2. Add pork chops to marinade. Cover and refrigerate for up to 24hrs..
  3. Pull pork chops out of refrigerator about 30 minutes before cooking.
  4. Grill/pan fry/bake until cooked through..
  5. Optional: serve with set aside marinade. Do not use with marinade pork was in unless you heat it to about 160*!.
