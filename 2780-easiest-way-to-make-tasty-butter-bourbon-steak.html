Ingredients of Butter Bourbon Steak

  1. Prepare 2 tablespoons of fresh ground black pepper.
  2. Prepare 1 clove of garlic.
  3. Prepare 5 pound of bottom tip sirloin steak.
  4. It's 1/2 of medium onion.
  5. Prepare To taste of pink Himalayan salt.
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup of bourbon.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of beef broth.
  8. Prepare 1/2 stick of butter.
  9. Prepare 2 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil.

Butter Bourbon Steak step by step

  1. Heat the oil. Season the steak with salt and pepper. Slice the onion and garlic very thin add to the oil with the black pepper. Add the steaks to the olive oil, and sear..
  2. When the garlic is soft remove from the oil. Same way with the onion. Continue to cook the steaks till your desired doneness..
  3. Remove the steaks add the broth and cook 5 minutes. Then add the garlic and onions..
  4. Add the bourbon reduce to half. Pour over the top of the steaks. I hope you enjoy!!!.
