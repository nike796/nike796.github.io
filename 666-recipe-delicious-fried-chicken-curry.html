Fried Chicken curry step by step

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add garam masala and onions and fry it til it changes the color.
  2. Now add washed chicken and fry it on mdium flame til golden brown.
  3. Take yogurt in a bowl, whisk it completely, add all spices and mix well, no lumps left.
  4. Now add yogurt mixture in chicken and mix it well..
  5. Gradually mix the curry, avoid sticking with pan, cover the pan and cook on low flame for 10 mints.
  6. When oil comes out from cornor, add water as you want gravy, cook on high flame until 1 boil.
  7. Spread green chillies and green coriander, and put on dam for 10 minutes,.
  8. Serve with chapati.
