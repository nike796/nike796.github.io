Sesame encrusted baked chicken step by step

  1. Pre heat oven 425. Combine soy sauce and sesame oil in a bowl. Combine sesame seeds, salt and panko bread crumbs on a plate..
  2. Let the chicken strips soak in the sauce for 5 minute's. Then place chicken strip in sesame mix and pat chicken to get it to stick..
  3. Place chicken on a baking sheet. Spray tops of chicken with oil. Bake for 10 minutes, then flip and cook another 5 minutes..
