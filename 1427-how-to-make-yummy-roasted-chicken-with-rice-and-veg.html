Roasted chicken with rice and veg instructions

  1. Boil rice in salty water.
  2. Steam carrots and green peas.
  3. Mix butter with : salt black pepper paprika ginger cumin curcuma mustard bay leaf powder rose marry powder.
  4. Cover the chicken with that butter and spices, fill it with onion garlic tomatoes and bay leaves, put it in roasting bag and cook it in the oven.
  5. Fry some garlic onion pepper carrots and green peas in 2 tbsp of oil, add rice and stir well, add chicken stock and simmer for a while, add the sauce from the roasting bag.
  6. Deep fry potatoes.
  7. Serve it with herbs and lemon slices.
