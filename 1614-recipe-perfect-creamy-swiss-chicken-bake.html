Ingredients of Creamy Swiss Chicken Bake

  1. Prepare 6 each of Chicken breasts.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of Sour cream.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of Mayonnaise.
  4. Prepare 6 slice of Swiss cheese.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of Garlic powder.
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Salt.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Pepper.
  8. It's 1 cup of Finely shredded Parmesan cheese.

Creamy Swiss Chicken Bake instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Pat chicken dry and place in greased 9 x 13 pan.
  3. Add sliced cheese on top of chicken breasts.
  4. In a bowl mix mayonnaise, sour cream, garlic powder, 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper.
  5. Spread mixture over chicken breasts.
  6. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese.
  7. Cook for 1 hour and serve..
