BBQ Honey chicken wings instructions

  1. Marinate chicken wings with salt, pepper, chilli powder, ginger garlic paste, zeera powder, dhania powder, soy sauce, vinegar, chilli sauce for 1-2 hours..
  2. After 2 hours marination coating wings with dry mixture (flour, cornflour, salt and pepper).
  3. Deep fry in oil and keep it aside..
  4. Make a sauce: heat oil and add chopped garlic in it and sauté than add bbq sauce, ketchup, sugar, chilli flakes and mix well and flame off your sauce is ready..
  5. Put fried wings into this sauce and toss well..
  6. Sprinkle some sesame seed and serve with any mayo dip..
