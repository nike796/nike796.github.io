Stir fried chicken with cashew nut instructions

  1. Coat chicken with tapioca flour.set aside.
  2. In the wok,warm vegetable oil over medium to low heat. Deep fry cashew nuts until crispy,spoon into a plate. Increase the heat and deep fry chicken chili. Leave from heat and drain from the oil..
  3. In the wok,heat 1 tps of vegetable oil. Pour seasoning mixture ingredients and stir until thicken over low heat. Add chicken,cashew nuts,dried chili. Stir them well..
  4. Add spring onion. Leave from heat and spoon onto a serving plate..
