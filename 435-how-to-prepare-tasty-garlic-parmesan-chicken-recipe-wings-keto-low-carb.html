Garlic Parmesan Chicken Recipe Wings (Keto/Low Carb) step by step

  1. Wash and dry wing sections.
  2. Season with your choice of seasoning. I used garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper..
  3. Bake on a cookie sheet lined with aluminum foil or parchment paper at 400 degrees for about a hour(depending on the size of the wings..
  4. Half way through turn wing sections so that they brown on both sides..
  5. While wings are cooking in a saucepan melt 1 stick of butter. Add the minced garlic and let simmer on low. After its bubbled for about 5 minutes then squeeze in the half a lemon. Then add the parmesan cheese..
  6. Once the wings are done take and toss the wings in the sauce. Put back on the cookie sheet place back in the oven for another 15-20 mins. Right when they come out sprinkle with more parmesan cheese..
