Beef salad with orange dressing instructions

  1. Put the oyster sauce,fish sauce,sugar and sesame oil in a bowl and stir until the sugar dissolves. Add the steak and turn to coat,cover with cling film n leave marinade for 1 hr.
  2. Preheat lightly oiled frying pan over medium-high,fry the steak 2 min for other side and 3-4 min each side,transfer and rest for 5 min.
  3. Blanch the asparagus and sugar snap in pan of boiling water for a minute until bright green n tender but still crisp.
  4. Rinse under water and drain well. Lightly toss with the herb,onion,and orange.
  5. Slice the beef and serve with asparagus sugar snap salad and the orange dressing.
