Ingredients of Quick and tasty Lamb Chops

  1. Prepare 1 kg of Lamb Chops.
  2. You need 1 tsp of chili powder.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of cumin seeds.
  4. It's 1 tsp of ground black pepper.
  5. It's 2 tsp of salt.
  6. You need 1 tsp of ground green chilli.
  7. It's 2 tsp of fine garlic.
  8. You need 1 tbsp of lemon juice.
  9. You need 1 of onion sliced into circles.
  10. Prepare 2-3 of tomatoes sliced into circles.
  11. You need of oil or butter for cooking.

Quick and tasty Lamb Chops step by step

  1. Add all the spices,chilli and garlic to the chops..
  2. Cover the chops with onions and tomatoes.
  3. Cook on low heat until the chops are soft and the onions and tomatoes melt.
