White sauce chicken steak instructions

  1. First cut chicken in long wide pieeces. Then press them with hammer..
  2. Dip them in vinegar. Add salt and black pepper..
  3. Now cook in a pan with little oil..
  4. Now add flour in that pan where we cook steaks..
  5. Mix this flour with all the oil and flavours of chicken in pan..
  6. Now transfer this flour in daighchi..
  7. Add milk and salt, and cook it. It will become thick sause..
  8. Now add steak in it. Cook for two minutes. Garnish with oregano and olives. And serve it with egg fried rice..
