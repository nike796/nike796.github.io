Mom's delicious chicken recipe step by step

  1. Preheat oven on 200 degrees..
  2. Wash chicken well with salt and then soak in water and vinegar..
  3. Rinse the chicken and put it in a colander to dry a little bit..
  4. Make some carves with the knife so that it absorbs the marinade..
  5. Grate the onions and tomato, add salt, pepper, lemon juice and spices. Mix well and cover the chicken on both sides..
  6. It is recommended that you leave the chicken int the fridge overnight, but you can cook it it directly if you are in a hurry..
  7. Put in oven tray and put in the oven till it is well cooked..
