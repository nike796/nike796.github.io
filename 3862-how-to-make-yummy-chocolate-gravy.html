Ingredients of Chocolate gravy

  1. You need 1/2 cup of cocoa.
  2. You need 3 tbsp of all-purpose flour.
  3. You need 3/4 cup of white sugar.
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp of salt.
  5. It's 2 cup of milk.
  6. You need 2 tbsp of butter (softened).
  7. It's 2 tsp of vanilla extract.

Chocolate gravy instructions

  1. Whisk cocoa, flour, sugar, and salt until no lumps.
  2. Pour in milk and whisk until mixed.
  3. Cook on medium heat for about 10-13 minutes, stir frequently.
  4. Remove from heat. Add butter and vanilla. Mix until melted.
