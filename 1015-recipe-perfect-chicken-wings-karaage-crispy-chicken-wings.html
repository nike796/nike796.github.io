Chicken wings Karaage (crispy chicken wings) step by step

  1. Wash the chicken wings and wipe off the moisture well. Chop the wings in half at the second joint from the edge..
  2. Rub in * marked base seasoning (sake and soy sauce) and dust lightly with flour..
  3. Mix the soy sauce and sugar well in a bowl.
  4. Fry the chicken in a shallow pan, shake off excess oil then dip in the bowl. Leave for 4-5 minutes..
