Honey Mustard Chicken Strips step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F..
  2. Combine mayo, mustard, paprika, honey, and salt and pepper in a bowl. Half will be a marinade half will be dipping sauce..
  3. Cut the chicken breasts into strips. Place it in a shallow bowl or bag..
  4. Coat the chicken in honey mustard sauce..
  5. Refrigerate this for 30 minutes or over night..
  6. Prepare a dredging station..
  7. One by one coat the chicken in panko bread crumbs..
  8. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes..
  9. Take out once completely done and let cool down a bit..
