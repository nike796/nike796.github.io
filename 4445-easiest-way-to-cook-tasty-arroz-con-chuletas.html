Ingredients of Arroz con chuletas

  1. Prepare 1 lb of boneless pork chops.
  2. It's 1 box of zaterans spanish rice.
  3. Prepare 15 oz of canned tomatoes undrained.
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of oil.
  5. It's 1 of sazon.
  6. Prepare 1 dash of adobo.
  7. You need 1 dash of Italian seasonings.
  8. You need 1 of butter.
  9. You need 1 can of tomato sauce (optional).

Arroz con chuletas instructions

  1. In a Med saucepan cook Zaterans Spanish Rice Follow directions on the box.
  2. In frying pan add oil and pork chops. dash on the seasonings and fry till crisp or to your liking..
  3. When Chops and Rice is done place into glass baking pan..
  4. Mix together. If you feel like the mixture is too dry add a little tomato sauce..
  5. Bake in the oven at 425° For 10-25 mins..
