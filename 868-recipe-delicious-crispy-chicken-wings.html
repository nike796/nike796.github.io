Ingredients of Crispy Chicken Wings

  1. Prepare of Chicken wings.
  2. You need 1/2 tbsp of Garlic powder.
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp of Baking powder.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of Cayenne pepper.
  5. You need 1 tbsp of Paprika.
  6. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of Black pepper.
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp of Salt.

Crispy Chicken Wings step by step

  1. Lay wings on cutting board and Pat dry with paper towel.
  2. Sprinkle salt over, flip and repeat.
  3. Add spices to large Ziploc bag.
  4. Add wings to bag and shake and massage.
  5. Lay wings on oven rack.
  6. Place in a 250 degree oven and bake for 30 minutes.
  7. Turn oven up to 450 and bake for another 40 minutes.
  8. Note: reheat at 350 for 15 mins on top aluminum foil.
