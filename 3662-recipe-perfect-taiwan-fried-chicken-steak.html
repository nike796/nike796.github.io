Ingredients of Taiwan Fried Chicken Steak

  1. You need 6 of boneless chicken legs.
  2. It's 3 tbsp. of all-purpose flour.
  3. You need 2 tbsp. of corn starch.
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp. of chicken stock.
  5. You need 1 cup of water.
  6. It's of Some salt and pepper.
  7. Prepare of Potato starch (enough to coat all the chicken steaks).

Taiwan Fried Chicken Steak step by step

  1. Beat the chicken legs flat. Heat the deep fryer to 375F/ 190C..
  2. Mix flour, corn starch, chicken stock, salt, pepper and water into a mixing bowl. Mix well..
  3. Dip chicken legs into step 2..
  4. Prepare the potato starch onto a plate and coat each chicken leg completely..
  5. Deep fry each chicken steak for 3-5 mins..
  6. Flip the chicken steaks and fry for another minute or until golden..
  7. Drip the oil..
