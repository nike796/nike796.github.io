Ingredients of KFC Style Hot Chicken Wings

  1. Prepare 6 pieces of half boiled chicken wings.
  2. Prepare 2 tbspn of butter/margarine.
  3. You need 1 1/2 cups of tomato sauce.
  4. You need 1 cup of hot n sweet sauce.
  5. You need 1 tbspn of tomato paste.
  6. You need 1 tbspn of red chilli powder.
  7. Prepare 2 tbspn of soy sauce.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of sugar.
  9. Prepare 1 tbspn of lemon juice.
  10. Prepare 1/2 of tspn of salt.

KFC Style Hot Chicken Wings step by step

  1. Pre boil the chicken wings half way done with salt and pepper.
  2. In a sauce pan,melt butter,add tomato sauce,hot n sweet sauce with medium heat and mix well.
  3. Add chilli powder,turmeric,soy sauce,tomato paste n sugar,combine n cook for about 5 min,keep stirring the sauce to evenly cook.
  4. Add your wings,evenly coat both sides with the sauce,add lime juice and cook on low till the sauce has thicken n reduce to quarter..
  5. Garnish with mint leaves,Serve hot with fries,or rice....enjoyyy.
