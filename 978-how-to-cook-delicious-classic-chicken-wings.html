Classic chicken wings instructions

  1. Marinade chicken wings with maggi, garlic, black pepper, ground coriander and soy sauce.
  2. Dice carrots, green pepper and onions.
  3. In a pot saute onions add in carrots and green pepper. Saute till soft.
  4. Brush grill pan with oil and grill wings on both sides. 4 mins for each side.
  5. Add the remaining marinade to the vegetable pot and add the wings. Stir well and let it cook on low for 8 to 10 mins. Serve hot.
