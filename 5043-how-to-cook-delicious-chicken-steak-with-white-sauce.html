Chicken steak with white sauce instructions

  1. In a bowl add chicken fillets with paprika powder,black pepper,red pepper, mustard paste,salt, chilli sauce,soya sauce and ginger garlic paste. Mix well and marinated for 40 minutes. In a grill pan add oil and marinated chicken fillets, grill on low flame from both sides until done..
  2. In a pan add butter and maida.cook very carefully for 2 minutes then add milk and mix well until smoothness now add mayonnaise, chilli sauce black pepper,mix herbs and salt cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Now chicken fillets and sauce is ready serve with baked cheese potatoes, vegetables or mashed potatoes and enjoy :).
