Banana Pancake Recipe instructions

  1. Mash ripe banana or make paste in mixie jar.
  2. Pour in a big bowl, add all the ingredients except oil.
  3. Mix it well, make sure there is no lumps in mixture.
  4. Heat a pan, pour 1tbs oil and pour 1big spoon of batter, cover lid for 2 mins, keep medium flame.
  5. After 2 mins, flip another sides and roast for 1 min.
  6. Now keep it in a plate, pour honey over on pancakes, Serve hot, Thanks..
