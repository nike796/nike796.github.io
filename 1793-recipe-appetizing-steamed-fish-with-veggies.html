Steamed fish with veggies step by step

  1. heat grill to medium heat.
  2. spray cooking spray on a piece of foil.
  3. slice bell peppers.
  4. cut stick of butter in half long ways.
  5. place half of butter in center of foil.
  6. place half of the sliced peppers around butter.
  7. pat fish fillets dry, sprinkle salt and pepper on both sides, lay on bed of peppers and butter.
  8. cut up garlic and place around and in between fillets.
  9. cut lemons in quarters, squeeze juice onto fillets, place remaining lemon quarters around fillets.
  10. place remaining pepper slices on top of fish.
  11. sprinkle sliced mushrooms over peppers, sprinkle a bit more salt and pepper.
  12. place basil leaves on top of mushrooms.
  13. place remaining butter on top.
  14. wrap up foil so nothing spilles out, place on gril.
  15. ready in 30 minutes.
