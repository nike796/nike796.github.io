Homemade Chicken katsu curry #familyfriendly step by step

  1. Put oil in medium heat saucepan then add onion in.
  2. Add all the ingredients in (apart from stock and potatoes) and stir well.
  3. Add potato and stock in stir well and leave it simmer for about 15-20 mins.
  4. Making chicken crumbs by prepare 2 trays- one with flour, salt and peper. Then cut chicken breast in half (vertical way) then dip chicken in the flour.
  5. Transfer chicken in to egg tray and make sure chicken all covered in egg.
  6. Dip chicken back to bread crumbs until all covered.
  7. Medium heat pan or saucepan with enough cooking oil to fry and cover the chicken.
  8. Fry chicken one by one. Once one side cook through turn over..
  9. Transfer to paper towels to get rid of access oil.
  10. Serve with curry and rice.
