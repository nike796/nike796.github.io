"Yellow Box" Chicken Strips instructions

  1. Lay out chicken strips and season with salt & pepper..
  2. Mix eggs with a tiny amount of water and whisk together. Pour some flour in a bowl..
  3. Coat strips in flour. Let sit 5 min..
  4. Coat strips in egg, then coat in flour again. Let sit 15 min.
  5. Fry a few strips for 2-3 minutes in 1/2 inch of vegetable oil. Turn strips.
  6. Fry 2-3 minutes on second side..
  7. Remove to clean rack. Repeat until all strips are cooked..
  8. Enjoy! Serve with cream gravy. Mashed potatoes are a nice addition too..
