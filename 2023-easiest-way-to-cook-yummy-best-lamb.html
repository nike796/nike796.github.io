Best lamb instructions

  1. Heat a frying pan on a high temperature and add oil..
  2. When heated place lamb chops in the pan and sear on both sides till a little brown. Add salt and pepper..
  3. Once seared, place rosemary all over lamb chops and cook for 20 - 30 more minutes on a low temperature.
  4. Enjoy!! (this is my favourite way to cook lamb chops!).
  5. Serve with some salad or mint sauce...or both its my favorite!!.
