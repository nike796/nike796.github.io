Chicken Egg fried rice step by step

  1. All veggies ko wast kar ke cut karain.
  2. Aik pan mn oil lkr zeera garlic ke sath chicken ko bhoon lain.
  3. Add carrot Shimla mirch well mix add black pepper salt crushed red chilli crushed well cooked sb veg ko side mn kar ke egg add karain or us ke oper thora sa zarda colour add kar dain mix karain.
  4. Add soya sauce chilli sauce Vinegar mix well & cook.
  5. Rice ko 2 hours ke leay bhigo dain phir salt &2 tsp Oil add kar boil karain &mix chicken.
  6. Fried &ready to cook chicken egg fried rice.
  7. Serve to platter & enjoy dilicious chicken egg fried rice.
