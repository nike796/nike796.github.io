Ingredients of Creamy pork chops

  1. You need 2 of onion.
  2. Prepare 4 of pork chops.
  3. It's 1/2 stick of butter.
  4. You need 1/2 cup of milk.
  5. Prepare 1 dash of salt.
  6. It's 1 dash of pepper.
  7. It's 3 dash of Worcestershire.
  8. It's 1 1/2 can of Cream of mushroom soup (large can, and a small).

Creamy pork chops step by step

  1. To cook this meal you need either a deep skillet, or crock pot..
  2. In a separate frying pan place pork chops in and season liberally. brown both sides slightly then remove from heat..
  3. Turn your attention now to the onions. slice onions slightly thin. In the deep skillet (or same frying pan used for pork chops for those using a crock pot) add the butter, let melt. Throw in onions..
  4. On medium great, saute onions till soft, and slightly brown. While that is going, grab a bowl and place the cream of mushroom soup inside. Stir to break up the mold of the can. Pour in milk and stir contents. (If still really thick, add a splash of more milk.) Next, add the Worcestershire and mix. Set aside..
  5. Lower heat of onions and place the pork chops on top..
  6. Grab soup mixture and pour over pork chops. Stir a little to incorporate..
  7. Cover entire pan with aluminum foil if no lid..
  8. This only gets better with time. Stir occasionally. I recommend atleast 2 hours. Pork will almost falls apart..
  9. I serve this with fried potatoes. But mashed would be excellent, for this makes a yummy gravy! :).
