Chicken Bone-in Fried Rice step by step

  1. In a pan, heat oil. Then add the onion, minced ginger-garlic and saute for a minute. Once this is done, add in all the veggies, chicken and stir fry for atleast 10-12 minutes. Then cover the lid and let it simmer on a low heat until done..
  2. Meanwhile, in a cooking pot add butter or ghee. To this, add in Bay leaves. You can also add other whole masalas such as cinnamon, black peppercorns and a pinch of nutmeg which is purely optional. Nutmeg can be omitted..
  3. After about a minute, then add in the soaked and drained rice. (The rice shouldn't be soaked for long. The more it gets soaked, the more delicate the rice will be.).
  4. Next, add water to a pot. (As easy this step may seem, it is the place where many of us tend to go wrong. Water should always be added proportionally. For one glass of rice that has been drained and soaked, 2 glasses of water will be required. This is the correct proportion.).
  5. Wait till the water boils before covering the pot with a lid. Don't overmix or the rice will break. After the rice is done, transfer the chicken to the pot and mix well. Allow this to stand for another 5 minutes before serving. Or you can use cooked rice and even leftover rice for this recipe..
