Marinated Fried Chicken step by step

  1. Rinse properly chicken and allow water to drain.
  2. Put in pot/ bowl..
  3. Add paprika, all spice, black pepper, curry, thyme, seasoning with salt and use clean palm to rub the ingredients well on the chicken.
  4. Cover and put in fridge overnight (or for some hours it's choice but mine stayed overnight).
  5. Remove from fridge, add in garlic and ginger paste with onion.
  6. Add 1 cup of water (I need the stock), cover and set on fire to cook until soft.
  7. Allow to cool, sprinkle flour.
  8. Heat pan of oil and deep in chicken at a time to fry as you change sides to golden.
  9. Drain and ready to enjoy as u desire.
