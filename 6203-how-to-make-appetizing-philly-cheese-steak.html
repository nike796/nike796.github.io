Ingredients of Philly Cheese Steak.

  1. Prepare of Boneless Rib Eye Steak.
  2. Prepare 3 Slices of Monteray Jack Cheese (Please Dont Use Cheese Whizz).
  3. Prepare 1 of Green Pepper.
  4. You need 1 of White Onion.
  5. It's 1 of Brioche Bun (60 Grams Is Ideal).
  6. It's of American/Englush Mustard (French's/Colman's).
  7. You need 2 Tsp of Cajun Seasoning.
  8. Prepare of Knob Of Butter.

Philly Cheese Steak. instructions

  1. Slice The Pepper And Onion And Fry In A little Butter And Cajun Seasoning. Mix Well..
  2. Very Thinly Slice The Rib Eye. 3 Or 4 Slices Will Be Enough. Keep Very Cold To Cut Easily..
  3. Cut The Bun Top To Bottom, Legnth Ways Leaving A Hinge Then Lightly Toast..
  4. Fry The Steak Until The Pinkness Has Almost Gone, Then Add Some Of The Onion Mix And The Jack Cheese. Mix Well And Evenly..
  5. Dress Brioche With Mustard And Add Cheese Steak..
