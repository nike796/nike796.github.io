Chicken with Mushroom & Asparagus Casserole step by step

  1. Boil chicken in 2 cups of water with 2 tsp Chicken Bouillon. Cook chicken until ALMOST done. It will finish cooking in the oven. Shred chicken once cooled and set aside..
  2. In a large bowl Mix Cream cheese and soups.
  3. Add spices, mix well..
  4. Added Asparagus, mix well..
  5. Add Rice, mix well.
  6. Add Chicken, mix well.
  7. Add cheese, leaving some for topping. Mix well..
  8. Spoon Mixture in a greased casserole dish and bake for 30 min @ 350.
  9. In a pan, melt 1/2 stick butter with 1/4 tsp of chicken bouillon and add crushed Ritz Crackers. Remove once crackers are moistened and set aside..
  10. Top casserole with Crackers and Shredded Cheese and bake til cheese is melted. About 25 min..
  11. Serve and Enjoy!.
