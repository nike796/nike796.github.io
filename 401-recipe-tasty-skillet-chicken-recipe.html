Skillet chicken recipe instructions

  1. Start with a simple seasoning on both side of the chicken with tumeric,nutmeg,suya spices,ginger,garlic,paprika,chicken seasoning ground,on ga salt pepper,and marinate for 1 hour..
  2. In the mean times,heat up veg oil on a large skillet.when oil is hot add in onion sliced then add in chicken to the pan cooked the chicken at a time bcos you don't want overcrowd your skillet..
  3. Cooked the chicken for like 15/20 minutes without moving them around.
  4. Flip the chicken for 7 minutes without moving them around..
  5. Add in,bell pepper,flip the chicken over and add 1 tbsp of butter and continues cook the chicken for 7 more minutes..
  6. Cook time depend on the tickeness of the chicken..
  7. When finished transfer to the serving plate and enjoy..
