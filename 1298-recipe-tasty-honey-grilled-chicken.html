Honey Grilled Chicken instructions

  1. Wash your chicken and cut it in to pieces, in a bowl put all the ingredients together..
  2. Marinate the chicken and allow it to soak the ingredients for 30 minutes..
  3. Put it under hot grill flip from time to time like after 4-5 minutes depending on how hot the grill is. Advisable put it on low heat..
