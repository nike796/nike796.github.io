Leftover fried rice instructions

  1. Throw rice on frying pan to start cooking at med heat with evoo.
  2. Cut chicken from bone and add to pan.
  3. Dice ginger and add with salt.
  4. Once everything looks well cooked (personally I like the rice a tad crispy) add eggs.
  5. Once eggs are cooked add soy sauce and cook another min.
  6. Enjoy.
