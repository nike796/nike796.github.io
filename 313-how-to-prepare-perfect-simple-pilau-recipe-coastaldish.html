Ingredients of Simple Pilau Recipe #coastaldish

  1. Prepare 2 Cups of White Rice.
  2. Prepare 1/4 Teaspoon of Salt.
  3. Prepare 4 Tablespoons of Vegetable Oil.
  4. Prepare 1/4 Cup of Pilau Marsala.
  5. It's 1/2 Cup of Onions.
  6. Prepare 4 1/2 Cups of Water.

Simple Pilau Recipe #coastaldish instructions

  1. Gather all your ingredients in one place for easier cooking.
  2. Heat a deep bottomed pan, add onions, vegetable oil.
  3. Cook the onions until they start to turn dark brown.
  4. Add in salt.
  5. Add in the ground Pilau Marsala.
  6. Pour water into the pan once the spice mixture has cooked for a few minutes.
  7. Meanwhile wash the rice until clear water runs through it.
  8. Once the water has boiled add in the rice.
  9. Cook the rice on high heat uncovered for several minutes.
  10. Once the water has reduced to below the rice turn down to medium heat and cover with a lid.
  11. Simmer the rice on low heat, fluff the rice with a fork, cover again and let it slowly cook.
  12. Cook the Pilau until all the water has completely dissolved. You can taste and if you feel that the rice is still a bit hard, do not add water. Take off the heat, keep the cover on and the steam will finish the cooking process for you.
  13. Serve your simple Pilau with your favorite stew, vegetables or even just eat it plain. Yummy!.
