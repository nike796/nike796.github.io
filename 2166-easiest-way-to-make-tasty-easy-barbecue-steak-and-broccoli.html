Easy Barbecue Steak And Broccoli instructions

  1. Season your steak with salt and pepper well..
  2. Then add your herbs and olive oil and rub it well..
  3. Fry in a nonstick pan for 5 minutes until slightly done..
  4. Then add your barbecue sauce , cover for another 5-10 minutes..
  5. Add more barbecue sauce and place pan in the oven for another 5-10 minutes or until done as desired. (You can add barbecue sauce as much as you'd like)..
  6. Add olive oil to your broccoli and a pinch of salt and pepper and cook in the oven. Enjoy.
