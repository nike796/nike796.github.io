Grilled Salmon with Creamy Avocado Sauce step by step

  1. Heat small amount of oil in a frying pan. Add salmon and cook for 3 minutes on each side. Remove salmon and set aside on a plate with a paper towel..
  2. Mix remaining ingredients until a creamy consistency is reached..
  3. Spoon half of sauce mix onto top of each salmon filet. Enjoy!.
