Peas pulao with chicken steak strips instructions

  1. First marinate the chicken fillet. For marination : mix all the ingredients together in a bowl. Add 2 tbsp of oil in it and mix thoroughly all the spices. Marinate chicken with this mixture..
  2. Now grill the marinated chicken. 10-12 mins per side.. or untill it's done..
  3. Make pulao: take oil in pan. Add onions. Stir fry them till golden..
  4. Add ginger paste. Stir it..
  5. Now add tomatoes. Mix it and add green chilli paste. Add all spices..
  6. When tomatoes become tender, fry it. And add peas. And fry it..
  7. Now add water (according to rice/ 5cups). When water starts boiling squeeze a lemon in it..
  8. Add rice now. Cook rice untill done. Peas pulao is ready..
  9. Now, cut the grilled chicken steak in strips. And serve it with rice..
