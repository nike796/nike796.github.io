Sweet and spicy Korean fried chicken sauce. The sauce is sweet and tangy with a little spicy kick from the gochujang (Korean red chili pepper paste). It's far from fiery hot, but reduce or omit the gochujang if you'd like. You can replace gochujang partially or entirely with ketchup. It's very common to use ketchup in a dakgangjeong sauce for a milder taste.
