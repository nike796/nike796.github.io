Ingredients of Spicy Beef Shoyu Ramen

  1. Prepare 4 of green onions, chopped (white and green parts separated).
  2. You need 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped.
  3. You need 1 tbsp of finely chopped fresh ginger.
  4. It's 1/4 tsp of red pepper flakes (or sambal oelek).
  5. You need 3 tbsp (45 ml) of olive oil.
  6. It's 4 cups (1 litre) of beef broth.
  7. You need 2 cups (500 ml) of water.
  8. It's 1 tbsp (15 ml) of fish sauce.
  9. You need 1 tbsp (15 ml) of soy sauce.
  10. It's 225 g of ramen-style noodles.
  11. It's 1 of sirloin steak, about 9 oz (250 g).
  12. You need of Bean sprouts, as desired.
  13. It's of Chopped fresh cilantro, as desired.
  14. It's of Lime wedges (optional).
  15. You need 1/2 cup of Mushrooms.
  16. It's 1/2 cup of broccoli.
  17. It's 2 of eggs fried.

Spicy Beef Shoyu Ramen step by step

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, soften the white parts of the onions, the garlic, ginger, mushrooms and red pepper flakes in 2 tbsp (30 ml) of the oil. Add the broth, water, fish sauce and soy sauce. Bring to a boil and simmer for 10 minutes. Adjust the seasoning..
  2. Add the noodles and cook for 3 minutes or until tender. Add broth, if needed..
  3. Meanwhile, in a skillet over high heat, brown the steak on both sides in the remaining oil until the desired doneness. Season with salt and pepper. Let rest for 5 minutes on a work surface, and then slice..
  4. Ladle the soup into bowls. Garnish with the meat, bean sprouts, the green parts of the onions, fried eggs and the cilantro. Serve with lime wedges, if desired..
