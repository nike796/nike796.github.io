Ingredients of Tapsilog

  1. It's 1 lb of sirloin beef.
  2. It's of Egg.
  3. You need of Marinade.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of salt.
  5. It's 1 tbsp of ground pepper.
  6. You need 1 tbsp of vinegar.
  7. You need 1 tbsp of soy sauce.
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp of sugar.
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp of garlic (grated).
  10. It's 2 tbsp of cooking oil.
  11. You need of Garlic Fried Rice.
  12. It's 2 tbsp of garlic (grated).
  13. It's 1 cup of rice (day old).

Tapsilog step by step

  1. Mix salt, pepper, vinegar, soy sauce, sugar and garlic.
  2. Tenderize and slice sirloin into strips.
  3. Add to marinade and leave for 30 mins to overnight.
  4. Saute garlic until golden brown.
  5. Saute rice then added sauted garlic.
  6. Fry egg.
  7. Saute marinaded beef.
