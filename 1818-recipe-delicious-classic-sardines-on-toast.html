Classic Sardines on Toast instructions

  1. Lightly toast your bread..
  2. Remove half of your tinned sardines with a fork & distribute them evenly on the two slices of bread,gently mashing them..
  3. Take a tiny amount of the oil from the tin with a teaspoon & drizzle it over the mashed sardines.
  4. Place under the grill on medium/high for 2-3 minutes..
  5. Cut into triangles & enjoy!.
