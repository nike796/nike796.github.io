Ingredients of Chicken chukka

  1. It's 750 gm of chicken.
  2. Prepare 4 tbsp of homemade chicken masala.
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp of grinded coconut with 3 cashew.
  4. Prepare to taste of salt.
  5. It's 3 tbsp of oil.
  6. You need 2 tsp of curry leaves.
  7. You need 2 of green chillies.
  8. Prepare 1 of chopped onion.
  9. Prepare 1 inch of Cinnamon stick.
  10. Prepare 2 of cloves.
  11. Prepare 1 of bay leaf.

Chicken chukka instructions

  1. Clean chicken and cut it to small pieces.
  2. Take a pan, add oil then add cinnamon, bay leaf, cloves, then add onion and ginger garlic paste. Saute till onion turns brown and then add chicken.
  3. Cover and cook for 10 min, then add grounded coconut paste and chicken masala.
  4. Adjust salt to taste, wait till all water evaporates and you get a semi gravy consistency.
  5. Serve hot.
