Mike's Easy Prego Steak Sandwiches step by step

  1. Cut your sirloin in two and salt and pepper to taste. Trim any fat..
  2. Using your mallet - pound your meat until it thins out a bit. Reverse, season again and pound..
  3. Chop garlic and pound it into your meat. Reverse and do the same..
  4. Fry / steam 1 minute on each side with a small dash of olive oil, a quality white wine and a tight lid..
  5. Slice and dress bread with mustard..
  6. Serve hot..
