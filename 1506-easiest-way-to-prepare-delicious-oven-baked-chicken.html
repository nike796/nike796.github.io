Oven baked chicken step by step

  1. Wash chicken and squeeze the juice of one lemon, all over it..
  2. Combine all the seasonings, spices,and oil together in a bowl. Add 1-2 tablespoons of water or vinegar..
  3. Place the chicken on a baking dish or pan and rub the mixed seasoning paste all over it. Make sure all sides are coated..
  4. Pre heat oven to 200degrees for 5 minutes and pop in your seasoned chicken. Top with lemon slices,carrots and green beans..
  5. Bake for about 30minutes and turn or flip the other side and bake until all sides are golden and shiny..
  6. Garnish with carrots and green beans.Enjoy with your meal or serve with salad..
