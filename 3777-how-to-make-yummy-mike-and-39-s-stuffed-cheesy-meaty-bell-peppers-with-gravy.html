Mike's Stuffed Cheesy Meaty Bell Peppers With Gravy instructions

  1. Fine chop all vegetables and herbs..
  2. Mix everything in the Meat/Cheese Filling section by hand..
  3. Pack your bell peppers tightly..
  4. Cap em' and bake em' at 350° for 40 minutes. Replace caps..
  5. At 35 minutes place shredded cheese on top..
  6. Place back in the oven for 5 minutes..
  7. Heat your beef gravy. Stir constantly..
  8. Serve piping hot. Enjoy!.
