Pan Fried Chicken with Thyme step by step

  1. Lightly season chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the pan with a touch of olive oil.
  3. Pan fry the chicken until it's white both side.
  4. Add thyme.
  5. Brown with butter.
  6. Serve on plate with a little thyme as topping.
  7. Serve with salad.
