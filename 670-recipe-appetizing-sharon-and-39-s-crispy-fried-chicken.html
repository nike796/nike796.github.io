Sharon's Crispy fried chicken instructions

  1. Rinse chicken pieces and pat dry.
  2. In a large bowl mix chicken and seasonings FOR CHICKEN. Let marinate for 1 or more hours.
  3. In deep fryer or frying pan pre heat oil to 365 degrees.
  4. In a large bowl mix all WET batter ingredients together.
  5. In another large bowl mix all BREADING ingredients together.
  6. When oil is ready add chicken pieces to the WET batter; coat well. Then add chicken to BREADING; coat well.
  7. Add chicken to fryer a few pieces at a time for 10-15 minutes depending on the size of your chicken pieces.
  8. Enjoy!!.
