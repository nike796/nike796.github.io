Ingredients of Oven Baked Cheesy-Ranch Chicken Strips

  1. You need 6 of Boneless chicken Breast.
  2. Prepare 1 of Ranch dip packet (powder form).
  3. It's 1 of sleeve of RITZ crackers.
  4. Prepare 2/3 cup of bread crums.
  5. It's 1 tsp of salt & pepper.
  6. You need 1 tsp of dry minced onions.
  7. You need 2/3 cup of parmesan cheese.
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese.
  9. You need 1 tsp of oregano.
  10. You need 2 cup of milk.

Oven Baked Cheesy-Ranch Chicken Strips instructions

  1. preheat oven 375Â°F.
  2. Put Milk in a large bowl.
  3. In a different bowl mix all dry ingredients EXCEPT the cheddar cheese.
  4. Slice chicken breasts into 3rds.
  5. dip chicken slices into milk then into the bread crum mix.
  6. oil the baking pan then place each strip side by side.
  7. put in the oven for 30mins.
  8. check to be sure its done then put the cheddar on top and put back in oven for 2mins.
  9. then serve.
