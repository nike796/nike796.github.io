Baked Hamburger Steak step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Fry onion and butter until almost starting to brown.
  3. Whisk water and Bistro together, mix in steak spice and can of soup.
  4. In an oven safe dish (i used a metal loaf pan) place the two patties, pour onion mixture over patties, place 2 bacon strips (pre- cooked) over each patty, pour Bistro mixture over dish.
  5. Place in oven for 30 minutes, serve with desired side dishes..
