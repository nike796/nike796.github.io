Simple egg curry with egg gravy recipe instructions

  1. Add oil in a frying pan and keep it on medium flame.
  2. Add 1 pinch hing to it..
  3. Add chopped onion. Saute it well till they become brownish..
  4. Now add tomato and mix it well. Let it cook properly..
  5. Add Ginger garlic paste to it and let it cook it till it gets rid of rawness.
  6. Now add all masalas and saute it well till you start seeing the oil..
  7. Break 2 eggs and slip into pan. Mix it well..
  8. Add half glass water and let the mixture boil for 5 minutes. Your gravy is ready.
  9. Now cut the boiled egg into two pieces and place it on top of gravy..
