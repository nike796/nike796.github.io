Baked chicken spaghetti instructions

  1. Take a pan add olive oil and saute all the veggies with salt and pepper. Put aside when it's done..
  2. Heat oil add garlic and add blanched tomato puree and season with salt and pepper..
  3. Add basil leaves and cook until sauce is ready..
  4. Mix all the veggies and chicken pieces in the sauce..
  5. Take a baking bowl or tray add all the sauce with veggies and chicken, add parmesan cheese and spaghetti..
  6. Add some parsley or oregano mix cover it with cheese slices and bake in oven for 15 minutes at 200 degree..
