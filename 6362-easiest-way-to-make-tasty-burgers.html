Ingredients of Burgers

  1. Prepare 2 lb of ground sirloin.
  2. It's 1/3 tsp of salt.
  3. You need 1/3 tsp of ground black pepper.
  4. Prepare 1/3 tsp of granulated garlic powder.
  5. You need 6 tsp of mayonnaise.
  6. You need 1/3 lb of lettuce whole leaves.
  7. It's 3 tsp of yellow prepared mustard.
  8. You need 3 tbsp of Heinz tomato ketchup.
  9. You need 1/2 cup of dill pickle chips.
  10. It's 2 medium of tomatoes sliced thinly.
  11. It's 1/3 small of onion sliced thinly.
  12. It's 1/2 cup of shredded fontina cheese.

Burgers step by step

  1. Form the sirloin into patties about a third pound each..
  2. Put into a hot pan fry till it forms a crust turn once season to taste with garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Turn once more after 3 minutes and add to second side of patties with garlic, salt and pepper..
  4. Carefully remove outside lettuce leaves set aside..
  5. When ground sirloin is done add cheese to top set on a paper towel and repeat till done.
  6. Add patty to lettuce. Add mayonnaise, mustard, ketchup, tomato, pickles, onions till you have everything on it..
  7. Wrap the lettuce around the patties serve, hope you enjoy!.
