Ingredients of Sloppy Joe Pizza

  1. You need 1 pound of ground sirloin.
  2. You need 12 inch of pizza dough.
  3. It's 1-1/2 tablespoons of yellow prepared mustard.
  4. It's 1-1/2 tablespoons of Heinz Tomato Ketchup.
  5. Prepare 15 ounce of diced tomatoes.
  6. It's 2-1/2 tablespoons of tomato paste.
  7. You need 1 teaspoon of kosher salt.
  8. You need 1 teaspoon of granulated garlic powder.
  9. Prepare 1 of small onion diced.
  10. You need 1/2 cup of yellow bell pepper diced.
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup of dark brown sugar loose packed.
  12. It's 1/2 cup of cooked bacon bits.
  13. Prepare 2/3 cup of shredded sharp and colby- jack cheeses mixed.
  14. You need 1 teaspoon of red wine vinegar.
  15. Prepare 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar.

Sloppy Joe Pizza instructions

  1. Preheat the oven 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Fry the bacon and save your drippings for another recipe..
  3. Dice the onion..
  4. Set the bacon aside..
  5. Heat a pan and sauté the onions I used a pan I just fried the bacon in..
  6. Add the sirloin. Sauté for 3 minutes. Add the spices..
  7. Add the peppers sauté for 2 minutes..
  8. Add the ketchup and mustard..
  9. Mix it well and simmer for 4 minutes..
  10. Add the tomatoes. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes..
  11. Add the brown sugar and both vinegars. Simmer 15 minutes to let reduce.
  12. Roll the dough out onto an oven safe pan..
  13. It shouldn't be too wet. Now add to the top of the dough..
  14. Now add the bacon and cheeses put into the oven for 35-40 minutes.
  15. Take out and let rest 7 minutes then cut the pizza.
  16. Serve I hope you enjoy!!.
