Fried chicken with herbs instructions

  1. soak the chicken cuts into the warm water and salt for 15 minutes to tenderize the chicken.
  2. mix the flour, granulated garlic, paprika, peppers and salt.
  3. get the egg whites in one container.
  4. bread crumbs into one container.
  5. coat the chicken with the flour mixture.
  6. then coat with egg white.
  7. then coat with bread crumbs.
  8. deep fry the chicken using medium heat temperature in a wok for 2 minutes or until it is crispy outside.
  9. pre heat the oven at 350 farenheit/180 celcius.
  10. wrap the chicken with aluminium foil.
  11. garnish with capsicum, garlic, shiitake, olive oil, tomato, herbs.
  12. place the cheese on top of the chicken.
  13. place the wrap in the oven for 20-25 minutes.
  14. ready to serve.
