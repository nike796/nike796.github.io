Vickys Beef Stroganoff, GF DF EF SF NF instructions

  1. Heat the butter and oil in a deep frying pan and add the beef strips and onions.
  2. Lightly fry off to soften but don't colour the onions, around 2 minutes. Add the cornstarch and mix into the meat juices.
  3. Add the mushrooms and fry a further 3 minutes https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/338187-vickys-homemade-worcestershire-sauce-vegan https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/333300-vickys-vegan-sour-cream.
  4. In a jug, mix together the stock, sour cream, mustard, tomato puree, Worcestershire, paprika and cayenne.
  5. Pour into the pan and bring to the boil then let simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in the parsley, adjust the seasonings to taste and serve over mashed potato, steamed greens, pasta or rice.
