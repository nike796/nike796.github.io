Ingredients of Pork Stir Fry

Pork Stir Fry step by step

  1. Mix first seven ingredients in bowl. Set aside..
  2. In large skillet over medium - high, heat oil and saute pork cubes until cooked through and nicely browned..
  3. Lower heat to medium and add sauce, cooking for several minutes to heat through and thicken..
  4. If sauce is too thick, add very hot water, a little at a time until you reach desired consistency..
  5. Add thawed veggies and cook for 3 minutes, a firmer texture is nicer on the veggies so don't overcook..
  6. If you want a thicker sauce, take equal amounts of cornstarch and water and add, heating through. Start off with 1/2 tablespoon each..
  7. Serve over hot cooked white or brown rice. I have a perfect white basmati rice recipe in my profile. https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/458063-perfect-white-basmati-rice.

  9. NOTE :If you wish to use fresh veggies try sprouts, broccoli, julienned carrots or zucchini.
