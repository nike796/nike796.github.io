Oven fried chicken step by step

  1. Take flour in a bowl and add salt, black pepper, red chilli powder, garlic powder and some seasoning and mix well..
  2. Line a pan or tray with foil and put butter on it. Melt the butter in hot oven..
  3. Coat the chicken pieces in the flour and place them on the melted butter..
  4. Place the tray in oven and bake at 170 degree Celsius till 30-40 minutes, once turning the pieces in between for even cooking on both sides..
  5. Take out the chicken pieces when golden on both sides and serve hot with some salad..
