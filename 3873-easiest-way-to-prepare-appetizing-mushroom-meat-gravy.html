Ingredients of Mushroom meat gravy

  1. Prepare 4 oz of whole mushrooms.
  2. Prepare 1 lb of ground meat your choice I used beef.
  3. It's 2/3 cup of dehydrated onion flakes.
  4. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of xantham gum.
  5. It's 1/2 tsp of salt.
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp of granulated garlic powder.
  7. You need 1/4 tsp of ground black pepper.
  8. Prepare 1 stick of butter.
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of broth your choice I used beef..
  10. You need 1 tsp of red wine vinegar.
  11. It's 1 tsp of worcestershire sauce.

Mushroom meat gravy step by step

  1. Slice the mushrooms into thirds. Melt the butter add your onions..
  2. Add ground meat, garlic, salt, pepper. When brown add xantham gum..
  3. Stir a lot. Then add 1 1/2 cups of broth. Stirring constantly it will thicken ..
  4. Add the worcestershire sauce and redwine vinegar stir in well. Serve hope you enjoy!.
