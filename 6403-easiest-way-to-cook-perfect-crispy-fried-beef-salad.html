Crispy Fried Beef Salad step by step

  1. In a bowl, mix together the steak, cornflour and soy sauce until completely combine..
  2. Add 1 inch of oil to a high sided frying pan and place over a medium heat..
  3. Fry the beef in batches, being sure not to over crowd the pan. Transfer the beef to a plate lined with kitchen paper..
  4. Place all the ingredients for the sauce into a saucepan and bring to a steady simmer over a medium heat..
  5. Place the beef in mixing bowl and add the sauce and sesame seeds. Toss to coat completely..
  6. Arrange the baby gem, carrot and cucumber on a serving platter. Top with the crispy beef and garnish with red chili and spring onion..
  7. Serve while still warm..
