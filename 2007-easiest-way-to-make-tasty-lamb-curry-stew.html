Lamb curry stew step by step

  1. Pour oil in a pot and brown the meat that have been seasoned with flour for few minutes.
  2. Stir the meat until it's all browned, then pour a dash of water and cover the pot..
  3. Let it cook then after a while pour your lamb stock stir and after some time the season your meat with, cumin, rajah, lamb spice, coriander, curry leaves, mixed herbs and teaspoon of vinegar..
  4. Stir again the as it's starting to fry then pour that chopped onion, garlic, and ginger then stir and add the tomatoes..
  5. Lower your heat now cause the lamb it's changing it's starting to thicken then you can simmer meat..
  6. This lamb chop curry will be serves with rice and broccoli and carrots as side dishes..
