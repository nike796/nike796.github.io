Ingredients of Grilled Pork Vietnamese style

  1. You need 1 1/2 lb of pork, chops, steaks, butt cut in thin sections, thin is good for this one.
  2. You need of marinade.
  3. It's 3 tbsp of fish sauce.
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp of brown sugar.
  5. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of vegetable oil.
  6. It's 1 tsp of acid (lime juice, lemon juice, vinegar).
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp of diced lemon grass (optional).
  8. You need 2 tbsp of diced onion or shallot.
  9. It's 3 clove of garlic, diced.
  10. Prepare 1 each of large green onion, sliced.
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp of hot sauce (optional).
