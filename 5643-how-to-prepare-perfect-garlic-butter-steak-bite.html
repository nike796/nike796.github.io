Ingredients of Garlic Butter Steak Bite

  1. You need 350 gr of beef diced.
  2. You need 5 cloves of garlic.
  3. Prepare 4 tbsp of butter.
  4. It's of parsley.
  5. You need of Marinade:.
  6. It's 1 tbsp of garlic powder.
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp of onion powder.
  8. You need 1 tbsp of smoke paprika.
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp of oregano.
  10. It's 1 tbsp of thyme.
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp of soy sauce.
  12. You need pinch of salt.
  13. You need 1 tbsp of pepper.

When cooking the garlic steak bites you will want to work in batches so not to overcrowd the pan. This recipe uses sirloin steak cut into small pieces. I chose sirloin because it's tender and flavorful, but it's not super expensive. Other great choices would be tenderloin, rib eye, or New York strip steak.

Garlic Butter Steak Bite instructions

  1. Mix the beef with garlic powder, onion powder, smoke paprika, oregano, thyme, soy sauce, salt and pepper. Let marinate the beef for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the hot pan with butter and pan the beef just 1 minute for each side (medium well), set aside.
  3. In the same pan, put the butter and garlic, fry it about 2 minutes and then put the beef back to the pan, turn off the heat.
  4. Finish it with fresh parsley on top.
