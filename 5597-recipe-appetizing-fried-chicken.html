Fried Chicken step by step

  1. Clean Chicken thoroughly.
  2. Separate boneless pieces of chicken and cut them in small chunks.
  3. Sprinkle tumeric, chilli powder (bedgi chilli).
  4. Squeeze lemons.
  5. Salt.
  6. Whisk an egg and mix it in with others.
  7. Cover the chicken with the marinate and rest it for 20 minutes.
  8. Put a table spoon or two of oil in a flat pan.
  9. After the pan gets hot, pick the marinated pieces of chicken.
  10. Cover the pieces with corn flour.
  11. Place the pieces separately on the pan.
  12. Shallow fry both the sides till it gets golden colour.
  13. Ready to serve.
