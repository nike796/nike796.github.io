KFC fried Chicken step by step

  1. Wash your hand say BismiAllah and Daroud shareef.
  2. Wash your chicken very well then after that marinate it with some salt to taste and vinegar after that wash it again with normal water.
  3. After that prepare second marination take a bowl add dahi, salt, red chilli, lemon juice, oil and 4 tbls crispy masala.
  4. Mix all above spices in dahi.
  5. Add chicken in it.
  6. Mix it well and keep in fridge for maximum 2h #tip if you have to used small pieces of chicken then you can rest it maximum 1h.
  7. After marination time coat all chicken pieces with maida and other spices.
  8. First Coat it very well with maida then dip in chilled water then again coat in maida...press it in maida from all sides.
  9. Coat all pieces as well until make crumbs on it.
  10. Take a wok add oil in it heat it then add coated chicken.
  11. Don't stir it immediately cook it for a while then stir it.
  12. Cook it on low medium flame untill well cooked from inner side.
