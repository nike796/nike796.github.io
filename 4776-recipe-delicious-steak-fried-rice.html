Ingredients of Steak fried rice

  1. It's 3 tbsp of oil.
  2. Prepare 1 each of bell pepper.
  3. Prepare 1/2 each of carrot.
  4. You need 1 each of leek or 1/2 of an onion.
  5. Prepare 250 grams of steak.
  6. It's 2 each of eggs.
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp of soy sauce add to taste.
  8. Prepare 2 cup of cooked rice.
  9. Prepare 2 pinch of salt if needed ~ I don't use salt.
  10. Prepare 1 tsp of pepper.

Steak fried rice step by step

  1. In a wok or large frypan heat oil over med/low heat.
  2. Add all veggies~ saute til they begin to soften.
  3. Add meat.
  4. After meat is finished cooking push to the side.
  5. If needed add more oil & scramble eggs on the empty side of the wok.
  6. Add rice & drizzle soy sauce over the top.
  7. Panfry ~ if rice is still white add more soy sauce.
  8. Salt & pepper to taste.
