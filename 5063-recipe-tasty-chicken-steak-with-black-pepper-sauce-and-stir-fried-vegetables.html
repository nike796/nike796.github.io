Chicken Steak with black pepper sauce and stir fried vegetables instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken and refrigerate for atleast 1 hour or keep overnight..
  2. Take a grill pan or electric grill and cook the chicken fillets along with sauce until tender..
  3. For the sauce, in a pan add the butter, garlic powder, cream and saute on low heat for a minute..
  4. Then add the salt and pepper, dried basil and mix well, then add the milk and keep whisking the sauce until bubbles appear on the side. Turn off the flame then..
  5. Stir fry the vegetables in oil and add the salt and pepper to taste..
  6. Serve hot with mashed potato balls..
