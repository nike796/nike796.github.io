Ingredients of Mushroom gravy

  1. You need 1 C of mushrooms, sliced.
  2. It's 2 T of butter.
  3. Prepare 2 C of onions, sliced thin.
  4. You need 4 of garlic cloves, crushed.
  5. Prepare 1/2 C of flour.
  6. Prepare 4 C of chicken broth.
  7. Prepare 2 tsp of cayenne.
  8. You need 2 of bay leaves.
  9. It's 1 T of paprika.
  10. It's 1/4 tsp of salt.
  11. Prepare 1/4 tsp of pepper.

Mushroom gravy step by step

  1. Melt the butter in a medium saute pan on medium low. Add the mushrooms and saute until brown. Add the onions and garlic and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the the rest of the spices. Now put in the flour (spread out over mixture) and pour in the broth slowly while whisking the entire time until it thickens, about 3 minutes..
  3. Remove from heat and serve on your meat or pasta..
