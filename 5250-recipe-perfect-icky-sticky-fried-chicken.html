Icky Sticky Fried Chicken instructions

  1. Heat frying pan with 1/2 cup of vegetable oil (enough to submerge chicken in halfway) on a medium high.
  2. While oil is heating, season chicken with garlic, pepper, salt and compete seasoning.

  4. When oil is hot enough to sizzle chicken about 5 minutes add chicken and now you sprinkle the Sazon over all chicken (keeps from dying hand orange).
